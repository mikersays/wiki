# UFO Vault

Topic: UFO / UAP / non-human intelligence / astrobiology coverage and primary sources.

## Scope

In scope:
- UAP / UFO sightings, incidents, congressional hearings, government programs (AARO, AATIP, etc.)
- Whistleblowers and disclosures (Grusch, Elizondo, Mellon, etc.)
- Pentagon / DoD activity, drone incursions, military intercepts
- Astrobiology / SETI / biosignatures / exoplanet life detection
- Primary sources (aaro.mil, dni.gov, dod.mil, congress.gov, nasa.gov)

Out of scope:
- Pure aviation news without anomalous-object angle
- General space exploration without a life-detection angle
- Tabloid or rehash coverage without new reporting

## Vault-specific notes

- Source filenames follow the shared `src-<topic>-<yyyy>[-mm].md` convention. See top-level `CLAUDE.md` for naming rules.
- The `/ufo-news` skill is pinned to this vault — it fetches and ingests directly into `ufo/raw/inbox/` and `ufo/wiki/`.
- Bias toward primary sources where available; cite news outlets as secondary unless they break new reporting.

# Sky Sabre

UK [[british-army|British Army]] [[ground-based-air-defence|ground-based air defence]] system. Composed of:
- Rafael's Surface to Air Missile Centre (SAMC) command and control
- Saab Giraffe Agile Multibeam (AMB) radar
- MBDA Land Ceptor launcher firing **Common Anti-Air Modular Missiles (CAMM)**

Intercept range ~25 km. Cannot defeat ballistic or hypersonic threats. Six systems in service as of 2024; two forward-deployed.

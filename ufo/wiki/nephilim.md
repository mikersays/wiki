**Nephilim** are figures named in Genesis 6:1–4 of the Hebrew Bible, sometimes interpreted as giants or the offspring of "sons of God" and human women. Within the [[theological-uap-framing]] strand they recur as a Christian-eschatological reading of UAP — non-human beings that are not extraterrestrial but rather supernatural or "fallen angel" entities.

In the vault, the Nephilim framing surfaces from two strands:

- **Rep. [[lauren-boebert]] (R-CO)** — May 2026 podcast: "There's nothing that says that fallen angels, that Nephilim just disappeared. I do believe that this is more spiritual, and, if you really want to go there, demonic." ([[src-religious-republicans-uap-files-2026-05]])
- **Pentecostal evangelist [[perry-stone]]** — claims of unnamed pastors briefed by U.S. officials to prepare churches for UFO disclosure ([[src-perry-stone-pastor-briefing-2026-05]]).
- **Rep. [[anna-paulina-luna]]** has separately pointed followers to the **Book of Enoch** (Watchers / Nephilim canon) post-PURSUE launch ([[src-newsweek-luna-teases-disclosure-2026-05]]).

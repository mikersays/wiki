# Grand Forks Air Force Base

U.S. Air Force installation in North Dakota; historically a Minuteman ICBM site, now host to the 319th Reconnaissance Wing (Global Hawk and other ISR platforms). Selected as one of two USAF sites (with [[whiteman-afb]]) for the May 2026 [[jiatf-401]] [[directed-energy-weapons]] [[counter-uas]] pilot (per [[src-directed-energy-5-bases-pilot-2026-05]]).

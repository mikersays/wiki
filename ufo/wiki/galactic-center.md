**Galactic Center** — central region of the Milky Way, hosting the supermassive black hole Sgr A* and an extreme density of stars.

## Relevance to UAP / SETI

The Galactic Center is a strategic SETI target because:

1. **Stellar density** maximizes the line-of-sight count of potentially habitable systems within a single telescope beam.
2. It would be a natural **beacon site** for a galaxy-spanning civilization seeking to transmit to the widest stellar population.

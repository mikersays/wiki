# Max Planck Institute for Astronomy (MPIA)

Heidelberg-based Max Planck Society research institute focused on stellar, planetary, and galactic astronomy. MPIA contributed filter-wheel hardware to [[jwst]]'s MIRI instrument and is a partner on the [[nancy-grace-roman-space-telescope]] coronagraph.

## Recent UFO/UAP-vault-relevant work

- 2026: Led the [[jwst]] direct-imaging study of Jupiter analogue [[epsilon-indi-ab]] reporting anomalously low NH₃ and inferred [[water-ice-clouds]] ([[elisabeth-matthews]] et al., [[src-jwst-ice-clouds-exoplanet-2026-04]]).

**Dr. Sofia Sheikh** is a [[seti-institute|SETI Institute]] astronomer and [[technosignatures]] researcher. She is the lead author of the **"Earth Detecting Earth"** study, which inventories the technosignatures present-day Earth emits — radio leakage, optical/laser signals, atmospheric and surface markers, and artificial objects — and estimates how far away each could be detected by an extraterrestrial civilization with comparable technology.

Key findings include that everyday 4G LTE leakage is detectable only to ~4 light-years (roughly to Proxima Centauri), while powerful planetary radar bursts (e.g., the decommissioned Arecibo dish) reach much farther. The study frames Earth as a calibration target for [[terrestrial-technosignature]] searches. It was discussed alongside [[simon-steel]] in a SETI Institute *SETI Live* segment ([[src-seti-detecting-earth-technosignatures-2026-05]]).

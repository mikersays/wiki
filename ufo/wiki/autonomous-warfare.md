# Autonomous Warfare

**Autonomous warfare** is one of five domains explicitly cited by [[pete-hegseth|Secretary of War Hegseth]] in his FY27 posture testimony to the [[house-armed-services-committee]] as an area where "strategic competitors" (i.e., China and Russia) are investing and which the U.S. must counter. The other four are space dominance, hypersonic weapons, nuclear deterrence, and conventional capabilities ([[src-hegseth-fy27-posture-testimony-2026-04]]).

## Adjacent investment lines

- [[drone-dominance]] — named FY27 priority
- [[counter-uas]] — defensive complement; [[jiatf-401]] portfolio
- **AI / Pace-Setting Projects** — USW(R&E) replaces traditional Programs of Record for AI; COTS AI tools rolled out across the workforce.
- **Directed Energy** — DIB postured for prototype quantities only; goal to scale to "tens to hundreds of units" through demand signaling.

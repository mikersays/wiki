# Ravit Helled

Planetary-interior modeler at the [[university-of-zurich]]. Co-author of the interior-modeling component of the [[toi-5205-b]] [[jwst|JWST]] atmosphere paper summarized in [[src-sciencedaily-forbidden-exoplanet-atmosphere-2026-04]]. Their work argues that the bulk planet is ~100× more metal-rich than its visible atmosphere — a result with implications for [[planetary-migration|planetary-migration]] models.

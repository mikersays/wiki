# Stars and Stripes

**Stars and Stripes** is a DoD-authorized but editorially-independent newspaper for the US military community, operating under statutory protections that bar Pentagon interference in news content. First appearance in this vault is its May 8, 2026 wire on the [[pursue]] launch.

## Coverage in this vault

- **2026-05-08** — [[src-stripes-pursue-uap-files-2026-05]] (Phillip Walter Wellman): launch wire on [[pursue]] Release 01 written for a US military readership. Notable for being the **only** Release 01 wire to explicitly name [[us-indo-pacific-command]], [[centcom]], and [[us-european-command]] as contributors of "unresolved" UAP videos, and the only launch-day piece to lead with [[apollo-12]] (vs. [[apollo-17]]) for the included lunar imagery.

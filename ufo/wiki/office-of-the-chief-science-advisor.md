# Office of the Chief Science Advisor (OCSA)

[[canada|Canadian]] federal advisory office, headed by Chief Science Advisor [[mona-nemer]]. Sponsor of the [[sky-canada-project]] preliminary (Jan 2025) and final (July 2025) reports on UAP reporting and analysis.

OCSA is **advisory only**: it has no implementation authority. Per [[src-canada-sky-canada-project-2025-02]], OCSA Chief of Staff Luc Gauthier characterized the office's role as ending at the recommendation stage — execution is the federal government's prerogative.

The final Sky Canada Project report ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]], CTV News, July 14 2025) formally recommends a dedicated federal UAP service led by [[canadian-space-agency]], a destigmatization push for [[transport-canada]] pilot reporting, public-records release, and a bilingual reporting app. Implementation has been deferred while OCSA focuses on supporting the new Carney government.

# Orbital Biosignature Recon: NASA AI for Tracking Harmful Algae

## Summary

[[astrobiology-com|Astrobiology.com]] ([[keith-cowing|Keith Cowing]], May 25 2026) reproduces a [[nasa-jpl|NASA JPL]] release on a self-supervised machine-learning tool that fuses data from five satellite missions/instruments — including **PACE** and **TROPOMI** — to detect harmful algal blooms (HABs) off western Florida and Southern California. Published in [[american-geophysical-union|AGU]]'s *Earth and Space Science*, the work is framed here as "orbital biosignature recon": using Earth-observation remote sensing of a known biological signal (chlorophyll / phytoplankton blooms) as an operational, ground-truthable analogue for life-detection methodology. The model was trained on 2018–2019 satellite data with field/lab measurements for context, then validated on later periods in the same regions, correctly mapping species such as *Karenia brevis*. The team — [[michelle-gierach|Michelle Gierach]] and [[kelly-luis|Kelly Luis]] (both NASA JPL) with data scientist Nick LaHaye (Spatial Informatics Group) — frames the AI as a "force multiplier" directing where and when to sample. Severe blooms cost U.S. coastal economies tens of millions annually and produce airborne toxins; [[noaa|NOAA]] issues seasonal HAB forecasts.

## Key Claims

- A self-supervised ML system fused five space missions/instruments (incl. PACE, TROPOMI) to detect HABs without requiring pre-labeled training data (NASA JPL).
- The tool correctly identified and mapped blooms, including specific species (*K. brevis*), in complex coastal waters with sediment, plants, and runoff (research team).
- Trained on 2018–2019 data, validated on later periods in the same geographic areas (research team).
- The team is extending the tool to additional coastlines and lakes, aiming for decision-maker use "in coming years."
- HABs (*K. brevis* in the Gulf; *Pseudo-nitzschia* off California) kill wildlife, poison marine mammals, and cause human respiratory illness via airborne toxins.

## Notable Quotes

**Darrell Evans** is a researcher at [[purdue-university]] working on UAP and aerospace correlation analysis. In a March 2026 *The Conversation* essay (see [[src-academic-uap-stigma-2026-04]]), he argued that despite federal UAP investigation, U.S. universities lack dedicated UAP research centers, federal grant pipelines, and doctoral-training programs — and that the deterrent is sociological "boundary work" rather than intellectual disagreement. He is developing a temporal aerospace correlation tool linking civilian UAP sightings to Cape Canaveral rocket launches; the underlying paper is under peer review at *Limina: The Journal of UAP Studies* ([[limina-journal]]).

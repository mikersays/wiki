# GEMS Survey (Red Dwarfs and the Seven Giants)

JWST's **largest Cycle 2 exoplanet program**. Targets the rare class of **giant exoplanets around M-dwarf stars (GEMS)** — sometimes called "**forbidden**" planets because their existence at close orbits around low-mass stars is poorly explained by core-accretion or disk-instability theory.

## Notable results

- **2026** [[jwst|JWST]] NIRSpec [[transmission-spectroscopy|transmission spectroscopy]] of [[toi-5205-b]]: atmosphere is **carbon-rich and metal-poor relative to the host star** (a first), CH₄ and H₂S detected. Interior models indicate ~100× decoupling between bulk and atmospheric metallicity. See [[src-sciencedaily-forbidden-exoplanet-atmosphere-2026-04]].
- Pioneered a **starspot-correction** methodology applicable across [[m-dwarf-stars|M-dwarf]] exoplanet spectra.

**Ted Cruz** is a Republican senator from Texas and chair of the Senate Committee on Commerce, Science and Transportation.

In March 2026, Cruz criticized the [[department-of-war]], [[faa]], and [[customs-and-border-protection|CBP]] for poor interagency coordination after two back-to-back [[counter-uas|C-UAS]] incidents in Texas. He called the Pentagon's reversal on the [[rotor-act|ROTOR Act]] air-safety bill "objectively false." See [[src-texas-counter-drone-senators-2026-03]].

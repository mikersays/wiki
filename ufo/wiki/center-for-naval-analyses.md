The **Center for Naval Analyses (CNA)** is a U.S. federally funded research and development center (FFRDC) supporting the Navy, Marine Corps, and other defense customers.

CNA appears in the vault as the venue for former [[aaro]] director [[sean-kirkpatrick]]'s April 27, 2026 presentation, in which he characterized the early-2023 high-altitude shootdowns — including the [[lake-huron-shootdown-2023|Lake Huron AIM-9X event]] — as hobbyist-balloon takedowns.

# White dwarf

Stellar remnant of low- and intermediate-mass stars after the end of their main-sequence and red-giant phases. The Sun will end as a white dwarf in ~5 Gyr.

## Relevance to UAP / astrobiology vault

- **Late-system architecture**: white dwarfs preserve outer planetary remnants while engulfing or destabilizing inner ones — a useful boundary condition for studies of [[post-main-sequence-evolution|post-main-sequence]] planet survival.
- **[[wd-0806b]]** orbits a white dwarf at 2,500 AU and is the **second-coldest directly imaged exoplanet** known. JWST atmosphere characterization in 2026 (see [[src-aas-nova-wd-0806b-atmosphere-2026-04]]) yielded the first observational altitude-dependent [[eddy-diffusion|K_zz]] in an exoplanet atmosphere.

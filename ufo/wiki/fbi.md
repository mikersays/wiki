# Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI)

U.S. domestic intelligence and law-enforcement agency under the Department of Justice.

## UAP-Adjacent Activity

- April 2026: received Rep. [[eric-burlison|Burlison's]] referral on [[sullivan-whistleblower|Sullivan's]] May 2024 death and is reportedly reviewing multiple cases involving cleared aerospace/defense personnel.
- **May 8, 2026:** Director [[kash-patel]] is co-principal on the [[pursue]] inaugural release. The FBI is the document-source for **Release 01 page 1** — all 10 PDFs are drawn from [[fbi-case-file-62-hq-83894]] (1947–1968 unidentified-objects/"flying discs" file, 18 sub-documents including Oak Ridge, Tennessee reports). Release 01 also includes a New Year's Eve 1999 FBI photo set showing alleged UAPs alongside U.S. aircraft, plus an FBI eyewitness-sketch composite of an "ellipsoid bronze metallic object" estimated at 130–195 feet. Patel: "first time in history" the public has "unfettered access to declassified government files on Unidentified Anomalous Phenomenon"; per CBS, separately said he has no information that the U.S. ever recovered alien bodies.

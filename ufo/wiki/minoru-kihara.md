**Minoru Kihara** is a Japanese Defense Minister. In **2024**, under his leadership, the Japanese Defense Ministry shifted posture to treat **unidentified aerial objects as security concerns**, emphasizing the need to analyze and respond to incursions in Japanese airspace — a significant doctrinal change in [[japan]]'s UAP policy.

This shift preceded [[yoshiharu-asakawa]]'s May 2025 proposal and the [[japan-uap-parliamentary-group]]'s March 2026 proposal ([[src-japan-uap-strategy-proposal-2026-03]]).

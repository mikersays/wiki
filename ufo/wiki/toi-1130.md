# TOI-1130

**TOI-1130** is the host star of a rare multi-planet system containing the inner warm mini-Neptune [[toi-1130b]] and an outer hot Jupiter, locked together in a **2:1 mean motion resonance**. The system was a TESS Object of Interest target later followed up by CHEOPS and [[jwst]] (NIRSpec G395H + NIRISS SOSS).

The architecture is dynamically informative: the resonance and the outer giant's likely "pebble-filtering" of inner-disk solids together favor an ex-situ origin for [[toi-1130b]] beyond the [[water-ice-line]], followed by [[planetary-migration]] into the present configuration ([[src-jwst-toi-1130b-atmosphere-2026-05]]).

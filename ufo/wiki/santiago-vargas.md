# Santiago Vargas

**Santiago Vargas** is a Colombian astrophysicist at the **Observatorio Astronómico de la Universidad Nacional de Colombia**. He is the most prominent named scientific skeptic of the [[buga-sphere]] in Colombian press.

## Position on the Buga Sphere

Per [[src-buga-sphere-el-pais-cali-maussan-handoff-2025-10]] and [[src-buga-sphere-infobae-true-origin-2025-10]]:

- The sphere's components — including identified **optical fibers** in its structure — are "very terrestrial."
- No scientific studies support [[jaime-maussan]]'s claims.
- The object is most plausibly a contemporary terrestrial artifact or **publicity stunt**, comparable to the Arizona desert monolith.

> "There is no technology in the famous Buga Sphere that is 'from another world.' On the contrary, its compounds are very terrestrial, like the optical fibers that have been identified." — Santiago Vargas (translated)

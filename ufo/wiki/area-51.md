# Area 51

Top-secret U.S. Air Force facility at Groom Lake, Nevada, used for Cold War aircraft testing (U-2, A-12/SR-71, F-117). Declassified by the CIA in 2013. Foundational locus of UFO conspiracy lore, fueled by 1989 claims from [[bob-lazar]] of UAP reverse-engineering work at the adjacent S-4 site.

## UAP relevance

- Long alleged in popular culture and conspiracy circles to house recovered alien craft and biological remains; no confirmed primary-source evidence supports those claims.
- [[bob-lazar]]'s 1989 claims of working on flying-saucer propulsion at S-4 launched the modern Area 51 / UAP association.
- **March 2026** — Vice President [[jd-vance]] told podcaster [[benny-johnson]] he had been trying to find time to investigate Area 51 since taking office. ([[src-trump-pentagon-uap-files-2026-05]])
- Geographic context: Trump's May 2026 White House UAP-files remarks came one day after he spoke in Las Vegas, "not far from Area 51." ([[src-trump-pentagon-uap-files-2026-05]])

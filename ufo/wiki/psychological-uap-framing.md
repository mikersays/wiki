# Psychological UAP Framing

Umbrella concept for interpretations that treat UAP encounters, abductions, and related anomalous experiences as **phenomena of the human psyche** — products of altered states, cultural expectation, archetypal projection, trauma, and social upheaval — rather than as evidence of non-human technology. Distinct from (but historically tangled with) the [[theological-uap-framing|theological / demonic]] frame and the materialist [[uap-reverse-engineering|crash-retrieval]] frame.

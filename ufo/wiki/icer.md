The **International Coalition for Extraterrestrial Research (ICER)** is an organization composed of scientists and academics engaged in UAP studies, operating with an explicitly international scope. [[gary-heseltine]], a former British police detective, serves as vice president. ICER participated in [[brazil]]'s June 2022 Senate UAP hearing, representing international scientific interest in the phenomenon.

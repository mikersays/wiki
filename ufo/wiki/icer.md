The **International Coalition for Extraterrestrial Research (ICER)** is an organization composed of scientists and academics engaged in UAP studies, operating with an explicitly international scope. [[gary-heseltine]], a former British police detective, serves as vice president. ICER participated in [[brazil]]'s June 2022 Senate UAP hearing, representing international scientific interest in the phenomenon. Prof. **Tim Murithi** (University of Cape Town) serves as ICER's South African representative; he presented "Pan-African Ufology" at the July 2026 [[src-breaking-the-taboo-uap-contact-symposium-2026-05|"Breaking the Taboo" symposium]].

# Canadian Space Agency

Federal civil-space agency of [[canada]]. Initially proposed by the [[sky-canada-project]] preliminary report (Jan 2025) as the home for a national UAP body, but CSA has stated UAP research is not part of its mandate (per [[src-canada-sky-canada-project-2025-02]]). MP [[larry-maguire]] argues [[transport-canada]] is the better institutional fit.

The final Sky Canada Project report ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]], July 2025) reaffirms CSA as a "trusted" candidate lead in collaboration with government and academic partners. CSA spokespeople said they were "pleased to be recognized" but acknowledged CSA is not currently involved and noted an "opportunity for coordinated action."

# Senate Armed Services Committee (SASC)

U.S. Senate committee with jurisdiction over the Department of War (formerly DoD); the primary statutory vehicle for the annual [[uap-ndaa-fy2026-intercept-provisions|NDAA]] and originating venue for the [[uap-disclosure-act]] under [[mike-rounds]] and [[chuck-schumer]].

Held public AARO oversight hearings in 2023 and 2024 under Democratic control. Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], SASC held no comparable public AARO hearing in 2025 after Republicans assumed the gavel. Concurrence from SASC chair and ranking member is one element of the "Four Corners" precondition imposed on UAPDA inclusion via the [[house-armed-services-committee]] (see [[four-corners-ndaa-process]]).

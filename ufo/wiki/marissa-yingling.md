**Marissa Yingling** is a researcher who, with co-authors Charlton Yingling and Bethany Bell, has authored peer-reviewed national surveys quantifying [[uap-stigma]] in U.S. academia. Their 2023 *Humanities and Social Sciences Communications* survey covered 1,460 faculty across 144 research universities and 14 disciplines, finding curiosity outweighing skepticism in every discipline but fewer than 1% having ever conducted UAP research. A 2024 follow-up reported that ~28% of faculty might vote against tenure for a UAP-researching colleague even when believing the topic warranted study.

The Yingling et al. surveys are the most-cited empirical evidence for institutional UAP stigma in 2026 discourse (see [[src-academic-uap-stigma-2026-04]]).

**Pope Pius XII** (Eugenio Pacelli, 1876–1958) reigned as Pope from 1939 to 1958. He appears in UAP discourse via [[david-grusch]]'s 2023 congressional testimony, which alleged that Pius XII knew of a 1933 UFO crash near Milan and backchanneled information about the recovered craft to the U.S. government, which took possession after WWII.

The claim is unverified and remains a contested element of [[crash-retrieval-programs]] lore. It is rehearsed in [[the-age-of-disclosure]] (2025) and revisited in OSV News coverage of [[sam-sorich]]'s in-progress Catholic UAP documentary.

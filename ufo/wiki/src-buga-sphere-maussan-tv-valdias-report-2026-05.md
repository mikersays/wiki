# Valdías Mountain Range Sphere — Maussan TV Recap

## Summary

A pro-Maussan Medium recap by Edward Lithium summarizing the May 10 2026 segment of [[jaime-maussan]]'s Spanish-language TV program *No Humano*. The broadcast introduces a **second metallic sphere** — the **[[valdias-sphere|Valdías (Las Baldías) Mountain Range Sphere]]** — recovered May 2026 from a remote site on the edge of a national park near San Félix hill in the Medellín region of Colombia, and frames it as a sibling artifact to the [[buga-sphere]] presented at the NPC the prior week ([[src-greer-disclosure-event-npc-2026-05]]). Recovery is credited to **[[william-zuniga|William Suúñiga / Zúñiga]]** of an "international UFO hunters" group; the sphere was physically handed to Maussan in Mexico City for further study. All physical, electrical, and chain-of-custody claims are **single-source and unverified**.

## Key Claims

- A new artifact — the **Valdías Mountain Range Sphere** (alt. *Las Baldías*) — was recovered from a remote site near San Félix hill in the Antioquia / Medellín region of Colombia. **"Valdías" is a recovery-site name, not an alternate name for Buga** — the two spheres come from two distinct Colombian sites.
- **Trigger event:** On March 15 2026 — during the Buga Sphere's first major reveal at Mexico City's National Auditorium — Zúñiga's team in Medellín relayed (a) a daytime video of a craft over Medellín (broadcast live to ~8,000 attendees) and (b) a nighttime video of a luminous sphere descending into the Valdías range. A local couple corroborated the descent.
- **Recovery expedition:** Zúñiga's second attempt succeeded after 12+ days in the jungle, navigating by compass with no cell signal. He carried the sphere out in a backpack, sustaining severe back pain, a broken tooth, and a split tongue, while evading guerrilla and army checkpoints.
- **Physical comparison vs. Buga:** similar metallic appearance, "very hard aluminum" construction. Differences: **29 equatorial black dots** (Buga has 31), less surface damage, **no proto-Sanskrit-style external inscription** ([[david-velez-el-potro|Vélez]] speculates the message may be inside), a natural "belt" attachment, and a hole "with no continuity."
- **Site anomalies:** soil at "ground zero" allegedly emits electrical pulses on instrument readings; five samples taken in a cross pattern per engineer Rodolfo Garrido's protocol. A white stone with parallel stripes and an unnatural 90° cut was also collected.
- **Chain of custody:** sphere transported by Zúñiga through Medellín and Mexico City security; physically handed to Maussan on-air. Soil samples remain with the Medellín team. No named accredited laboratory, analyst, or methodology is given.
- **Release strategy:** Maussan — contrasting Buga — says he will **withhold individual findings** and only publish at the end of the full investigation, citing "haters and hackers." Planned platforms: *Interstellar*, *Tercer Milenio*, in-person appearances with Zúñiga, Vélez, and Pier Giorgio Caría.
- **Greer endorsement (paraphrased):** Maussan attributes to [[steven-greer]] (mis-spelled "Steven Green" in the source) the line that the Buga sphere is "extraterrestrial" and "too advanced for any ancient Earth culture" — consistent with Greer's NPC co-presentation.
- **Adjacent content:** the same broadcast referenced pulsating lights in Maryland on April 12 2026, analyzed by Arturo Rodríguez Cabello and Garrido, and expected new U.S. government UAP color-video releases.

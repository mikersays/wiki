**Matthew Steckman** is the president and chief business officer of [[anduril]]. In March 2026 reporting on the new $20B Army enterprise IDIQ contract vehicle for Anduril (see [[src-anduril-army-20b-counter-drone-idiq-2026-03]]), Steckman emphasized that the figure is a ceiling, not obligated funding — characterizing the vehicle as "an ordering guide where any buyer within the federal government can buy Anduril commercially made products."

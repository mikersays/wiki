# White Sands Missile Range

U.S. Army installation in southern New Mexico. Per Rep. [[gabe-vasquez]], has seen "exponential rate" of unidentified [[drone-incursions]]; primary motivating site for the [[counter-act|COUNTER Act]].

Per [[src-directed-energy-5-bases-pilot-2026-05]], White Sands has also served as a primary [[directed-energy-weapons]] [[counter-uas]] test venue. Col. [[scott-mclellan]] (deputy director, [[jiatf-401]]) cited White Sands testing — together with operational use along the U.S.–Mexico border — as evidence that DE C-UAS does not pose "undue risk to passenger aircraft," underwriting the May 2026 5-base pilot.

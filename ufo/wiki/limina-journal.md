***Limina: The Journal of UAP Studies*** is a double-blind peer-reviewed academic journal operated by the [[society-for-uap-studies]]. It is among the few formal peer-reviewed venues dedicated to [[uap-scientific-study]] (see [[src-academic-uap-stigma-2026-04]]).

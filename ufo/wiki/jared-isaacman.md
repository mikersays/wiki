# Jared Isaacman

**Jared Isaacman** is the [[nasa|NASA]] Administrator in [[donald-trump]]'s second administration. He is on-record as a co-principal of the May 2026 [[pursue]] interagency UAP declassification effort. NASA's contribution to Release 01 includes [[apollo-12]] and [[apollo-17]] photographs and Apollo crew voice transcripts — see [[lunar-uap-historical-claims]].

## UAP Disclosure Role

- **2026-05-08:** "At NASA, our job is to bring the brightest minds and most advanced scientific instruments to bear, follow the data, and share what we learn. We will remain candid about what we know to be true, what we have yet to understand, and all that remains to be discovered" ([[src-dow-uap-files-release-2026-05]]).
- Tone is notably more measured than the political principals — frames NASA's posture as data-driven and explicitly concessive about unknowns.

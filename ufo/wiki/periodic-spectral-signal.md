**Periodic spectral signal** — a [[technosignatures|technosignature]] class consisting of a **train of narrowband pulses** (sub-FM-channel bandwidth, periodic temporal structure). Distinct from:

- **Classical CW narrowband beacons** — continuous emission at a single frequency.
- **Pulsars** — naturally periodic but broadband.
- **Fast radio bursts** — singular or stochastic, generally broadband.
- **[[src-brats-radio-technosignatures-2026-05|Broadband leakage signatures]]** — terrestrial-style civilization radio leakage.

## Rationale

A train of pulses is **more energy-efficient** for interstellar communication than continuous emission ([[vishal-gajjar]], [[src-seti-galactic-center-search-2025-07]]). The combination of narrow bandwidth + periodic temporal structure is the discriminator from natural astrophysical sources ([[steve-croft]]).

# William Zúñiga

**William Suúñiga / Zúñiga** is a Medellín, Colombia–based UAP enthusiast described in [[src-buga-sphere-maussan-tv-valdias-report-2026-05]] as the lead of an "international UFO hunters" organization with claimed presence in 21+ countries and 300+ UAP videos. He is the credited recoverer of the [[valdias-sphere|Valdías Mountain Range Sphere]] in May 2026.

## Account (per [[jaime-maussan]]'s *No Humano* TV segment)

- After a March 15 2026 nighttime video of a luminous sphere descending into the Valdías mountain range near San Félix hill, organized a recovery expedition.
- After a failed first attempt, succeeded on a second 12+-day jungle expedition, navigating by compass without cell signal and evading guerrilla and Colombian-army checkpoints.
- Carried the sphere out in a backpack; sustained severe back pain, a broken tooth, and a split tongue.
- Took five soil samples in a cross pattern at "ground zero," per engineer Rodolfo Garrido's protocol; claims the soil emits electrical pulses on instrument readings.
- Physically delivered the sphere to Maussan in Mexico City on-air.

Treat all claims as **unverified, single-source**.

# COUNTER Act

Provision included in the 2026 NDAA, sponsored by Rep. [[gabe-vasquez]] (D-NM). Expands the definition of a "covered facility" so base commanders at any military base with a secure perimeter can defeat unidentified drones in their airspace.

Builds on the [[safer-skies-act]] (FY2025 NDAA) and is paired with the proposed [[secure-our-skies-drone-safety-act]]. Origin: [[drone-incursions]] at [[white-sands-missile-range]]. See [[ndaa-uap-provisions]], [[counter-uas]].

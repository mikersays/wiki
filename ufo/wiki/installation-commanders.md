**Installation commanders** are U.S. military base commanding officers empowered under [[10-usc-130i]] to detect and mitigate UAS threats to their installations. Under the December 8, 2025 SecWar-signed [[jiatf-401|JIATF-401]] homeland counter-UAS guidance ([[src-jiatf-401-counter-drone-homeland-guidance-2026-01]]), installation commanders must issue installation-specific operating procedures within **60 days** of the guidance.

The guidance frames installation commanders as the operational unit of decision in JIATF-401's homeland C-UAS doctrine, supported by [[joint-c-suas-university]] training, knowledge sharing, and the [[jiatf-401-marketplace]] materiel pipeline.

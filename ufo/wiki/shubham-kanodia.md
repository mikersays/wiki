# Shubham Kanodia

Astronomer at the [[carnegie-institution-for-science]]; co-lead of the JWST Cycle 2 [[gems-survey|GEMS Survey]] (Red Dwarfs and the Seven Giants) with [[caleb-canas]] and [[jessica-libby-roberts]].

## Work

- **2023**: Led ground-based follow-up confirming [[toi-5205-b]] from [[tess|TESS]] candidate data.
- **2026**: Co-led the JWST atmosphere characterization of [[toi-5205-b]] reported in [[src-sciencedaily-forbidden-exoplanet-atmosphere-2026-04]]. Provided the interpretive frame for interior–atmosphere decoupling and inward heavy-element migration.
- Co-developed (with Wallack) a starspot-correction technique for [[m-dwarf-stars|M-dwarf]] [[transmission-spectroscopy|transmission spectra]] now extended to a follow-on JWST program.

> "We observed much lower metallicity than our models predicted... its heavy elements migrated inward during formation and now its interior and atmosphere are not mixing." — Shubham Kanodia

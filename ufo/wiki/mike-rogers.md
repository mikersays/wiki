# Mike Rogers (R-AL)

U.S. Representative (R-AL); chair of the [[house-armed-services-committee]]. Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Rogers told Rep. [[eric-burlison]] the [[uap-disclosure-act]] could only be folded into the FY2026 NDAA with **"Four Corners" sign-off** — concurrence from chairs and ranking members of HASC, SASC, HPSCI, and SSCI. Liberation Times sources characterize this precondition as designed to fail. See [[four-corners-ndaa-process]].

# Hegseth FY27 DoW Posture Testimony — April 29, 2026

## Summary

Primary-source written testimony of [[pete-hegseth|Secretary of War Pete Hegseth]] before the [[house-armed-services-committee]] on the FY27 [[department-of-war|Department of War]] budget request ($1.5 trillion topline). The testimony formally institutionalizes [[aaro]] as the U.S. government's "focal point" for detecting, tracking, identifying, and mitigating UAP, and commits to continued classified and unclassified Congressional briefings plus public document releases. Two distinct uses of "anomalous" appear: AARO's UAP mission and the [[anomalous-health-incidents|Anomalous Health Incidents (AHI)]] Cross-Functional Team — realigned under USW(R&E) — with nearly $3 million in HAVANA Act payments disbursed since Trump's inauguration. Hegseth names "[[drone-dominance|drone dominance]]" and "[[autonomous-warfare|autonomous warfare]]" as FY27 priorities and identifies [[golden-dome-for-america|Golden Dome for America]] as the homeland aerial-threat-defense program. [[operation-epic-fury]] is acknowledged as ongoing in the Middle East, alongside Operations Rough Rider, Midnight Hammer, and Absolute Resolve.

## Key Claims

- **AARO is the U.S. government's "focal point"** for UAP detect/track/identify/mitigate; commits to "rigorous scientific and intelligence-based" approach (Hegseth).
- AARO will continue regular UAP document releases and Congressional briefings "at all levels of classification" (Hegseth).
- **Anomalous Health Incidents (AHI)** Cross-Functional Team realigned to OUSW(R&E); first HAVANA Act payments approved; ~$3M disbursed since Trump's inauguration; Navy Rear Admiral SEAL/MD added to leadership team (Hegseth).
- "**Drone dominance**" named as a top FY27 investment priority alongside space superiority and magazine depth (Hegseth).
- "**Autonomous warfare**" listed as a domain where strategic competitors (China, Russia) are investing and which the U.S. must counter (Hegseth).
- **Golden Dome for America** described as "a revolutionary approach to defending our nation from advanced aerial threats," created by one of Trump's first executive orders (Hegseth).
- [[operation-epic-fury]] is acknowledged as ongoing; cited alongside Rough Rider, Midnight Hammer, and Absolute Resolve as proof of "second-to-none operational flexibility" (Hegseth).
- FY27 topline: **$1.5 trillion**, up from FY26's $1 trillion (Hegseth).
- Nuclear posture: explicit reference to **testing** "nuclear weapons and nuclear delivery systems on an equal basis as others"; triad modernization (Hegseth).
- **CYBERCOM 2.0** described as the most comprehensive overhaul since founding (Hegseth).
- **MQ-9 Reaper production** restarted; space-based **Air Moving Target Indicator (AMTI)** under development (Hegseth).
- Strong support for **FISA Section 702** reauthorization (Hegseth).
- Sharp criticism of NATO allies for not supporting U.S. operations against Iran: "we will remember" (Hegseth).
- Document uses "[[department-of-war|Department of War]] / DoW" and "Secretary of War" branding throughout.
- [[jiatf-401]], [[norad]], [[usnorthcom]], and homeland C-UAS commands are not named in the written testimony — implied via [[golden-dome-for-america|Golden Dome]] and CYBERCOM 2.0 framing only.

## Notable Quotes

> "The All-Domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) is the focal point for the government's efforts to detect, track, identify, and mitigate UAP. The AARO's mission is to bring a rigorous scientific and intelligence-based approach to the study of these reported phenomena. To further transparency, AARO regularly releases UAP documents of public interest, and will continue to brief Congress at all levels of classification." — Pete Hegseth

> "Under this Administration, we are fully meeting the intent of Congress on Anomalous Health Incidents, or AHIs... we have approved the first HAVANA Act payment to victims, with the DoW disbursing nearly $3 million in compensation since President Trump's Inauguration." — Pete Hegseth

> "[The FY27 budget invests in] space superiority, establishing drone dominance, increasing magazine depth, and maintaining superiority across all warfighting domains." — Pete Hegseth

> "[Strategic competitors are investing in] space dominance, hypersonic weapons, autonomous warfare, nuclear deterrence, and conventional capabilities." — Pete Hegseth

> "Golden Dome for America... a revolutionary approach to defending our nation from advanced aerial threats." — Pete Hegseth

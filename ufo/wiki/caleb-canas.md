# Caleb Cañas

Astronomer at [[nasa-goddard|NASA Goddard Space Flight Center]]; lead author of the 2026 [[jwst|JWST]] [[toi-5205-b]] atmosphere paper and co-lead of the [[gems-survey|GEMS Survey]] (Red Dwarfs and the Seven Giants).

## Work

- Lead author on the [[src-sciencedaily-forbidden-exoplanet-atmosphere-2026-04|2026 TOI-5205 b transmission-spectrum paper]] (*The Astronomical Journal*).
- Builds Goddard's GEMS-class observational program alongside [[shubham-kanodia]] (Carnegie) and [[jessica-libby-roberts]] (University of Tampa).

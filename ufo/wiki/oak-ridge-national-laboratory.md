**Oak Ridge National Laboratory** (ORNL) is a DOE national laboratory in Oak Ridge, Tennessee. ORNL has partnered with [[aaro]] on UAP detection capabilities and analytical methods, as disclosed in the [[src-odni-fy2024-uap-annual-report-2024|FY2024 ODNI/AARO annual report]].

Oak Ridge also features prominently in historical UAP records: the [[fbi-case-file-62-hq-83894|FBI case file 62-HQ-83894]] (1947-1968) includes photographs and reports from Oak Ridge, and the PURSUE Release 02 [[src-dow-pursue-release-02-2026-05|116-page Sandia NM 1948-1950 file]] documents UAP sightings at nuclear installations during the same era.

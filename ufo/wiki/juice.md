**JUICE** (JUpiter ICy moons Explorer) is an [[esa]] mission launched April 2023 to study Jupiter's icy moons — Ganymede, Callisto, and [[europa]]. It is scheduled to arrive at the Jupiter system in **2031**, one year after [[europa-clipper]].

## Relevance

Together with Europa Clipper, JUICE will follow up on [[juno]]'s 2022 ice-shell thickness measurement (~29 km conductive layer) to further characterize Europa's subsurface ocean and assess [[ocean-worlds]] habitability.

# Abdallah Zaki

Sedimentologist; former [[caltech]] postdoc, now Distinguished Postdoctoral Fellow at UT Austin's Jackson School. Co-author with [[michael-lamb]] of the April 2026 *Nature* paper proposing continental-shelf topographic evidence for an [[ancient-mars-ocean|ancient northern Martian ocean]] (see [[src-mars-ancient-ocean-zaki-lamb-2026-04]]).

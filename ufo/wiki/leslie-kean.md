**Leslie Kean** is an investigative journalist and a foundational figure in modern UAP disclosure. She co-authored the December 2017 *New York Times* story that revealed the Pentagon's [[aatip|AATIP]] program and the Nimitz "Tic Tac" UAP videos — widely regarded as the event that returned UAP to mainstream credibility. She is the author of *UFOs: Generals, Pilots, and Government Officials Go on the Record* (2010).

## UAP activity

- Co-hosting (with filmmaker [[james-fox]]) the **June 9, 2026** Capitol Hill UAP disclosure press conference featuring whistleblower [[david-grusch]] and Reps. [[eric-burlison]], [[anna-paulina-luna]], [[tim-burchett]], and [[jared-moskowitz]]. See [[src-grusch-disclosure-press-conference-2026-05]].
- Her 2017 reporting is part of the lineage that the current [[pursue|PURSUE]] disclosure push and the [[uap-disclosure-act]] build upon.

**Roger Wicker** is a Republican U.S. Senator from Mississippi and chairman of the Senate Armed Services Committee. On CBS [[src-60-minutes-drone-swarms-2025|60 Minutes]] (March 2025) he said the Pentagon and senior national-security advisors remain "mystified" by [[drone-incursions]] despite classified briefings, and stated that the activity logically appears to be "spying incursions."

As SASC chair, Wicker oversees [[ndaa-uap-provisions|NDAA legislation]] including FY2026 provisions requiring AARO briefings on UAP intercepts.

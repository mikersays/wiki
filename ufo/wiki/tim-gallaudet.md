# Tim Gallaudet

Retired U.S. Navy Rear Admiral, former naval oceanographer, and former [[noaa]] acting administrator. As of 2024, signed no UAP-related NDA. Prominent UAP disclosure advocate; on-camera in the 2025 documentary [[the-age-of-disclosure]].

## January 2015 Roosevelt incident

While Commander, [[naval-meteorology-and-oceanography-command]], received an "URGENT SAFETY OF FLIGHT ISSUE" email from the [[us-fleet-forces-command|U.S. Fleet Forces Command]] operations officer warning of multiple near-midair collisions during a [[uss-theodore-roosevelt|USS Theodore Roosevelt]] pre-deployment exercise off the East Coast. Attached: the now-declassified [[go-fast-video|"Go Fast" FLIR video]]. The email vanished from his and other recipients' accounts the next day; the matter was never raised again, even in weekly safety meetings. Gallaudet inferred a compartmented [[crash-retrieval-programs|special access program]] excluding flag officers (see [[src-gallaudet-house-testimony-2024-11]]).

## House Oversight testimony — November 13, 2024

Testified at the joint hearing "Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena: Exposing the Truth" with three pillars:

1. Congressional oversight of [[aaro]], [[odni]], NSA on UAP; pursue UAP at IC, NASA, DOE, DHS, NSF; leverage UAP to fix [[drone-incursions]] vulnerabilities.
2. Pass the [[uap-disclosure-act]]'s [[uap-records-review-board|UAP Records Review Board]] provisions stripped from FY2024 NDAA conference.
3. After enactment, designate a cabinet department lead for whole-of-government UAP coordination (endorsed by [[sol-foundation]]).

Alleged that during a 2024 meeting with then-acting AARO director [[tim-phillips]] and senior staff, he was the target of an "hours-long influence operation" defending AARO's Historical Records Report Vol. 1 and disparaging UAP whistleblowers ([[src-gallaudet-house-testimony-2024-11]]).

## May 1, 2025 expert briefing

Briefed Capitol Hill (hosted by [[uap-disclosure-fund|UAP Disclosure Fund]] + [[house-oversight-committee|House Oversight & Accountability]]) alongside [[chris-mellon]] and [[avi-loeb]]. Recounted a 2021 [[uss-dwight-d-eisenhower|USS Dwight D. Eisenhower]] SH-60 Seahawk FLIR account of a metallic sphere accelerating to the horizon, plus many F-18 UAP encounters during the Eisenhower Strike Group deployment ([[src-uap-expert-briefing-2025-05]], [[uss-eisenhower-uap-incident]]). Framed institutional response as "over-classification and a deliberate, decades-long disinformation campaign by the U.S. Department of Defense and the intelligence community."

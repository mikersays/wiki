**Ross Coulthart** is an Australian investigative journalist known for in-depth coverage of UAP/UFO topics. He has closely followed the careers of key disclosure figures including [[lue-elizondo]] and has reported extensively on [[uap-disclosure]] developments for [[newsnation]] (the *Reality Check* podcast) and other outlets.

Coulthart publicly shared details of Elizondo's March 17, 2026 near-fatal motorcycle crash and urged helmet use in public safety messaging (see [[src-elizondo-motorcycle-crash-2026-04]]).

## On Trump's disclosure directive (Feb 2026)

In [[src-newsnation-elizondo-pandoras-box-2026-02]] (Feb 21, 2026), Coulthart issued a sharply procedural caution against over-reading [[donald-trump]]'s Feb 19 Truth Social announcement: Trump "is not saying… that he is going to declassify anything. All they are talking about is **reviewing records**." This review-not-declassification caveat was largely absent from disclosure-community reception of the directive and held up against the eventual May 8 [[pursue]] launch, which surfaced existing-FOIA-holdings material rather than newly declassified content.

**Rep. Eric Burlison** (R-MO) is a leading UAP legislative figure in the 119th Congress, serving on the House Oversight Committee alongside [[anna-paulina-luna]] and [[tim-burchett]].

## Key Actions

- Introduced the **UAP Disclosure Act of 2025** as an NDAA amendment on August 29, 2025, seeking government record preservation, National Archives access, and an independent declassification board for UAP records.
- At the September 9, 2025 House hearing ([[src-uap-transparency-hearing-2026-04]]), publicly released for the first time footage of an MQ-9 firing a Hellfire missile at a high-speed orb off Yemen (October 2024). The object was struck but continued on course, apparently intact.
- Co-drafted UAP transparency legislation with Rep. Luna and Rep. Burchett.
- **April 2026:** referred the May 2024 death of UAP whistleblower [[sullivan-whistleblower|Sullivan]] (Bronze Star intelligence officer, [[nasic]]/[[nsa]]) to the [[fbi]] as "sudden and suspicious," days before he was scheduled to testify on [[crash-retrieval-programs]] ([[src-sullivan-whistleblower-death-2026-04]]). Adds a whistleblower-safety advocacy dimension to his prior legislative profile.

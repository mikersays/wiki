# Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution (WHOI)

Private Massachusetts oceanographic research institution; lead institution of the [[nasa]]-funded **[[invow|Investigating Ocean Worlds (InvOW)]]** project (2026–2031, ~$5M, 16-laboratory consortium). PI [[chris-german]] (WHOI senior scientist) and co-I Susan Lang (WHOI) anchor InvOW's terrestrial-ocean and seafloor-hydrothermal expertise.

WHOI previously led the predecessor NASA-funded **[[exow|Exploring Ocean Worlds (ExOW)]]** initiative — also under German — which focused on physical and geological processes rather than [[biosignatures|organic-biosignature]] interpretation.

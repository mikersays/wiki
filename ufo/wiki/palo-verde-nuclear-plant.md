**Palo Verde Nuclear Generating Station** is an Arizona nuclear power plant — the largest single-site electricity producer in the United States. Palo Verde was the target of an unexplained 2019 drone incursion first documented by [[the-war-zone]] and later reported on national television by CBS [[src-60-minutes-drone-swarms-2025|60 Minutes]].

The Palo Verde case is one of several [[drone-incursions]] over U.S. nuclear or nuclear-adjacent sites, feeding the broader [[nuclear-uap-correlation]] pattern.

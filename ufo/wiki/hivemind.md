**Hivemind** is [[shield-ai]]'s swarm/autonomy software stack, pitched as an "AI pilot" for autonomous aircraft. It enables collaborative autonomy in communications-constrained environments under a single human operator — dynamically rerouting mission plans, responding to unexpected conditions, and avoiding obstacles — in contrast to preplanned-route autopilots.

In May 2026 the Pentagon selected Hivemind to act as the AI pilot for the [[lucas-drone|LUCAS]] one-way attack drone, with a fall 2026 capability demonstration planned. The selection ties Hivemind to [[operation-epic-fury]] and the FY27 [[autonomous-warfare]] / [[drone-dominance]] budget lines.

**Tic Tac UAP** is the elongated, cigar-shaped UAP shape class named for the November 2004 Nimitz/Baja California sighting by F/A-18 Super Hornet pilot Cmdr. David Fravor. The class has recurred across military observations, including a [[hc-144-ocean-sentry|USCG HC-144 Ocean Sentry]] FLIR capture previewed for [[pursue]] Release 02.

Distinguish from [[metallic-orb]] (spherical) and from [[transmedium-uap]] (cross-medium transitions).

# Control-System Hypothesis

[[jacques-vallee|Jacques Vallée]]'s thesis that the UAP/UFO phenomenon operates as a **cybernetic feedback loop**: it presents absurd or otherwise disorienting stimuli, observes the cultural response, and adjusts the form of subsequent manifestations to maintain a particular relationship with human consciousness over time. The hypothesis explains the historical mutability of the phenomenon (airships → flying saucers → crash retrievals → orbs) without requiring a fixed physical ET-craft inventory and is the formal complement to Vallée's [[psychological-uap-framing|psychological-phenomenon]] reading.

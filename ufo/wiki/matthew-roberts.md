# Matthew Roberts

Former **Office of Naval Intelligence** cryptologist and intelligence analyst, and a self-described experiencer of "the Phenomenon." Roberts states he was aboard the **USS *Theodore Roosevelt*** during the collection of the **Gimbal** and **Go Fast** UAP footage, and that he began having follow-on anomalous experiences in the aftermath. He is the author of *Initiated: UAP, Dreams, Depression, Delusions, Shadow People, Psychosis, Sleep Paralysis, and Pandemics*.

At the July 2026 [[src-breaking-the-taboo-uap-contact-symposium-2026-05|"Breaking the Taboo" symposium]], Roberts presents "The Phenomenon as a Driver of Evolutionary Psychological Development," framing contact experiences as catalysts of psychological maturation. His military-sensor proximity makes him a notable bridge between the [[alien-abduction-phenomenon|experiencer]] community and the official Navy UAP-footage cases.

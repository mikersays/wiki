**Majestic 12** (MJ-12) is an alleged secret committee of scientists, military leaders, and government officials supposedly formed in 1947 by President Truman to facilitate the recovery and investigation of alien spacecraft.

## Disinformation Campaign

In the 1980s, AFOSI used falsified MJ-12 documents as part of a disinformation campaign to manipulate beliefs in extraterrestrial visitors and deter UFO researchers considered threats to U.S. national security (Pilkington 2010, Bishop 2005). [[richard-doty]] was involved in disseminating these forgeries as part of the broader Bennewitz Affair.

According to [[src-uap-reddit-disclosure-conspiracy-2026-05]], while the [[robertson-panel]]'s 1953 goal was to deter public discussion of UFOs, the Majestic 12 disinformation campaign specifically targeted individual researchers. Both are cited by Bastos & Duarte (2026) as examples of [[confirmed-conspiracy-theory|confirmed conspiracy theories]] — once-speculative claims later substantiated by documentary evidence.

## Ongoing Debate

The existence of an actual MJ-12 committee remains unverified. The confirmed element is the disinformation campaign that used fabricated MJ-12 documents, not the committee itself.

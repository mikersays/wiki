# Sandia National Laboratories

U.S. Department of Energy national laboratory; nuclear-weapons engineering and impact-physics expertise. Co-author institution on the 2026 LPSC Earth-to-Venus [[panspermia]] modeling study reported in [[src-venus-panspermia-earth-origin-2026-04]] — supplied bolide-fragmentation ("pancake-model") simulations used to estimate microbial transfer rates from Earth to Venus's clouds.

## Notes per Agency

### FBI (56 records, all from one case file)
All 56 FBI entries reference the same case file: **62-HQ-83894** (1947–1968, "Unidentified Flying Objects and flying discs"). 48 PDFs are sections/serials; 8 IMGs are photographic evidence including the "ellipsoid bronze metallic object" composite sketch (130–195 ft, September 2023 sighting). See [[fbi-case-file-62-hq-83894]]. Per the official description, this is the **complete case file** with newly declassified pages and minor redactions — distinct from the partially-public FBI Vault version.

### NASA (16 records — all blank-agency rows are NASA-titled)
Spans the U.S. crewed-spaceflight program from Gemini through Apollo and Skylab:

- **[[gemini-7]]** — Dec 1965 transcript (NASA-UAP-D3); Frank Borman / Jim Lovell mission. *New to wiki.*
- **[[apollo-11]]** — 1969 Technical Crew Debriefing (NASA-UAP-D4). *New to wiki.*
- **[[apollo-12]]** — 1969 transcript (NASA-UAP-D1) + 5 photographs (NASA-UAP-VM1–VM5).
- **[[apollo-17]]** — 1972 transcript (NASA-UAP-D2), 1973 Crew Debriefing for Science (NASA-UAP-D5), 1973 Technical Crew Debriefing (NASA-UAP-D6) + 1 photograph (NASA-UAP-VM6).
- **[[skylab]]** — 1973 Technical Crew Debriefing (NASA-UAP-D7). *New to wiki.*
- **1965 Low Earth Orbit "255-T-763" tape** — Dec 5 1965 PDF transcript + video excerpt. *New to wiki.*
- **"UFO's and Defense — What Should We Prepare For"** — undated NASA paper (Record 255_413270).

See [[lunar-uap-historical-claims]] for the umbrella concept; [[nasa]] for the agency-level summary.

### Department of War (82 records)
Two structurally distinct subsets:

1. **Historical / Cold War-era records (~10 PDFs)** — pre-1960 military intelligence files including:
   - "319.1 Flying Discs 1949"
   - "General 1946-7", "General 1948"
   - "Incident Summaries 1-100, 101-172, 173-233" (boxes from Record 38_143685)
   - Numerical Files (1944-1945 Germany, 1955 Azerbaijan, 1948-1955 Netherlands)
2. **DOW-UAP-D series (~70 records, 27 videos + ~45 PDFs)** — Mission Reports from Iraq, Syria, UAE, Greece, Persian Gulf, Aegean Sea, Mediterranean, Strait of Hormuz, Gulf of Oman, etc., dated 2020–2026. Heavy Middle East operational concentration — likely the bulk of [[centcom|CENTCOM]]-AOR encounters that produced public 2022–2024 UAP video interest.

Distinct location count: 25× Western United States, 12× Syria, 8× Iraq, 7× Arabian Gulf, 6× Persian Gulf, plus single-instances from Detroit, Iran, Djibouti, East China Sea, Japan, Pacific, etc.

# Italo Venturelli

Brazilian physician and alleged first-hand witness to the [[varginha-incident]]. Says he was called on January 20, 1996 to medically assess one of the non-human entities allegedly captured by the Brazilian government near Varginha, Minas Gerais.

## Public account

Venturelli described the patient as conscious, calm, breathing well, with no pain or bleeding; physically resembling a 7-year-old, white, with a large teardrop-shaped head, small mouth, and "a beautiful gaze, one without judgment." He said the entity's manner of looking suggested "an intelligence greater than ours, than human" and that despite its defenseless position it appeared unafraid and aware of the situation. ([[src-burlison-marching-orders-2026-01]])

He spoke publicly at the January 2026 30th-anniversary UAP press conference hosted by [[james-fox]], where Rep. [[eric-burlison]] also appeared.

# Americans for Safe Aerospace

Advocacy organization founded by [[ryan-graves]] (former Navy F-18 pilot, AIAA UAP Integration Committee chair) pushing for FAA-mandated UAP reporting procedures and treatment of UAP encounters as aviation-safety issues. Endorsed by [[tim-gallaudet]] in his November 2024 House Oversight testimony ([[src-gallaudet-house-testimony-2024-11]]).

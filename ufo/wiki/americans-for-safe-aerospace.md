# Americans for Safe Aerospace

Advocacy organization founded by [[ryan-graves]] (former Navy F-18 pilot, AIAA UAP Integration Committee chair) pushing for FAA-mandated UAP reporting procedures and treatment of UAP encounters as aviation-safety issues. Endorsed by [[tim-gallaudet]] in his November 2024 House Oversight testimony ([[src-gallaudet-house-testimony-2024-11]]). Gallaudet also serves as a named advisor per [[src-defensescoop-pursue-not-disclosure-2026-05]].

## PURSUE-era priorities (May 2026)

Per [[src-defensescoop-pursue-not-disclosure-2026-05]], ASA is mining [[pursue]] Release 01 for **aviation-safety-relevant records**: sensor data, pilot and aircrew reports, analyses of operational hazards, and process changes that let commercial and military aviators report encounters without [[uap-stigma|stigma]]. Operates as the pilot-witness counterpart to [[enigma-labs]]'s civilian-sightings analytics in the broader [[uap-reporting-infrastructure]].

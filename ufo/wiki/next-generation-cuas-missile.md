**Next Generation cUAS Missile (NGCM)** — also marketed as **Freedom Eagle-1**. Kinetic counter-drone interceptor missile manufactured by [[aerovironment]] for the U.S. Army.

## Procurement

- **FY27 budget request:** 24 NGCM units, embedded in a $132M Army "effectors" line that also funds 800 kinetic and 29 non-kinetic effectors ([[src-breaking-defense-army-1b-counter-drone-2026-05]]).
- Sits within the Army's broader FY27 ~$994M small-counter-UAS portfolio, alongside the [[enduring-high-energy-laser|E-HEL]] directed-energy line and the legacy [[family-of-counter-uas|FoCUS]] program.

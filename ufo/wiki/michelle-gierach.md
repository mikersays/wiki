# Michelle Gierach

**Michelle Gierach** is a [[nasa-jpl|NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory]] scientist who led development of a self-supervised machine-learning tool that fuses data from five satellite missions/instruments (including PACE and TROPOMI) to detect harmful algal blooms off Florida and California, published in [[american-geophysical-union|AGU]]'s *Earth and Space Science*. The work is a ground-truthable Earth-observation analogue for remote [[biosignatures|biosignature]]-detection methodology ([[src-nasa-orbital-biosignature-algae-ai-2026-05]]). Co-authors include [[kelly-luis|Kelly Luis]] (NASA JPL) and Nick LaHaye (Spatial Informatics Group).

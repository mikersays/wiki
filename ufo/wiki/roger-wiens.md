**Roger Wiens** is a professor at [[purdue-university]] (EAPS) and a principal investigator on the Mars rover [[supercam]] LIBS instrument. His Purdue lab built the nickel-rich calibration standards that enabled the 2026 [[neretva-vallis]] nickel quantification (see [[src-perseverance-neretva-nickel-rocks-2026-04]]); follow-up calibration work was performed at [[los-alamos-national-laboratory]], where Wiens previously held leadership of the SuperCam team.

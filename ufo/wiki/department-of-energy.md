# Department of Energy

The **U.S. Department of Energy (DOE)** is a Cabinet-level department whose mission spans nuclear-weapons stewardship, civilian nuclear-energy oversight, and basic-science research at the national laboratory complex. DOE relevance to the UFO/UAP wiki sits at two intersections: (1) its national laboratories' historical and present-day involvement in classified materials-science work and instrumentation that intersects UAP investigations, and (2) its inclusion in the May 2026 [[pursue]] interagency disclosure architecture.

## UAP Disclosure Role

- **2026-05-08:** Named as an interagency participant in the [[pursue]] inaugural release ([[src-uap-files-public-conclusions-2026-05]], [[src-war-department-uap-declassification-2026-05]]) — the first explicit DOE inclusion in the federal UAP-disclosure architecture documented in this vault.
- DOE labs already in the wiki: [[los-alamos-national-laboratory]], [[sandia-national-laboratories]].

# Anna Kelly

**Anna Kelly** is a White House deputy press secretary in the second [[donald-trump]] administration. She is the first administration spokesperson named in this wiki on the record for [[uap-disclosure]] messaging:

> "President Trump directed the Department of War to begin the process of identifying and releasing government files related to alien and extraterrestrial life, unidentified aerial phenomena, and unidentified flying objects because he is the most transparent President in history. Stay tuned." — Anna Kelly, April 2026

The statement formally confirmed the [[department-of-war]] tasking that originated in Trump's February 2026 directive ([[src-uap-trump-disclosure-2026]]).

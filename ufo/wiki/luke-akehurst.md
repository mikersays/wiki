# Luke Akehurst

UK Labour MP for North Durham. Led the 27 November 2024 Westminster Hall cross-party debate criticizing UK [[ground-based-air-defence|GBAD]] gaps against the [[drone-incursions]] over USAF UK bases. Revealed the business case for additional [[sky-sabre]] launchers had been approved but no orders placed, and that no business case existed for additional missiles.

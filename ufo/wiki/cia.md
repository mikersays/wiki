The **Central Intelligence Agency (CIA)** is the primary U.S. foreign-intelligence agency. In the vault, CIA appears as the document originator for two UAP records surfaced by [[pursue]]:

- **1955 [[richard-russell|Sen. Richard Russell]] Soviet-train sighting** — partially-redacted CIA cable released as part of PURSUE Release 01 ([[src-npr-pursue-release-2026-05]]).
- **1973 [[sary-shagan]] Soviet-source UAP report** — bright-green concentric-circle object at the Soviet missile-defense test range, surfaced via PURSUE Release 02 ([[src-pentagon-second-batch-ufo-release-2026-05]]).
- **1972 USSR concentric-green-circles sighting** — per [[src-nbc-pursue-firsthand-encounters-2026-05]], a CIA officer in the USSR watched a green circle widen into concentric circles after a Canada–USSR sports competition. NBC dates this to 1972; it may be the same record as, or distinct from, the 1973 [[sary-shagan]] report — both describe Soviet bright-green concentric-circle UAP.

CIA is one of the interagency participants in PURSUE alongside [[department-of-state]], [[nasa]], FBI, and the [[department-of-energy]].

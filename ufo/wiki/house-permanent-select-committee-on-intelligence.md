# House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence (HPSCI)

Standing select committee of the U.S. House of Representatives with oversight of the U.S. Intelligence Community. One of three House committees to which the **UAP Whistleblower Protection Act (H.R. 5060)** was referred in August 2025, alongside the [[house-oversight-committee|House Oversight and Government Reform Committee]] and the [[house-armed-services-committee|House Armed Services Committee]].

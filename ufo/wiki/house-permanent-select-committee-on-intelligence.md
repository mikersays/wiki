# House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence (HPSCI)

Standing select committee of the U.S. House of Representatives with oversight of the U.S. Intelligence Community. One of three House committees to which the **UAP Whistleblower Protection Act (H.R. 5060)** was referred in August 2025, alongside the [[house-oversight-committee|House Oversight and Government Reform Committee]] and the [[house-armed-services-committee|House Armed Services Committee]]. ([[src-uap-whistleblower-protection-act-2025-09]])

## Mike Turner posture (2023–)

Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], former HPSCI chair [[mike-turner]] (R-OH) reportedly opposed key elements of the [[uap-disclosure-act]] during the FY2024 NDAA conference committee, contributing to the bill's dilution from its original Schumer–Rounds form. HPSCI concurrence is one element of the [[four-corners-ndaa-process|"Four Corners" precondition]] [[mike-rogers]] later imposed for FY2026 inclusion.

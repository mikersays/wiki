# El Paso International Airport

Texas regional airport. In early February 2026 the [[faa]] imposed a sudden TFR around the airport citing a purported [[cartel-drone-threat|cartel drone]]; per Rep. [[gabe-vasquez]], the incident was actually caused by [[department-of-war|DoD]] / FAA / [[department-of-homeland-security|DHS]] miscommunication during unapproved [[counter-uas|C-UAS]] testing in protected airspace.

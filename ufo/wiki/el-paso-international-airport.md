# El Paso International Airport

Texas regional airport. In early February 2026 the [[faa]] imposed a sudden TFR around the airport citing a purported [[cartel-drone-threat|cartel drone]]; per Rep. [[gabe-vasquez]], the incident was actually caused by [[department-of-war|DoD]] / FAA / [[department-of-homeland-security|DHS]] miscommunication during unapproved [[counter-uas|C-UAS]] testing in protected airspace.

Breaking Defense ([[src-northcom-southern-border-counter-drone-sandbox-2026-05]]) adds specificity: the closure resulted from the DoD firing a counter-drone **[[directed-energy-weapons|laser]]** system, which closed the airport and surrounding airspace for several hours. The episode prompted an [[faa]]/DoD assessment (~April 2026) that subsequently cleared high-energy laser C-UAS for border use as not posing "undue risk to passenger aircraft."

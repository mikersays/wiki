# Elisabeth Matthews

Astronomer at the [[max-planck-institute-for-astronomy]] in Heidelberg; lead author of the 2026 [[jwst]] MIRI coronagraph study of [[epsilon-indi-ab]] reporting [[water-ice-clouds]] on a Jupiter analogue ([[src-jwst-ice-clouds-exoplanet-2026-04]]).

## Notable contributions

- 2026 *Nature* / equivalent: Direct-imaging detection of patchy water-ice clouds on Epsilon Indi Ab via anomalously low NH₃ retrieval.
- Frames the result as the first time JWST has allowed solar-system analogue planets to be studied "in detail" — a Jupiter-equivalent observation from external vantage.

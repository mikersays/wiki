# Paul Peyerl

Subject of an FBI document surfaced via [[pursue]] Release 01 and recapped by [[src-euronews-trump-162-ufo-files-2026-05|Euronews (May 9 2026)]]. Peyerl claims that during WWII the Nazis built a **disc-shaped, jet-engine-equipped, remotely-controlled aircraft** in Germany's Black Forest in 1944.

## Claimed observations

- Approximately **6.5 metres in diameter**.
- **Remotely controlled.**
- Several **jet engines on the outer part** of the structure.
- The **outer section rotated around a stationary central dome**.
- Photographed in flight at **7,000 m altitude**; a Xerox of the negative is appended to the FBI file.
- Photographed in a hangar "at the risk of his own life."
- Allegedly designed by a German engineer named **Kuehr** (first name unknown), suspected arrested by the Allies after the war.

## Provenance

FBI document released as part of the May 8 2026 [[pursue|PURSUE]] Release 01 tranche; preserved within the broader FBI UAP records collection. The [[fbi]] explicitly states the document contains "**neither recommendations nor conclusions**," making the claim unverifiable from agency adjudication alone.

**Barack Obama** is the 44th U.S. President. His February 2026 remarks on Brian Tyler Cohen's podcast ("They're real but I haven't seen them") went viral and are widely credited as the trigger for [[donald-trump]]'s February 2026 [[uap-disclosure]] directive ([[src-uap-trump-disclosure-2026]]).

Obama later clarified on social media that he "saw no evidence during my presidency that extraterrestrials have made contact with us" — framing his podcast comment as referring to the statistical likelihood of life elsewhere in the universe rather than confirmed UAP incidents.

As president, Obama signed **Executive Order 13526** (2009), which establishes the framework for classified national security information and underpins the presidential declassification authority now being invoked for UAP releases.

# Turning Point USA

**Turning Point USA (TPUSA)** is a U.S. conservative youth political organization. It hosted the **TPUSA Action Conference in Phoenix on April 17, 2026**, where President [[donald-trump]] publicly previewed imminent UAP file releases — telling the crowd the first releases would begin "very, very soon" ([[src-trump-tpusa-ufo-tease-2026-04]]).

The choice of venue is itself a data point: Trump framed the disclosure topic as something he "saved for this crowd" because attendees were "a little bit out there," signaling he is positioning [[uap-disclosure]] as MAGA-base messaging.

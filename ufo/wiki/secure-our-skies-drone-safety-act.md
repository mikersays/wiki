# Secure Our Skies Drone Safety Act of 2025

Proposed bipartisan bill introduced by Rep. [[gabe-vasquez]] (D-NM). Would task the GAO with auditing federal/state/local/tribal use of UAS and [[counter-uas|C-UAS]], examining foreign-vs-domestic drone procurement, quantifying drones bought from "adversarial entities," and recommending legal/policy changes.

Currently pending in the [[house-transportation-infrastructure-committee]]. Vasquez plans to attach it to the bipartisan Surface Reauthorization Bill as must-pass legislation.

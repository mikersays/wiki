# AirSight

Industry vendor of counter-UAS products, including the **AirGuard** [[remote-id|Remote ID]] detection system. Publishes analyst commentary at airsight.com/blog.

## Relevance to UAP / counter-UAS vault

- Authored the May 6, 2026 industry-side analysis ([[src-faa-drone-rule-critical-infrastructure-2026-05]]) of the FAA's [[unmanned-aircraft-flight-restriction|UAFR]] notice of proposed rulemaking — flagged the §74.56 Remote ID receiving requirement as the rule's most operationally significant provision.
- **Disclosure of interest:** AirSight sells the equipment the new federal baseline mandates, so its framing should be read as advocacy as well as analysis.

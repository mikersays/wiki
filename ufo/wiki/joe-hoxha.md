**Joe Hoxha** is a Connecticut state representative (R-Bristol) who introduced **H.B. 5422**, a bill directing the University of Connecticut to partner with state agencies to study UAP and explore establishing a dedicated state research center. Self-described longtime UFO enthusiast and researcher, he frames the bill as objective scientific inquiry rather than conspiracy ([[src-connecticut-uap-disclosure-bill-2026]]).

The **Vatican Observatory** (Specola Vaticana) is the Catholic Church's pontifical astronomical research institute, headquartered at Castel Gandolfo with a research telescope at Mount Graham, Arizona.

In late 2025 OSV News reporting (see [[src-catholic-filmmaker-vatican-ufo-2026-04]]), former director Br. [[guy-consolmagno]] articulated the observatory's standing policy of avoiding UFO topics in media to avoid association with charlatans — an institutional artifact of [[uap-stigma]]. The observatory declined all interviews for filmmaker [[sam-sorich]]'s in-progress Catholic UAP documentary.

# John Mack Institute (JMI)

A not-for-profit organization carrying forward the work of Harvard psychiatrist [[john-mack|John E. Mack]], focused on supporting people who have faced "unconventional, isolating, and anomalous experiences" (experiencers) and on legitimizing their academic study. JMI pairs **clinical/mental-health support** with **research infrastructure**.

Named figures: **Simon Bresler** (LCSW, Clinical Director), **[[kimberly-engels|Kimberly S. Engels]]** (Research Director), Andrea Oddo (Executive Director), Charles Holt (Communications Director; r/Experiencers co-founder). JMI co-sponsored the July 2026 [[src-breaking-the-taboo-uap-contact-symposium-2026-05|"Breaking the Taboo" symposium]] alongside Rice's [[center-for-the-impossible|Center For The Impossible]].

JMI's stated support model is a **"Middle Path"** that does not impose a single worldview on experiencers but fosters open, relational meaning-making, integrating digital peer communities with clinicians and researchers.

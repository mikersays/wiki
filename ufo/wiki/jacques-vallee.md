# Jacques Vallée

French-born computer scientist and astronomer; among the most influential post-Project-Blue-Book UFO researchers. Author of *Passport to Magonia* (1969), which catalogues parallels between modern UFO/abduction accounts and pre-modern folklore (fairy abductions, demonic visitations) — arguing the **phenomenon is older than the spaceship hypothesis** and may be best understood as a recurring interaction between human consciousness and a non-material substrate.

Originator of the **[[control-system-hypothesis|control-system hypothesis]]**: the UAP phenomenon behaves as a cybernetic feedback loop, presenting absurd stimuli, reading cultural response, and adjusting manifestations over time.

Vallée's personal papers are archived at [[jeffrey-kripal|Jeffrey Kripal]]'s **Archives of the Impossible** at Rice University.

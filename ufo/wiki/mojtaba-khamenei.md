# Mojtaba Khamenei

New Iranian Supreme Leader after the death of his father, Ali Khamenei, during the U.S. strike campaign of [[operation-epic-fury]]. As of late April 2026, Mojtaba Khamenei is reportedly in hiding and has sustained grave injuries; [[irgc-navy|IRGC]] commanders are effectively running operational decisions, including the diplomatic posture toward the stalled U.S.–Iran ceasefire process ([[src-epic-fury-update-2026-04]]).

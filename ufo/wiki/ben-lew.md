# Ben W.P. Lew

Astronomer affiliated with the Bay Area Environmental Research Institute and [[nasa-ames-research-center]]. Lead author of the 2026 *AJ* paper "JWST Spectral Retrieval of Cold Directly Imaged Planet WD 0806b and the First Measurement of Altitude-dependent K_zz in Exoplanet Atmospheres" (summarized in [[src-aas-nova-wd-0806b-atmosphere-2026-04]]). The paper provided the first observational evidence that bulk mixing weakens with altitude in an [[wd-0806b|exoplanet]] atmosphere — a constraint for [[exoplanet-atmosphere-models]].

**Romania** is a NATO ally on the alliance's eastern flank. In April 2026 the Romanian Minister of National Defense and the U.S. Secretary of the Army signed an agreement enabling Romania to procure [[counter-uas]] capabilities through the [[jiatf-401-marketplace]] — making Romania the second named partner nation in the marketplace after the [[united-kingdom]], and a step toward the Army secretary's stated goal of 25 partner nations by end of summer 2026 (see [[src-jiatf-401-marketplace-romania-2026-04]]).

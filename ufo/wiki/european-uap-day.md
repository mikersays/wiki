# European UAP Day

Annual March 20 anniversary observance commemorating the **March 20 2024 European Parliament meeting on "UAP in European Airspace"**. The third European UAP Day (March 20 2026) was marked by EuroUFO's annual recap of EU-level UAP developments ([[src-european-uap-day-third-2026-03]]), which surveyed the [[uap-coalition-netherlands]]'s MEP engagements ([[fabio-de-masi]], [[lukas-mandl]]), the 24→36-scientist ERA Act feedback on [[uap-stigma]], the EU Space Act consultation, the third [[sol-foundation|SOL]] Symposium in Baveno (Italy, October 2025), the December 2025 DG RTD consultation, and the launch of the Euro UFO Index.

## Why it matters

European UAP Day is the most concrete sustained handle on EU-level UAP advocacy. It complements the U.S.-centric thread anchored on AARO/PURSUE, the Canadian thread anchored on the [[sky-canada-project]], and the Japanese thread on the [[japan-uap-parliamentary-group]].

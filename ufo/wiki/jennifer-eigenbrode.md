# Jennifer Eigenbrode

Astrobiologist at [[nasa-goddard|NASA Goddard Space Flight Center]] and a co-lead of the [[sample-analysis-at-mars|SAM]] instrument-team aboard [[curiosity-rover]].

Co-author on the April 2026 *Nature Communications* paper (lead author [[amy-williams]]) reporting Curiosity's TMAH wet-chemistry detection of more than 20 [[organic-molecules-mars|organic molecules]] in [[gale-crater|Gale Crater]]'s Glen Torridon clays ([[src-curiosity-mars-life-molecules-2026-04]]).

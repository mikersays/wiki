# Harry Reid

Late U.S. Senator (D-NV); foundational figure in modern Pentagon UAP funding through his role securing FY2008–2012 appropriations for AAWSAP/AATIP. Cited by [[chuck-schumer]] as a personal mentor who cared deeply about UAP transparency, motivating Schumer's 2023 push for the [[uap-disclosure-act]] and [[uap-records-review-board]].

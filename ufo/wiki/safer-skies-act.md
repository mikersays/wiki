# Safer Skies Act

Provision passed via the FY2025 NDAA. Extends limited [[counter-uas|C-UAS]] authority to state, local, tribal, and territorial law enforcement. Predecessor to the [[counter-act|COUNTER Act]] and [[secure-our-skies-drone-safety-act]].

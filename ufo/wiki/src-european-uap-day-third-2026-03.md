## Summary

EuroUFO's annual recap by Italian researcher [[edoardo-russo]] marks the third anniversary of the March 20 2024 European Parliament meeting on "UAP in European Airspace" and surveys EU-level UAP developments through early 2026. Highlights include: a coordinated 11-language, 14-organization publication of Charles Maxence Layet's "European Strategic Autonomy and the Unidentified Aerospace Phenomena" essay (March 2025); [[uap-coalition-netherlands]]'s sustained pressure on EU institutions, including meetings with MEPs [[fabio-de-masi]] (April 2025) and [[lukas-mandl]] (September 2025); a 24-scientist (later 36) joint feedback to the European Commission on the European Research Area (ERA) Act framing [[uap-stigma]] as a systemic research barrier; a parallel feedback to the EU Space Act; the [[sol-foundation]]'s third SOL Symposium in Baveno, Italy (October 2025, 440 guests); a December 2025 DG RTD consultation meeting; the 2025 European UAP Barometer ([[philippe-ailleris]]: 33,600 reports across 40 countries, 2019–2024); and the launch of the Euro UFO Index uniting 23 national organizations and 4,000+ indexed 2024 reports. EuroUFO is a niche European-ufology advocacy site; coverage is sympathetic and self-reporting, so claims about MEP meetings and EC engagement should be read as advocacy framing rather than independent reporting.

## Key Claims

- March 20 2024 EU Parliament meeting on "UAP in European Airspace" baptized the first [[european-uap-day]]; this article marks the third anniversary.
- March 20 2025: Charles Maxence Layet's "European Strategic Autonomy and the Unidentified Aerospace Phenomena" published simultaneously in 11 languages on 14 national UFO-organization websites; relaunched by J. Allen Hynek Center for UFO Studies in the U.S.
- April 30 2025: [[uap-coalition-netherlands]] met MEP [[fabio-de-masi]] (Germany), who had filed a December 2024 EU Commission parliamentary question on UAP and member-state critical infrastructure; topics: stigma & mental health, flight safety, EU cooperation.
- September 24 2025: Same agenda discussed with MEP [[lukas-mandl]] (Austria).
- July 20 2025: [[edoardo-russo]] presented "New Perspectives in UAP Studies from the Old Continent" at MUFON's Cincinnati symposium, covering AR field-investigation tools, cognitive-psychology interview techniques, UFO photo-analysis software, automated IFO identification, machine-learning case classification, automatic sky-monitoring, mass digitization, specialized search engines, AI archival tools, and UFO chatbots.
- September 10 2025: 24-scientist (14-country) feedback on the EU European Research Area (ERA) Act, sponsored by UAP Coalition / UAP Check; framed [[uap-stigma]] as a systemic research barrier. Signatures grew to 36 by January 2026.
- September 26 2025: UAP Coalition feedback to the EU Space Act consultation: explicit UAP recognition, operator reporting obligation, synergy with existing monitoring, transparent central data collection.
- October 24-27 2025: Third [[sol-foundation|SOL]] Symposium — first held in Europe — in Baveno, Lake Maggiore, Italy; 20+ speakers, 440 guests; described as the main European UAP event of 2025.
- December 19 2025: UAP Coalition consultation meeting with the European Commission's DG RTD (Research and Innovation), with researchers from [[stockholm-university]] / [[nordic-institute-theoretical-physics]] orbits, on stigma in unconventional research.
- December 24 2025: [[philippe-ailleris]]'s annual European UAP Barometer for EuroUFO totals 33,600 reports across 40 European countries (2019–2024).
- December 25 2025: Launch of the Euro UFO Index by EuroUFO + UAP Check, 23 national + 2 international orgs sharing data; experimental phase covers 2024 only with 4,000+ indexed reports across 43 nations; planned extension back to 1947+.

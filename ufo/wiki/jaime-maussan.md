# Jaime Maussan

**Jaime Maussan** is a Mexican journalist and long-time UAP advocate. He is best known internationally for presenting purported "non-human bodies" to the Mexican Chamber of Deputies in September 2023 — a presentation that mainstream scientists and forensic specialists widely rejected (the bodies were assessed as composites of pre-Columbian human and animal remains).

## 2026 Buga Sphere

In May 2026, Maussan co-presented with [[steven-greer]] at the Disclosure Project's NPC event ([[src-greer-disclosure-event-npc-2026-05]]) a purported recovered object from Buga, Colombia (the **Buga Sphere**), claiming a preliminary resin-dating result of ~12,560 years. The release does not name the laboratory, chain of custody, or methodology. The wiki treats the claim as **unverified, single-source** pending independent analysis.

## Pattern

- Maussan's previous artifact presentations have followed a similar arc: dramatic public unveiling, no peer-reviewed corroboration, eventual mainstream rejection.

# RAF Fairford

Royal Air Force base in Gloucestershire, England, used as a U.S. Air Force strategic-bomber forward operating location (B-1, B-2, B-52). Operates under [[usafe-afa]].

Days after the November 2024 incursions over [[raf-lakenheath]], [[raf-mildenhall]], and [[raf-feltwell]], early-December reporting added Fairford to the list of UK bases with [[drone-incursions|UAP/sUAS incursions]] ([[src-uk-mod-uap-no-threat-2024]]). The pattern of strategic-bomber-base incursions parallels the March 2026 [[barksdale-afb]] waves.

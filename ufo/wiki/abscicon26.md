# AbSciCon26

The 2026 biennial **Astrobiology Science Conference** hosted by the [[american-geophysical-union|American Geophysical Union]] at Monona Terrace, Madison WI, **May 17–22 2026**. ~900 scientific contributions (posters, talks, town halls, plenaries) grouped under four umbrellas:

1. [[biosignatures|Biosignatures]]
2. Earth extremes
3. Evolution of life near and far
4. Exoplanets / habitable zones

# Greer "Disclosure Event" Press Conference at the National Press Club

## Summary

A PR Newswire release dated April 29, 2026, announcing a press conference hosted by [[steven-greer]] at the National Press Club on **May 8, 2026, 3:00–4:30 PM ET**, marketed as the **25th anniversary of Greer's May 9, 2001 NPC briefing**. Organized by [[the-disclosure-project]]. The event is calendared for the same day as the official Pentagon [[pursue]] launch ([[src-dow-uap-files-release-2026-05]], [[src-pursue-portal-launch-2026-05]]), positioning it as a competing/parallel narrative rather than a coordinated one.

> **Caveat:** All testimony and evidence below is presented as advance billing from the event's own promotional release. Greer-affiliated events have historically featured anonymous witnesses, low independent corroboration, and contested artifact provenance. Treat every claim on this page as **unverified, single-source**.

## Billed Speakers and Testimony (all unverified)

- **Anonymous U.S. Army Green Beret** — claims to have been taken to an underground facility in Indiana housing "non-human artifacts," and to have witnessed a man-made "Tic Tac" craft during a Middle East mission.
- **Anonymous U.S. Marine #1** — claims a 300-foot-diameter man-made UAP is being used in human-trafficking operations.
- **Anonymous U.S. Marine #2** — claims observation of a large triangular craft near 29 Palms, California.
- [[steven-greer]] — frames the program around covert unacknowledged special-access programs (USAPs) and alleged misuse of reverse-engineered tech.
- [[jaime-maussan]] (special guest) — co-presents the "Buga Sphere" analysis (purported recovered object from Buga, Colombia, with claimed preliminary finding that resin inside dates to ~12,560 years ago; methodology, chain of custody, and laboratory unnamed).

## Policy Recommendations

Greer says he will close with proposed Presidential executive actions and Congressional initiatives for UAP oversight, accountability, and transparency. The release does not enumerate specific bills or executive orders. See [[uap-disclosure]], [[uap-disclosure-act]], and [[uap-whistleblower-protection-act-2025]] for the legislative landscape Greer's asks would intersect.

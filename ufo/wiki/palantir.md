**Palantir Technologies** is a U.S. defense and enterprise software firm. In August 2025 the Army awarded Palantir an enterprise IDIQ vehicle with a **$10B ceiling** — the closest precedent for the March 2026 [[anduril]] $20B award and an anchor data point for the [[army-enterprise-contracts]] consolidation strategy (see [[src-anduril-army-20b-counter-drone-idiq-2026-03]]).

The **SETI Institute** is a non-profit astrobiology and [[seti|Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence]] research organization based in Mountain View, California. It is institutionally distinct from the broader [[seti]] concept but runs or partners on many of the major technosignature programs, including [[breakthrough-listen]].

The Institute publishes a news channel that covers its scientists' work and related exoplanet/astrobiology results — source of [[src-alpha-centauri-a-exoplanet-2026]] (Franck Marchis interviewing STScI's Julien Girard on the JWST Alpha Centauri A candidate).

SETI Institute also conceived and advocates for [[project-blue]], a dedicated telescope to directly image planets around [[alpha-centauri]] A and B.

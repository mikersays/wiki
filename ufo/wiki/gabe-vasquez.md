# Gabe Vasquez

U.S. Representative (D-NM, southern New Mexico). Member of the [[house-armed-services-committee]]. Sponsored the [[counter-act|COUNTER Act]] (included in the 2026 NDAA) and introduced the bipartisan [[secure-our-skies-drone-safety-act|Secure Our Skies Drone Safety Act of 2025]]. Drove the legislation from concerns about [[drone-incursions]] at [[white-sands-missile-range]] and grilled DoD over the February 2026 [[el-paso-international-airport]] and [[fort-hancock]] incidents.

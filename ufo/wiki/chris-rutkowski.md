# Chris Rutkowski

Canadian UAP researcher and author; archived [[transport-canada]] UAP reports 1999–2022 after Canadian federal agencies (RCMP, NRC, Transport Canada) progressively exited public UAP-report intake. Listed as a consultant in the [[sky-canada-project]] preliminary report.

Per [[src-canada-sky-canada-project-2025-02]], Transport Canada ceased forwarding UAP reports to Rutkowski in 2021 due to a surge in ATIP (Access to Information and Privacy) requests after U.S. political attention spiked.

# Chris Rutkowski

Canadian UAP researcher and author; archived [[transport-canada]] UAP reports 1999–2022 after Canadian federal agencies ([[royal-canadian-mounted-police|RCMP]], [[national-research-council-canada|NRC]], Transport Canada) progressively exited public UAP-report intake. Listed as a consultant in both the [[sky-canada-project]] preliminary and final reports.

Per [[src-canada-sky-canada-project-2025-02]], Transport Canada ceased forwarding UAP reports to Rutkowski in 2021 due to a surge in ATIP (Access to Information and Privacy) requests after U.S. political attention spiked.

In the final-report coverage ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]], CTV News): "UAP reports are deserving of scientific study in that they represent a real phenomenon that has been witnessed by reliable and responsible observers."

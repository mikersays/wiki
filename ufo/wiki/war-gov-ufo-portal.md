# war.gov/UFO Portal

The [[department-of-war|Department of War]]'s public-facing UAP files portal launched **May 8, 2026** as the operational instrument of the [[src-pursue-portal-launch-2026-05|PURSUE Release 01]]; mirrored at pentagonufofiles.io by [[enigma-labs]]. Hosts video files, image carousels, FBI PDFs, and interagency records under a "make up your own minds" framing.

## Catalog conventions and caveats

- **January-1 "date taken" filler** — many video records list "01 January" of a given year, which Gizmodo's [[matt-novak]] reads as a year-only-precision filler convention rather than a literal capture date ([[src-gizmodo-find-ufos-pentagon-videos-2026-05]]).
- **DOW-UAP-PR-** prefixes identify individual video records (e.g. [[dow-uap-pr38]] for the 2013 eight-pointed-star clip; PR47 for the 2023 INDOPACOM IR formation near Japan).

# Noam Izenberg

[[jhuapl|Johns Hopkins APL]] planetary scientist; lead developer of the [[venus-life-equation]] (Izenberg et al. 2021), a [[drake-equation]]-style decomposition L = O × R × C used to estimate the likelihood of extant life on [[venus]]. The framework was reused in the 2026 LPSC Earth-to-Venus [[panspermia]] modeling study reported in [[src-venus-panspermia-earth-origin-2026-04]].

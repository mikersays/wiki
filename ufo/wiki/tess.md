# Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite (TESS)

[[nasa|NASA]] all-sky transit survey launched in 2018; identifies exoplanet candidates via brightness dips, often passed to JWST or ground-based facilities for atmospheric follow-up.

## Vault-relevant results

- **2023** — flagged [[toi-5205-b]] (Jupiter-sized giant around an 0.4 M☉ [[m-dwarf-stars|M dwarf]]); follow-up confirmation led by [[shubham-kanodia]]. Cited in [[src-sciencedaily-forbidden-exoplanet-atmosphere-2026-04]].
- Discovery instrument for several other vault exoplanet entities including [[toi-1130b]].

## Technosignature use

- 72 TESS targets supplied the stellar-target set for the [[sardinia-radio-telescope|Sardinia Radio Telescope]] high-frequency (6/18 GHz) technosignature survey ([[src-sardinia-technosignature-search-2026-05]]).

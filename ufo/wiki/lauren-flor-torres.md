# Lauren Flor Torres

**Lauren Flor Torres** is a Colombian astrophysicist at the **Instituto de Física, Universidad de Antioquia** (Medellín). A Universidad del Valle alumna, she works on statistical analysis of exoplanet atmospheres and is a named scientific critic of the [[buga-sphere]] in Colombian press.

## Position on the Buga Sphere

Per [[src-buga-sphere-el-pais-cali-maussan-handoff-2025-10]] and [[src-buga-sphere-infobae-true-origin-2025-10]]:

- Real meteorites entering Earth's atmosphere show **heat-fractured crusts** from atmospheric friction; the Buga Sphere's pristine ("luce impecable") surface is **inconsistent with a space origin**.
- Cites ~6,000 confirmed exoplanets but no detected extraterrestrial intelligence.
- Endorses caution while keeping ET probability "not zero."

> "How does a sphere supposedly coming from space not show [atmospheric entry] features and instead look pristine?" — Lauren Flor Torres (translated)

**CISA (Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency)** is the DHS agency that manages the federal **.gov domain registry** and oversees civilian-government cybersecurity.

Per [[src-alien-gov-domains-2026-03]], CISA verifies .gov registrants but does **not** audit or control how agencies use their registered domains — a distinction relevant to the unexplained March 2026 registrations of **alien.gov** and **aliens.gov** (see [[alien-gov-portal]]).

Acting EAD for Cybersecurity Chris Butera spoke on the record for the agency.

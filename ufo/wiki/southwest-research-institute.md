**Southwest Research Institute** (SwRI) is a San Antonio-based independent R&D organization. It is the home institution of [[scott-bolton]], principal investigator of the [[juno]] mission.

SwRI also leads the **Ultraviolet Spectrograph (UVS)** instruments aboard both ESA's [[juice|JUICE]] and NASA's [[europa-clipper|Europa Clipper]] (PI [[kurt-retherford]]). In November 2025 these twin instruments made the first simultaneous two-direction views of interstellar comet [[3i-atlas]]'s coma ([[src-3i-atlas-dual-spacecraft-imaging-2026-05]]).

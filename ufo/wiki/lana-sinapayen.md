**Lana Sinapayen** is a researcher at the [[earth-life-science-institute]] (ELSI), Institute of Science Tokyo. She co-developed (with [[harrison-b-smith]]) the [[agnostic-biosignature|agnostic population-statistics biosignature detection method]], published April 2026. The method searches for life across exoplanet populations via statistical correlations arising from [[panspermia]]-driven spread and environmental modification, without requiring any specific biosignature chemistry. See [[src-panspermia-agnostic-biosignature-2026-04]].

## Key Quote

> "Even if life elsewhere is fundamentally different from life on Earth, its large-scale effects may still leave detectable traces."

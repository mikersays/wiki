# Kurt Retherford

Dr. **Kurt Retherford** is a [[southwest-research-institute|Southwest Research Institute]] scientist and principal investigator of the Ultraviolet Spectrograph (UVS) instruments aboard ESA's [[juice|JUICE]] and NASA's [[europa-clipper|Europa Clipper]] missions.

In November 2025 he led the coordinated dual-spacecraft UVS observation of interstellar comet [[3i-atlas]], obtaining the first simultaneous two-direction views of a comet's coma and detecting hydrogen, oxygen, and carbon emissions extending ~5 million km from the nucleus ([[src-3i-atlas-dual-spacecraft-imaging-2026-05]]).

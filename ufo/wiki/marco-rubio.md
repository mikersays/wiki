# Marco Rubio

U.S. Senator (R-FL); has held positions on the Senate Intelligence Committee with public UAP advocacy. As of 2025-2026 also serving as U.S. Secretary of State under [[donald-trump]].

## UAP positions

- On-camera in the 2025 documentary [[the-age-of-disclosure]] discussing UAPs ([[src-age-of-disclosure-review-2025-03]]).
- Quoted on [[nuclear-uap-correlation]]: "We've had repeated instances of something operating in the airspace over restricted nuclear facilities."

## 2025-2026 — SecState and Acting NSA

Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Rubio is now Trump's Secretary of State and acting National Security Adviser. Although a [[uap-disclosure-act]] co-sponsor as senator, he has been inactive on UAP transparency legislation in his current role. Rep. [[tim-burchett]] is reportedly scheduled to meet with Rubio to raise UAP issues directly.

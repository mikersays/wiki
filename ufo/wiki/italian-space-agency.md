# Italian Space Agency (ASI)

Italy's national space agency (Agenzia Spaziale Italiana). Partner on multiple [[nasa]] and [[esa]] missions. Notable astrobiology-relevant contribution: built the high-gain antenna for the [[cassini-huygens|Cassini]] orbiter, which (alongside [[esa]]'s [[huygens-probe]]) made the program a treaty-level commitment that survived four U.S. administrations of budget pressure.

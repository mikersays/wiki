# Ryan Graves

**Ryan Graves** is a former U.S. Navy F/A-18 pilot who has become one of the most prominent UAP advocates. He chairs the [[aiaa]] Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena Integration Committee and founded [[americans-for-safe-aerospace]].

## Key Positions

- Frames UAP as a **national security and airspace safety** issue rather than an extraterrestrial question, a strategy that has generated bipartisan political support
- Emphasizes the credibility of observers: "Highly credible people and professional observers are seeing objects" with unexplained capabilities
- Advocates for improved [[uap-reporting-infrastructure]] to reduce [[uap-stigma]] among military and commercial pilots

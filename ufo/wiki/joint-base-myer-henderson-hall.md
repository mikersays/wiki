**Joint Base Myer-Henderson Hall** is a joint Army/Marine Corps installation in the Arlington, Virginia / DC area, within the [[bumblebee-counter-uas|National Capital Region]] counter-drone footprint.

## Activity in this vault

- **2026-04-16** — Per Army Secretary [[dan-driscoll]]'s House Appropriations testimony, the base has hosted **350 state and local police departments** for [[jiatf-401|JIATF-401]] counter-UAS familiarization, intended to sync civilian law enforcement into a common "sight picture" with the military marketplace (see [[src-army-counter-drone-marketplace-stateside-2026-04]]).

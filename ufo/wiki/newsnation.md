# NewsNation

**NewsNation** is a US cable news network operating since 2021. Within this vault it is a recurring venue for UAP coverage, primarily through investigative journalist [[ross-coulthart]]'s *Reality Check* podcast and the *CUOMO* program, which has hosted [[lue-elizondo]] and [[anna-paulina-luna]] for UAP-related interviews.

**David Fravor** is a retired U.S. Navy Commander and fighter pilot best known for his role in the 2004 [[uss-nimitz-uap-incident]], where his team encountered a "tic-tac-shaped" object off the coast of Southern California.

## USS Nimitz Encounter

Fravor was the commanding officer of VFA-41 (Black Aces) when, on November 14, 2004, he and Lt. Cmdr. Alex Dietrich observed an anomalous object during a training exercise. The object demonstrated flight characteristics that defied conventional aerodynamics — rapid acceleration, no visible propulsion, and transmedium capability.

## Public Disclosure

Fravor's account gained mainstream attention after the 2017 *New York Times* coverage of government UAP programs and his subsequent interview on Lex Fridman's podcast (2020). According to [[src-uap-reddit-disclosure-conspiracy-2026-05]], his disclosure is described as a milestone in a "monumental looming UFO scandal" (Lomas et al. 2025). Unlike many UAP witnesses, Fravor states he has been "believed and relatively unharmed" by his account.

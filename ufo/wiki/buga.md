# Buga

**Buga** (officially Guadalajara de Buga, locally known as "Ciudad Señora") is a city in the **Valle del Cauca** department of Colombia. It is the alleged March 2025 discovery site of the [[buga-sphere]], in the **Alto Bonito sector**.

## UAP relevance

- March 2025: [[jose-arias-restrepo|José Arias Restrepo]] reports finding the [[buga-sphere|Buga Sphere]] in Alto Bonito while metal-detecting; the case ignites local UAP panic and global attention through [[jaime-maussan]]'s amplification.
- [[oscar-amaya-montoya]]'s **Fundación Casa de la Ciencia de Buga** runs community-facing science-education outreach in response to the case.

The sphere narrative is centered on Buga but the parallel [[valdias-sphere|Valdías Sphere]] account ([[src-buga-sphere-maussan-tv-valdias-report-2026-05]]) places its recovery in the Antioquia / Medellín region, not Buga itself.

## Summary

The Washington Times reports that a bipartisan group of lawmakers and whistleblower [[david-grusch]] will hold a Capitol Hill press conference on **June 9, 2026** to press the federal government for full UAP disclosure. Grusch is expected to elaborate on [[non-human-intelligence]] evidence he encountered in classified channels and argue it should be publicly released, framing the event as moving "from testimony to action." Participants include Reps. [[eric-burlison]], [[anna-paulina-luna]], [[tim-burchett]], and Democrat [[jared-moskowitz]]; journalist [[leslie-kean]] and filmmaker [[james-fox]] will host. The members plan to rally around passage of the [[uap-disclosure-act]]. The piece situates the event against the [[pursue|PURSUE]] rolling document releases, noting a second tranche came the prior week, with officials cautioning that "unidentified" does not mean "alien."

## Key Claims

- A Capitol Hill press conference is scheduled for **June 9, 2026** featuring Grusch (event organizers).
- Grusch will elaborate on UAP and non-human-intelligence evidence from classified channels and believes it should be released (Grusch).
- The event aims to "move from testimony to action" and let the public "judge the facts" (Grusch).
- Attendees: Reps. Burlison (R-MO), Luna (R-FL), Burchett (R-TN), and Moskowitz (D-FL); Burchett co-chairs the [[us-congress-uap-caucus|House UAP Caucus]] with Moskowitz; Luna chairs the [[task-force-declassification-federal-secrets|Task Force on Declassification of Federal Secrets]] (article).
- Lawmakers will rally around passage of the [[uap-disclosure-act]], which would require the president to direct agencies to declassify UAP records and publish them (article).
- Investigative journalist [[leslie-kean]] and filmmaker [[james-fox]] will host the event (article).
- The Trump administration has been releasing declassified UAP material via [[pursue|PURSUE]]; a second tranche came "last week" (article).
- Federal officials stress "unidentified" does not mean "alien" (officials).

# Apollo 11

**Apollo 11** was NASA's first crewed lunar landing (July 1969), with crew Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin, and Michael Collins.

## UAP Relevance

The **Apollo 11 Technical Crew Debriefing (1969)** — record `NASA-UAP-D4` — was included in [[pursue]] Release 01 (May 8, 2026) alongside debriefings from Apollo 12, 17, and Skylab. It is the first appearance in this wiki of Apollo 11 as part of an official U.S. government UAP record set ([[src-pursue-portal-launch-2026-05]], [[pursue-release-01-catalog]]).

The specific UAP-relevant content of the debriefing has not yet been catalogued in this vault — pending text extraction from the released PDF in `ufo/raw/assets/pursue-release-01/`. The record's existence is confirmed via the PURSUE manifest CSV.

See [[lunar-uap-historical-claims]] for the umbrella concept (competing IVA-debris vs. anomalous interpretations of crew transcripts).

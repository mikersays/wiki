# Eric Schmidt

Former Google CEO and founder of the defense startup that became [[perennial-autonomy|Perennial Autonomy]], maker of the [[merops-interceptor|Merops]] counter-drone interceptor.

Per [[src-dronexl-perennial-interceptor-doctrine-2026-05]], Schmidt launched the venture quietly as **White Stork** in 2023 — after 2022 meetings with Ukrainian officials — to build a low-cost interceptor for Ukrainian crews. It was renamed **Project Eagle** in February 2024 and **Perennial Autonomy** in 2026. Schmidt recruited former Pentagon innovation chief [[will-roper|Will Roper]] plus Apple and SpaceX engineers.

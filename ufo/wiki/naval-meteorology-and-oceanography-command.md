# Naval Meteorology and Oceanography Command

U.S. Navy command for fleet meteorology and oceanography. [[tim-gallaudet]] served as Commander while concurrently functioning as the Oceanographer of the Navy. The command was a recipient of the January 2015 [[us-fleet-forces-command]] "URGENT SAFETY OF FLIGHT ISSUE" UAP email ([[src-gallaudet-house-testimony-2024-11]]).

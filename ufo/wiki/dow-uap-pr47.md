**DOW-UAP-PR47** is the [[pursue|PURSUE]] Release 01 catalog identifier for a 2023 [[us-indo-pacific-command|USINDOPACOM]] infrared video classified as an "Unresolved UAP Report" with the location field "Japan" ([[pursue-release-01-catalog]]). [[src-japan-pentagon-uap-trove-reaction-2026-05|IBTimes UK]] reports the clip "appears to show several contrasting objects maintaining formation during a military operation."

[[japan|Japan]]'s Chief Cabinet Secretary [[minoru-kihara]] confirmed on May 13, 2026 that Tokyo is reviewing this material alongside U.S. partners. PR47 is part of the same DOW-UAP-PR4x INDOPACOM cluster as PR46 (2024, East China Sea) and PR48 (2024, candidate offshore wind turbines per [[src-metabunk-pursue-analysis-2026-05]]). Skeptical analysis from [[joseph-trevithick]] argues the visually adjacent "football-shaped" 2024 object is consistent with a Chinese surveillance balloon ([[src-twz-pursue-shrug-2026-05]]).

# Enigma Labs

**Enigma Labs** is a data and analytics company that operates a public UAP reporting and analysis platform. As of mid-2026, the platform holds more than **200,000 civilian UAP sightings**, exposed through a free app and website with map visualizations clustering reports by location and time.

## Role in PURSUE-era disclosure

- Per [[src-leonard-david-pursue-column-2026-05]], Enigma Labs **mirrored** the May 8, 2026 [[pursue]] Release 01 at `pentagonufofiles.io` shortly after the war.gov/ufo launch.
- Per [[src-defensescoop-pursue-not-disclosure-2026-05]], Enigma consultant **Rojas** is **cross-referencing PURSUE cases against Enigma's >200,000-sighting database** to surface location/time clusters that would be invisible if the official files remained in a classified vault, and is folding PURSUE items into the public app's maps.

## Significance

Enigma is one of the larger pieces of civilian [[uap-reporting-infrastructure]] outside of the formal government channel ([[aaro]] / war.gov/ufo). Where [[americans-for-safe-aerospace]] focuses on pilot/aircrew reporting and aviation-safety records, Enigma occupies the public-sightings/data-analytics niche.

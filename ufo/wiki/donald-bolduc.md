# Donald Bolduc

Retired U.S. Army Brigadier General and Special Forces officer.

In April 2026, Bolduc published commentary arguing that [[operation-epic-fury]] achieved tactical and operational gains but exposed strategic gaps and lacked a defined exit strategy ([[src-epic-fury-update-2026-04]]). One of the few named retired-general critics of the Trump-administration Iran campaign as of late April 2026.

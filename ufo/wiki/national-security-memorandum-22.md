# National Security Memorandum 22 (NSM-22)

White House memorandum defining the 16 sectors of U.S. **critical infrastructure** that the federal government treats as protection priorities (energy, water, transportation, communications, financial services, healthcare, food and agriculture, etc.).

## Relevance to UAP / counter-UAS vault

- Provides the **eligibility scaffold** for the FAA's proposed [[unmanned-aircraft-flight-restriction|UAFR]] rule (May 2026, [[src-faa-drone-rule-critical-infrastructure-2026-05]]): only facilities within an NSM-22 sector qualify for a Standard UAFR.
- Ties FAA airspace policy directly to broader homeland critical-infrastructure protection — meaning the same memo that frames cyber, physical security, and supply-chain protection now anchors a slice of national-airspace regulation.

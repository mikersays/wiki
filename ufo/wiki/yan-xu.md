# Yan Xu

Lead author of the April 2026 *Nature Communications* paper analyzing 2004-2010 [[cassini-huygens|Cassini]] magnetometer data to characterize the structure of [[saturn]]'s magnetic cusp. The paper found the cusp sits in the afternoon sector — produced by Saturn's rapid rotation combined with [[enceladus]]-supplied plasma — extending the rotation-dominated [[saturn-magnetosphere|magnetospheric regime]] previously observed at Jupiter. UCL / Lancaster University collaboration with co-author [[andrew-coates]]. ([[src-cassini-huygens-ocean-worlds-2026-04]])

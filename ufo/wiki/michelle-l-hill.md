**Michelle L. Hill** — planetary scientist at [[uc-riverside|UC Riverside]]. Lead author of the 2026 [[stehm-model|Smaller Than Earth Habitability Model (STEHM)]] arXiv preprint, which couples Jeans/XUV atmospheric escape with stagnant-lid interior cooling to set a ~0.8 R⊕ lower size limit for habitable-zone exoplanets.

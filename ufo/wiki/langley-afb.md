**Langley Air Force Base** is a U.S. Air Force installation in Hampton, Virginia, home to dozens of F-22 Raptor stealth fighters. Langley became the public flashpoint for the modern homeland C-sUAS problem after a 17-night [[drone-incursions|drone swarm]] beginning Dec. 14, 2023 — F-22s were relocated to a nearby base during the incursions. The episode was first reported by [[the-war-zone]] and later dissected on national television by CBS [[src-60-minutes-drone-swarms-2025|60 Minutes]], with retired NORAD/NORTHCOM commander [[glen-vanherck]] and retired Gen. Mark Kelly describing drone sizes from commercial quadcopters up to "a bass boat or a small car." Gen. [[gregory-guillot]] cited Langley as the centerpiece of his 90-day NORTHCOM assessment.

The identities of the Langley drone operators remain publicly unknown. The Biden White House framed the swarm as "likely hobbyists"; VanHerck publicly rejects that assessment and warns of a "foreign nexus."

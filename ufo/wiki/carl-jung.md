# Carl Jung

Swiss psychiatrist (1875–1961); founder of analytical psychology. Author of *Flying Saucers: A Modern Myth of Things Seen in the Skies* (1958), in which discs are read as **mandala** projections of the Self compensating for collective psychic dissociation in the atomic age. Carl Sagan publicly acknowledged Jung's psychological lens as "compelling in its explanatory power" at the 1968 UFO Symposium — a fact frequently elided in later popular UFO discourse.

Jung's framing is the philosophical taproot of the contemporary [[psychological-uap-framing|psychological-phenomenon]] school (Vallée → Kripal → Strieber), which treats UAP encounters as primarily about the human mind rather than non-human hardware.

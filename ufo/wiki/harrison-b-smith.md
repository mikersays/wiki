**Harrison B. Smith** is a researcher at the [[earth-life-science-institute]] (ELSI), Institute of Science Tokyo. He co-developed (with [[lana-sinapayen]]) the [[agnostic-biosignature|agnostic population-statistics biosignature detection method]] based on [[panspermia]] and environmental modification assumptions, published April 2026. The method detects life across exoplanet populations via statistical correlations without requiring knowledge of life's specific chemistry. See [[src-panspermia-agnostic-biosignature-2026-04]].

## Key Quote

> "By focusing on how life spreads and interacts with environments, we can search for it without needing a perfect definition or a single definitive signal."

**Aundre Bumgardner** is a Connecticut state representative (D-Groton) who co-sponsored **H.B. 5422**, the state UAP study bill introduced by [[joe-hoxha]]. He represents a submarine-building district and frames UAP study as a national-security concern ([[src-connecticut-uap-disclosure-bill-2026]]).

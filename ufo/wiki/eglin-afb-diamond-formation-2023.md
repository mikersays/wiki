The **Eglin AFB Diamond-Formation UAP** incident of January 26, 2023 involved a military pilot observing four objects in a diamond formation over the Gulf of Mexico while experiencing simultaneous aircraft system malfunctions. The video, designated "IIR 1 665 SO301 23/Eglin AFB," is among the up to 46 [[aaro]]-held UAP clips previewed for [[pursue]] Release 02.

AARO's published case-resolution report concluded the formation was "very likely an ordinary object" — possibly a lighting balloon — with only "moderate confidence." [[mick-west]], speaking to The Debrief, said AARO's [[lighting-balloon-hypothesis|lighting-balloon hypothesis]] "always felt like something someone at AARO liked, but wasn't really supported by much evidence."

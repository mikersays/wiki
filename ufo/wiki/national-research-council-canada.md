# National Research Council Canada (NRC)

Canada's federal research and technology agency. From **1967 to 1995** the NRC operated the Canadian government's UAP-report intake function. The resulting archive — ~15,000 pages, of which ~9,500 have been digitized — is now held by [[library-and-archives-canada]] ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]]).

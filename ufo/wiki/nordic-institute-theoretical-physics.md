The **Nordic Institute for Theoretical Physics** (NORDITA) is a research institute in Stockholm jointly hosted by [[stockholm-university]] and KTH Royal Institute of Technology. It has actively published peer-reviewed UAP research since 2017 (see [[src-academic-uap-stigma-2026-04]]) — a leading European counterpoint to U.S. academic UAP stigma.

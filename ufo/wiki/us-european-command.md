# US European Command

**US European Command (EUCOM)** is one of eleven unified combatant commands of the US Department of Defense, responsible for military operations across Europe and parts of the Atlantic, the Caucasus, and the Arctic. First appearance in this vault is via the [[pursue]] Release 01 video corpus.

## UAP context

- **2026-05-08** — [[src-stripes-pursue-uap-files-2026-05]] identifies EUCOM as one of three combatant commands ([[us-indo-pacific-command]], [[centcom]], EUCOM) that contributed videos described as "**unresolved**" to [[pursue]] Release 01. Stripes is the only launch-day wire to enumerate all three commands by name. The specific EUCOM submissions in Release 01 are not individually identified in any wire to date.

**Dr. Simon Steel** is the Deputy Director of the [[carl-sagan-institute|Carl Sagan Center]] at the [[seti-institute|SETI Institute]]. He co-presented a *SETI Live* segment with [[sofia-sheikh]] discussing her "Earth Detecting Earth" study on the detectability of Earth's own [[technosignatures]] at interstellar distances ([[src-seti-detecting-earth-technosignatures-2026-05]]).

**Matt Gaetz** is a former Republican U.S. Representative from Florida (2017–2024). In 2026 reporting cited by *Christian Post* (see [[src-perry-stone-pastor-briefing-2026-05]]), Gaetz claimed an Army official briefed him on alleged human-extraterrestrial "breeding programs." [[tim-burchett]] reportedly implied Gaetz was telling the truth about the briefing.

The claim is unverified by federal authorities and remains a fringe element of the broader [[uap-disclosure]] discourse.

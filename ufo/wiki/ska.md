# Square Kilometre Array (SKA)

Next-generation international radio telescope array, with stations under construction in South Africa (SKA-Mid) and Australia (SKA-Low). Will be the world's largest and most sensitive radio observatory once complete; supports astrophysics, cosmology, and [[seti|SETI]].

## Relevance to UAP / astrobiology vault

- **Primary BRaTs platform.** [[michael-a-garrett]]'s May 2026 BRaTs framework ([[src-brats-radio-technosignatures-2026-05]]) makes SKA (and its precursors) the central wide-field instrument for detecting **broadband** radio [[technosignatures]] — leakage from distributed digital infrastructure rather than narrowband beacons.
- Provides the sensitivity and field of view needed to enable "SETI Deep Fields" — long integrations on the same patch of sky to push the [[kardashev-scale|Kardashev Type I]] leakage detection volume to ~100 pc.
- SKA-flagged broadband candidates are intended to be followed up at sub-arcsecond resolution by [[vlbi|VLBI]] to confirm planetary-scale origin via astrometric co-motion with nearby stars.

# Los Alamos

Los Alamos, New Mexico — site of **Los Alamos National Laboratory**, the Manhattan Project and ongoing U.S. nuclear-weapons R&D center.

**UAP relevance:** [[pursue|PURSUE]] Release 02 includes a 1970 handwritten letter from a Los Alamos scientist reporting green lights regularly seen in the mountains between 9 and 11 p.m. during 1948–1951, once "moving in some kind of formation" ([[src-nbc-pursue-firsthand-encounters-2026-05]]). The account overlaps the broader 1948–1951 New Mexico **green-fireball** wave and the nearby [[sandia-new-mexico|Sandia, New Mexico]] 1948–1950 sighting cluster, reinforcing the recurring nuclear-site / UAP correlation theme.

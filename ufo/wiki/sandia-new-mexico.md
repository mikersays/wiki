**Sandia, New Mexico** is the area in central New Mexico encompassing Sandia National Laboratories, Kirtland Air Force Base, and the broader U.S. nuclear-weapons enterprise hub.

Per the May 22, 2026 [[pursue]] Release 02 ([[src-pentagon-second-batch-ufo-release-2026-05]] and corroborated by [[src-newsweek-pursue-release-02-2026-05]]), newly declassified files document UAP sightings at Sandia from 1948 to 1950:

- **116-page file** (Newsweek inventory).
- **209 reports** of "green orbs," "discs," and "fireballs" by the U.S. Air Force and the [[armed-forces-special-weapons-project]].
- Accounts of objects "maneuvering, vanishing or exploding."
- Investigations into **residual copper powder** found at several sighting sites.
- Material fed into [[project-grudge|Project Grudge]], the short-lived 1949 USAF UFO program (precursor to Project Blue Book).

This is one of the densest documented early UAP–nuclear-facility clusters on record. The Sandia cluster reinforces the nuclear-equities framing [[lue-elizondo]] emphasized in his November 2024 House Oversight testimony ([[src-elizondo-house-hearing-2024-11]]).

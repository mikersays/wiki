**Jeremy Corbell** is an investigative filmmaker and journalist focused on UAP. He partners with veteran reporter [[george-knapp]] and has been a key conduit of U.S. military UAP video evidence to the public.

## Role in the 2026 disclosure push

Per [[src-war-department-uap-release-2026-04]], Corbell and Knapp provided Congress with file names, locations, and additional USAF-filmed UAP assets not publicly listed — feeding the 46-video request that Rep. [[anna-paulina-luna]] put to Secretary of War [[pete-hegseth]].

Corbell publicly positions his **Sleeping Dog** effort as an independent-journalism backstop if official [[uap-disclosure]] stalls.

## WEAPONIZED podcast (December 2025)

Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Corbell and [[george-knapp]] hosted Reps. [[eric-burlison]] and [[tim-burchett]] on the WEAPONIZED podcast in late 2025; the episode is the source for Burlison's Epstein-list-style subpoena proposal and Burchett's tunnel "shadowy figure" anecdote. Corbell publicly positions his **Sleeping Dog** effort as an independent-journalism backstop if official [[uap-disclosure]] stalls.

## Prior leaks resurfaced in PURSUE Release 01

Per [[mick-west]]'s May 2026 Metabunk thread ([[src-metabunk-pursue-analysis-2026-05]]), **PURSUE Release 01 record PR-19** is the same "**Baghdad Phantom**" video Corbell already published unredacted in 2023 — i.e., the PURSUE inclusion adds nothing new beyond official sanction for that case. The observation is part of a broader skeptical-community thread arguing that significant fractions of [[pursue]] Release 01 are recycled previously-public material.

# Royal Air Force (RAF)

UK air service. Runs the [[quick-reaction-alert]] (QRA) force from RAF Coningsby and RAF Lossiemouth. Hosts [[usafe-afa]] tenants on its base estate, including [[raf-lakenheath]], [[raf-mildenhall]], [[raf-feltwell]], and [[raf-fairford]].

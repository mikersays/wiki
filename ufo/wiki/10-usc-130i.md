**10 U.S.C. § 130i** is the U.S. statutory authority under which the Department of Defense / [[department-of-war]] detects, tracks, identifies, and mitigates UAS threats to **"covered facilities"** — military installations and certain related sites. It is the legal basis for the December 8, 2025 SecWar-signed homeland counter-UAS guidance issued via [[jiatf-401]] ([[src-jiatf-401-counter-drone-homeland-guidance-2026-01]]).

10 USC 130i runs in parallel with **6 U.S.C. § 124n** (the corresponding DHS authority). The legislative track to expand the "covered facility" definition runs through the COUNTER Act and the Safer Skies / Secure Our Skies Drone Safety bills ([[src-counter-act-secure-skies-bills-2026-04]]).

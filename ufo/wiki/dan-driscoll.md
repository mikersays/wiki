**Dan Driscoll** is the U.S. **Secretary of the Army** (in office as of April 2026). He is the senior civilian principal for the Army's counter-drone procurement posture, including the [[jiatf-401-marketplace]] expansion to stateside high-profile events and to state/local/federal law enforcement.

## Activity in this vault

- **2026-04-16** — Told the House Defense Appropriations subcommittee that JIATF-401 has booked ~$13M in marketplace sales and is leaning on the marketplace to secure the FIFA World Cup 2026, America 250, and the 2028 LA Summer Olympics. Confirmed state, local, and federal law enforcement can purchase from the marketplace; cited 350 state/local police departments synced at [[joint-base-myer-henderson-hall]] (see [[src-army-counter-drone-marketplace-stateside-2026-04]]).
- Disclosed that the Army bought **13,000 [[merops-interceptor|Merops interceptors]]** at ~$15k/unit in the opening days of [[operation-epic-fury]] to defeat [[shahed-drone|Shaheds]].
- Named signatory on the Romania marketplace agreement (see [[src-jiatf-401-marketplace-romania-2026-04]]).

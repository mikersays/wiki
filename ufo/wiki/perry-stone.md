**Perry Stone** is a Tennessee-based Pentecostal evangelist, founder of Perry Stone Ministries, and a dispensational premillennialist (pre-tribulation rapture, Islamic Antichrist, third-temple eschatology). His YouTube channel has roughly 925,000 subscribers as of 2026.

In an April 27, 2026 video, Stone claimed an unnamed pastor friend told him a group of pastors had been quietly briefed by U.S. government officials in "a certain state" about imminent UFO disclosure (see [[src-perry-stone-pastor-briefing-2026-05]]). The claim is single-source and unverified — Stone declines to name the friend, the location, the officials, or the date.

Stone interprets the alleged disclosure within a [[theological-uap-framing|theological frame]], speculating that government may use an "alien invasion" narrative to explain a future rapture and that some of the entities described could be demonic or fallen-angel manifestations from Genesis 6.

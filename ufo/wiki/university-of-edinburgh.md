The **University of Edinburgh** is a UK research university whose astronomy and astrobiology groups partner with Cornell's [[carl-sagan-institute]] on exoplanet biosignature work. Beth Biller (direct-imaging / exoplanet atmospheres) and Sean McMahon (astrobiology / geobiology) co-authored the 2026 mineral-false-positives paper with first author Mia Belle Parkinson and [[lisa-kaltenegger]] ([[src-mineral-false-positives-biosignatures-2026-05]]).

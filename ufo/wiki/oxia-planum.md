**Oxia Planum** is the confirmed landing site for [[esa]]'s [[rosalind-franklin-rover]] mission on Mars. Located in a clay-rich terrain indicating ancient water, the site was selected for subsurface life-detection drilling using the rover's 2-meter drill and MOMA (Mars Organic Molecule Analyzer) instrument.

The [[src-nasa-rosalind-franklin-implementation-2026-04|NASA ROSA implementation]] confirms the mission's late-2028 launch target.

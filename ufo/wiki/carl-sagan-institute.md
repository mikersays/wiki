The **Carl Sagan Institute** is a Cornell-based interdisciplinary exoplanet and astrobiology research center, directed by [[lisa-kaltenegger]]. It focuses on the search for life beyond Earth via spectral modeling, biosignature design, and habitable-zone characterization.

## Work covered in this wiki

- Institutional home of the 2026 mineral-false-positives study (Parkinson, Kaltenegger et al., with [[university-of-edinburgh]] collaborators) characterizing abiotic mimics of the [[photosynthesis-red-edge]] for [[habitable-worlds-observatory|HWO]] direct-imaging missions ([[src-mineral-false-positives-biosignatures-2026-05]]).

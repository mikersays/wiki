## Summary

Newsweek's May 8, 2026 piece frames [[pursue]] Release 01 around the [[apollo-11]] technical-crew-debriefing material involving [[buzz-aldrin]]'s "unusual" sightings, while inventorying parallel [[apollo-12]] (Bean) and [[apollo-17]] (Cernan, Schmitt) transcript passages. It catalogs additional non-Apollo Release 01 items: a 1957 FBI interview with [[wladyslaw-krasuski]] about a 1944 75–100-yard "circular vertically-rising vehicle" near a German military compound; a March 2023 "blue triangular" object hovering near a U.S. national-security facility; a November 2023 [[centcom]] "bouncy ball" UAP in Syria; and a June 2024 [[usnorthcom]] "inverted teardrop" video. The piece foregrounds the disclosure politics around Rep. [[anna-paulina-luna]]'s cryptic "8 a.m." X post pre-launch, the celebratory reactions of Luna, [[tim-burchett]], and [[eric-burlison]], and frames the rollout as the first of an ongoing series per [[tulsi-gabbard]]. It closes with the [[theological-uap-framing]] thread — Rep. [[lauren-boebert]]'s "demonic" reading and Luna's directing followers to read the Book of Enoch.

## Key Claims

- PURSUE Release 01 contains "more than 160 files" issued under [[donald-trump]]'s directive to [[department-of-war]].
- White House spokesperson [[anna-kelly]] frames Trump as "the most transparent president in history."
- **Apollo 11 (Aldrin):** "first unusual thing… one day out or something pretty close to the moon," sizeable enough to prompt monocular use; "little flashes inside the cabin"; "a fairly bright light source which we tentatively ascribed to a possible laser." Crew speculated the daylight object was the **S-IVB** stage of the Saturn V.
- **Apollo 12 (Bean):** "particles and flashes of light 'sailing off in space.'"
- **Apollo 17:** Schmitt compared "very bright particles or fragments" tumbling near the spacecraft to "the Fourth of July"; Cernan described a deep-space flash "as bright as a train headlight shining between his eyes." Crew speculated ice or paint fragments — "largely a 'wild guess.'"
- **1957 FBI / Krasuski:** vehicle described as "circular in shape, 75 to 100 yards in diameter, and about 14 feet high," with a "rapidly moving" middle section "producing a continuous blur similar to an aeroplane propeller," observed in 1944 near a German military compound.
- **March 2023 object:** "large blue featureless triangular" with "powerful 'whitish blue' light," hovering near a national-security facility ~3 minutes; "backing up" in a "jerking" or "jumping" manner.
- **Nov 2023 [[centcom]] / Syria:** UAP "shaped as a bouncy ball" observed by USAF personnel ~7 minutes; not a threat.
- **June 2024 [[usnorthcom]] video:** "object with a vertical pole or bar attached to the bottom"; described as "an inverted teardrop with a vertically linear trailing mass."
- Luna pre-announced with "8 a.m." on X and pointed followers to a [[joe-rogan]] episode featuring [[tim-burchett]] and to the Book of Enoch (Watchers / Nephilim).
- Reactions: [[tulsi-gabbard]] frames Release 01 as "the first in what will be an ongoing joint declassification and release effort." Burlison: "historic first step." Burchett: "a great start." Luna: "a massive first step in the right direction."
- Theological framing: [[lauren-boebert]] — "more spiritual and, if you really want to go there, demonic."

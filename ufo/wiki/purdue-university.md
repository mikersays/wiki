**Purdue University** is a public research university in West Lafayette, Indiana. Its Department of Earth, Atmospheric, and Planetary Sciences (EAPS) hosts [[roger-wiens]]'s SuperCam calibration lab and led the 2026 *Nature Communications* paper on [[neretva-vallis]] nickel-rich iron sulfides ([[henry-manelski]] lead author; see [[src-perseverance-neretva-nickel-rocks-2026-04]]).

Separately, Purdue researcher [[darrell-evans]] argued in *The Conversation* (2026) that despite federal UAP investigation, U.S. universities — Purdue included — lack dedicated UAP research centers, doctoral training, or funding pipelines (see [[src-academic-uap-stigma-2026-04]]).

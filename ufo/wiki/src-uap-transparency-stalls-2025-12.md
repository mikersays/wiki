## Summary

Liberation Times reports that the [[uap-disclosure-act]] was again excluded from the final FY2026 NDAA, with original Senate champions [[mike-rounds]] and [[chuck-schumer]] notably muted compared to their forceful December 2023 colloquy. The piece traces the bill's stall to House leadership resistance ([[mike-turner]] as former HPSCI chair, House Armed Services chair [[mike-rogers]]'s "Four Corners" precondition told to [[eric-burlison]]), waning Senate-side political capital, and alleged threats against Senate staff. House Oversight transparency advocates [[anna-paulina-luna]], [[tim-burchett]], and [[eric-burlison]] continue pressing, including possible subpoena motions raised on the WEAPONIZED podcast with [[jeremy-corbell]] and [[george-knapp]]. The article also relays uncorroborated source claims that late 2024–early 2025 East Coast [[drone-incursions]] were Chinese craft using anti-gravitic propulsion, that similar reverse-engineering activity is operational in both China and the U.S., and that former Green Beret [[matthew-livelsberger]]'s classified manifesto warrants scrutiny as a potential [[nuclear-triad|nuclear-triad-adjacent]] warning.

## Key Claims

- The full [[uap-disclosure-act]] was not included in the final FY2026 NDAA and the language did not even appear in the Senate version this cycle.
- Senators [[mike-rounds]] and [[chuck-schumer]] have been "relatively muted" publicly despite their December 2023 colloquy pledge to keep pushing the Review Board and recovered-material provisions.
- House Armed Services chair [[mike-rogers]] told [[eric-burlison]] the provision could be added only with "Four Corners" sign-off (HASC/SASC/HPSCI/SSCI chairs and ranking members) — characterized as designed to fail.
- Senate Armed Services held no public AARO hearing in 2025 after Republicans took the gavel, breaking the 2023–2024 pattern.
- [[anna-paulina-luna]], [[tim-burchett]], and [[eric-burlison]] continue UAP transparency efforts via the [[task-force-on-declassification-of-federal-secrets]]; Burlison floated a subpoena motion modeled on the Epstein-list maneuver (WEAPONIZED podcast).
- [[tim-burchett]] described a tunnel encounter where a "shadowy figure" warned him off UAP investigations and suggested implications for religion (WEAPONIZED podcast).
- Sources claim late-2024/early-2025 East Coast [[drone-incursions]] were Chinese-operated anti-gravitic craft and that both China and the U.S. operate such propulsion (uncorroborated).
- Sources allege both Chinese exotic drones and separately non-human craft conduct ISR around East Coast ranges and a National Capital Region installation on the Chesapeake Bay tied to UAP reverse engineering and [[lockheed-martin-skunkworks]] (uncorroborated).
- [[matthew-livelsberger]]'s manifesto, recovered after his Las Vegas Trump Hotel vehicle detonation, was placed under Pentagon control and classified per Las Vegas Metropolitan Police.
- Some Senate advocates' enthusiasm reportedly waned in part due to alleged threats to senators' staff.

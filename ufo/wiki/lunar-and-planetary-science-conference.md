# Lunar and Planetary Science Conference (LPSC)

Annual planetary-science meeting hosted by the Lunar and Planetary Institute (LPI/USRA) in Houston; a primary venue for new mission and modeling results across the solar system. The 2026 edition is the venue at which the [[jhuapl]]/[[sandia-national-laboratories]] team presented Earth-to-Venus [[panspermia]] modeling using the [[venus-life-equation]] (per [[src-venus-panspermia-earth-origin-2026-04]]).

The 57th LPSC also hosted the Orpheus Mars hopper mission proposal by [[connor-bunn]] and [[pascal-lee]] ([[seti-institute]]), targeting Cerberus Fossae volcanic vents for extant-life [[biosignatures]] ([[src-orpheus-mars-volcanic-hopper-2026-04]]).

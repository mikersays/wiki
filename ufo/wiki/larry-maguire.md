# Larry Maguire

Conservative Member of Parliament ([[canada|Canada]]); UAP transparency advocate. Co-sponsor with Benjamin Bruce Schofield of a November 2024 petition urging a national UAP task force.

Per [[src-canada-sky-canada-project-2025-02]], Maguire argues [[transport-canada]] (not the [[canadian-space-agency]]) is the better fit to host any future Canadian UAP body, and stresses Canada lacks a designated UAP contact for international coordination with [[aaro]] and [[geipan]]. He cites the 2023 Yukon object shootdown as illustrating opaque DND handling and calls "openness by default" the proper posture absent national-security triggers.

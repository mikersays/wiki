## Summary

[[newsweek|Newsweek]] (Amanda Castro & Matthew Robinson, May 22 2026) covers the [[department-of-war|Department of War]]'s second [[pursue|PURSUE]] tranche. Unlike the [[washington-times|Washington Times]] wire ([[src-pentagon-second-batch-ufo-release-2026-05]]), Newsweek leads with Pentagon footage of the **February 2023 [[lake-huron-shootdown-2023|Lake Huron]] shootdown** — the clearest military-engagement clip in the archive — and adds an [[apollo-12|Apollo 12]] post-mission medical-debrief audio in which Conrad, Gordon, and Bean describe "streaks of lights" with eyes closed (NASA later attributed to internal visual effects / cosmic-ray phosphenes). The piece foregrounds a "two large orbs" Form 302 — the orbs forming a "distinct triangle" before disappearing — the [[sandia-new-mexico|Sandia, NM]] 1948–1950 green-orb cluster (**116-page file, 209 reports, residual copper powder**), and [[project-grudge|Project Grudge]] as the historical USAF program these cases fed. Additional new specifics: a 2022 IR clip of four objects over water near Iran, a 2024 [[u-s-coast-guard|U.S. Coast Guard]] recording over the southeastern U.S., a 2022 video of spherical objects entering/exiting water near a submarine ("flagged for further analysis" by [[aaro]] — [[transmedium-uap]]), and [[centcom|CENTCOM]] encounters 2018–2023.

## Key Claims

- PURSUE Release 02 dropped roughly two weeks after Release 01; war.gov/UFO has had **>1 billion hits** since launch.
- The release includes Pentagon footage "appearing to show the 2023 [[lake-huron-shootdown-2023|Lake Huron]] shootdown" of an unidentified object by a U.S. fighter jet — assembled by [[aaro]] in response to lawmaker requests; framed as "one of the clearest military engagement clips" in the archive.
- A U.S. intelligence officer's late-2025 Form 302 describes "two large orbs flare up" near his helicopter — oval, orange with white/yellow center, emitting light in all directions; later seen "chasing" fighter jets and forming a **distinct triangle** before disappearing. Likely the same encounter family as [[src-fox-helicopter-near-miss-orbs-2026-05]], with new triangle-formation detail.
- The [[sandia-new-mexico|Sandia, NM]] file is **116 pages**, **209 reports** of green orbs / discs / fireballs between 1948–1950, with accounts of objects "maneuvering, vanishing or exploding" and investigations into **residual copper powder** found at several sites — material fed into [[project-grudge|Project Grudge]], the short-lived 1949 USAF UFO program.
- Apollo 12 post-mission medical-debrief audio: astronauts [[pete-conrad|Pete Conrad]], [[richard-gordon|Richard Gordon]], and [[alan-bean|Alan Bean]] described "flashes appearing even with their eyes closed" during sleep periods; NASA later concluded **internal visual effects** (cosmic-ray phosphenes) rather than external light sources.
- >50 videos and additional military encounter reports across multiple decades; specifically: a 2022 IR clip of **four unidentified objects over water near Iran**; a 2024 [[u-s-coast-guard|Coast Guard]] recording over the southeastern U.S.; CENTCOM-AOR encounters 2018–2023 including over the Persian Gulf.
- A 2022 video shows **several spherical objects repeatedly entering and exiting the water near a submarine** — flagged by AARO for further analysis ([[transmedium-uap|trans-medium]] signature). Corroborates the Wiley 2X Zinc / submarine-vicinity USO clips previewed in [[src-debrief-pursue-next-batch-preview-2026-05]].
- Pentagon caveat: files "vary significantly in quality, sourcing and verification standards."

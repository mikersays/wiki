**Project Grudge** was a short-lived 1949 U.S. Air Force program that evaluated UFO sightings during the early Cold War. It succeeded Project Sign and was the direct precursor to [[project-blue-book|Project Blue Book]].

Per [[newsweek|Newsweek]] coverage of [[pursue]] Release 02 ([[src-newsweek-pursue-release-02-2026-05]]), Project Grudge absorbed the 209-report 1948–1950 [[sandia-new-mexico|Sandia, NM]] green-orb / disc / fireball cluster reported by the U.S. Air Force and the [[armed-forces-special-weapons-project]] — making Grudge a documented institutional consumer of the densest known early UAP–nuclear-enterprise cluster.

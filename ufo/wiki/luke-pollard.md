# Luke Pollard

UK Labour MP for Plymouth Sutton and Devonport. Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for Defence under the Starmer government.

On 11 December 2024, Pollard told the House of Commons that more than 50 years of [[ministry-of-defence|Ministry of Defence]] investigation into [[uap-disclosure|UAPs]] had found "no military threat" to the [[united-kingdom|United Kingdom]], and confirmed that the MoD ceased investigating UFO/UAP reports in 2009 ([[src-uk-mod-uap-no-threat-2024]]).

The statement landed only weeks after [[usafe-afa]]'s 48th Fighter Wing publicly reported [[drone-incursions|UAP/drone incursions]] over [[raf-lakenheath]], [[raf-mildenhall]], [[raf-feltwell]], and [[raf-fairford]].

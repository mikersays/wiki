# EuroUFO

Pan-European ufology federation publishing at euroufo.net. Edited by Italian researcher [[edoardo-russo]]. Hosts the annual [[philippe-ailleris|Ailleris]] European UAP Barometer (33,600 reports across 40 countries 2019–2024) and co-launched (December 2025) the **Euro UFO Index** with UAP Check, a 23-organization shared sightings catalog ([[src-european-uap-day-third-2026-03]]).

EuroUFO is a niche advocacy site rather than an independent news outlet; coverage of EU institutional engagement should be read as advocacy framing.

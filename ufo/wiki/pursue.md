# PURSUE (Presidential Unsealing and Reporting System for UAP Encounters)

**PURSUE** is a U.S. federal UAP records portal launched May 8, 2026 by the [[department-of-war]] at `war.gov/UFO`, operationalizing [[donald-trump]]'s February 19, 2026 Truth Social directive to release government files on UAP, UFOs, and "alien and extraterrestrial life" ([[src-uap-trump-disclosure-2026]]).

## Etymology

The name expands as **P**residential **U**nsealing and **R**eporting **S**ystem for UAP **E**ncounters. As of the May 8, 2026 launch capture ([[src-pursue-portal-launch-2026-05]]), the portal contains no public sighting-submission form — the "Reporting" element of the acronym appears to refer to government release rather than civilian intake. AARO retains civilian/insider intake under existing statute.

## Origin

Established in response to [[donald-trump]]'s Truth Social directive dated 2026-02-19 tasking the Secretary of War "and other relevant Departments and Agencies" with finding and releasing UAP/UFO/alien-life records. Buildup: [[src-trump-tpusa-ufo-tease-2026-04]], [[src-trump-pentagon-uap-files-2026-05]], [[src-alien-gov-domains-2026-03]].

## Operating Structure

- **Lead:** [[department-of-war]] ([[pete-hegseth]] as on-record principal).
- **Coordinating support:** [[odni]] ([[tulsi-gabbard]]).
- **Interagency participants:** [[department-of-energy]], [[aaro]], [[nasa]], [[fbi]], plus "additional Intelligence Community components" ([[src-dow-uap-files-release-2026-05]]).
- **Scope:** "unresolved" UAP cases only; [[aaro]] retains statutory responsibility for resolved-case reporting.
- **Cadence:** rolling tranches "every few weeks."
- **External pointer:** "Learn More" link routes only to `aaro.mil`.

## Release 01 (May 8, 2026)

- "CLEARED FOR RELEASE — MAY 8, 2026."
- Pentagon X-post and CBS reporting: **162 files** total (120 PDFs, 28 videos, 14 images); **108 redacted** (≈67%). [[src-space-com-pursue-video-trove-2026-05]] cites 161, a one-off counting variance.
- Page 1 of 17 (~10 records/page → ~170 portal entries) is composed entirely of [[fbi]] PDFs from a single file series — see [[fbi-case-file-62-hq-83894]].
- Featured imagery: an Apollo 17 lunar photograph with three dots above the surface; an FBI eyewitness sketch of an "ellipsoid bronze metallic object" 130–195 feet long; FBI photographs from New Year's Eve 1999; an "inverted teardrop" object over the UAE (2024); an object over Iraq (2022); a circular UAP in Greek airspace; a 2026 U.S. Army case; a 2020 USAF southern-U.S. case; an INDOPACOM "football-shaped" object near Japan.
- Apollo 12/17 voice transcripts include the "very jagged, angular fragments that are tumbling" / "Fourth of July out of Ron's window" passages — see [[lunar-uap-historical-claims]] and [[apollo-17]].
- **[[apollo-11]] Aldrin content surfaces post-launch:** AP-wire (Kim/Binkley) headlines [[buzz-aldrin]]'s 1969 debriefing description of "a 'sizeable' object close to the moon" and "a fairly bright light source… could be a laser" — first widely circulated text fragment from `NASA-UAP-D4` ([[src-fortune-aldrin-apollo-11-uap-2026-05]]; corroborated and expanded in [[src-newsweek-luna-teases-disclosure-2026-05]] with the **S-IVB Saturn V third-stage** prosaic hypothesis).
- **Newly surfaced Release-01 records (May 9–10 follow-up coverage):** 1957 [[fbi]] interview with [[wladyslaw-krasuski]] describing a 1944 75–100-yard "circular vertically-rising vehicle" near a German military compound; March 2023 "blue triangular" object hovering for ~3 minutes near a U.S. national-security facility; November 2023 [[centcom]] Syria "bouncy ball" UAP observed ~7 minutes by USAF personnel; June 2024 [[usnorthcom]] "inverted teardrop with a vertically linear trailing mass"; an Aegean Sea report describing "multiple 90-degree turns at an estimated 80 mph" plus "super-hot" orb and 4–5 additional flaring orbs; 1948 U.S. airmen / Netherlands report citing Swedish observers ("no presently known culture on earth").

## Relationship to Other Disclosure Efforts

- Distinct from but linked to [[aaro]]'s statutory case work.
- Complements the [[alien-gov-portal]] domains (`alien.gov` / `aliens.gov`) registered March 2026.
- Operates parallel to, not under, the [[uap-disclosure-act]] framework.
- Greer's Disclosure Project NPC event ([[src-greer-disclosure-event-npc-2026-05]]) ran the **same day** as a competing/parallel narrative, not a coordinated event.
- Promised to deliver [[anna-paulina-luna]]'s 46 demanded UAP videos ([[src-luna-46-uap-videos-demand-2026-03]]) "in a later release."

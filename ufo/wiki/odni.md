**Office of the Director of National Intelligence (ODNI)** oversees and coordinates the U.S. intelligence community. It has been a key interlocutor in [[uap-disclosure]] efforts, including:

- Prior UAP preliminary assessments to Congress
- Coordinating with [[aaro]] and DoD on UAP records
- In March 2026, publicly announcing via social media that UAP files "will soon be declassified" under [[donald-trump]]'s February 2026 directive ([[src-trump-uap-files-delay-2026-03]])
- On May 8, 2026, DNI [[tulsi-gabbard]] became the named ODNI coordinator for the [[pursue]] inaugural release: "actively coordinating the Intelligence Community's declassification efforts with the Department of War" for "comprehensive and unprecedented review" of holdings; called the May 8 release "the first in what will be an ongoing joint declassification and release effort" ([[src-dow-uap-files-release-2026-05]]).
- [[src-nbc-pursue-firsthand-encounters-2026-05]] attributes the [[pursue]] Release 02 late-2025 senior-intelligence-officer orange-orb helicopter encounter to an **ODNI memo** — the same encounter Fox framed as an FBI Form 302 ([[src-fox-helicopter-near-miss-orbs-2026-05]]); the location is withheld in both.

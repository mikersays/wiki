**Office of the Director of National Intelligence (ODNI)** oversees and coordinates the U.S. intelligence community. It has been a key interlocutor in [[uap-disclosure]] efforts, including:

- Prior UAP preliminary assessments to Congress
- Coordinating with [[aaro]] and DoD on UAP records
- In March 2026, publicly announcing via social media that UAP files "will soon be declassified" under [[donald-trump]]'s February 2026 directive ([[src-trump-uap-files-delay-2026-03]])

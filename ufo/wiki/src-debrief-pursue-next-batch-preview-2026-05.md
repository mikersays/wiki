## Summary

[[micah-hanks]] in [[the-debrief]] previews an imminent [[pursue]] "Release 02" tranche — up to 46 [[aaro]]-held UAP videos that Rep. [[anna-paulina-luna]] demanded in her March 31, 2026 letter to SecWar [[pete-hegseth]]. The [[department-of-war]] PURSUE portal's "Release 01" heading was relabeled simply "Release," and a transient site-listing of up to 140 blank pages on May 21 suggests imminent rollout (anticipated by week's end). Hanks walks through specific named clips: an August 26, 2022 four-object formation over Iran; a 2021 Syrian "instant acceleration" object; "transmedium" USOs near a U.S. submarine (March 25, 2022); "UAP USO formation Wiley 2X Zinc"; multiple spherical-orb cases; Tic Tac and "cigar-shaped or fat spherical" objects; "fast movers" (October 29, 2020); plus AARO-resolved cases including the **January 26, 2023 [[eglin-afb-diamond-formation-2023|Eglin AFB diamond-formation]] incident** and the **February 12, 2023 [[lake-huron-shootdown-2023|Lake Huron AIM-9X Sidewinder shootdown]]** filmed by F-16C pilot callsign [[aesir11|"AESIR11"]]. Luna's May 15 X post with current AARO director [[jon-kosloski]] and Rep. [[eric-burlison]] confirmed "40+ videos set for declassification" cleared review.

## Key Claims

- Up to 46 new UAP videos held by [[aaro]] are anticipated in PURSUE's next tranche, possibly by Friday May 22, 2026.
- The DoW PURSUE page's "Release 01" header has been shortened to "Release," interpreted as preparation for "Release 02."
- On May 21 the portal briefly showed up to 140 blank file-listing pages before reverting to Release 01 only.
- Named videos include: 4-object formation over Iran (Aug 26 2022); Syrian "instant acceleration" (2021); multiple [[unidentified-submerged-objects|sphere USOs]] near U.S. submarine "in and out of water" (Mar 25 2022); "UAP USO formation Wiley 2X Zinc"; sphere over Afghanistan in/out of clouds (Nov 23 2020); sphere with "erratic movement" (2022); "pulsing" sphere over water; three-video April 12 2021 spherical sequence.
- AARO's 2025 mission brief described a 2022 [[reaper-uas|MQ-9 Reaper]] EO capture of a "metallic orb" with characteristics "consistent with other 'metallic orb' observations in the region."
- "Cigar-shaped or fat spherical UAP" recorded Oct 15, 2022; a [[tic-tac-uap|Tic Tac-shaped]] object filmed by USCG EADS [[hc-144-ocean-sentry|HC-144 Ocean Sentry]] FLIR.
- October 29, 2020: three [[fast-mover|"fast moving UAPs"]] observed by U.S. personnel.
- One video designated "IIR 1 665 SO301 23/Eglin AFB" depicts the January 26, 2023 four-object diamond formation over the Gulf of Mexico whose pilot reported simultaneous aircraft system malfunctions. AARO's case-resolution report concluded "very likely an ordinary object" — possibly a [[lighting-balloon-hypothesis|lighting balloon]] — with only "moderate confidence."
- [[mick-west]] told The Debrief AARO's lighting-balloon hypothesis "always felt like something someone at AARO liked, but wasn't really supported by much evidence."
- Another video captured Feb 12, 2023 by F-16C pilot callsign **AESIR11** during the [[lake-huron-shootdown-2023|Lake Huron AIM-9X shootdown]]; former AARO director [[sean-kirkpatrick]] (CNA presentation, Apr 27 2026) said it was one of several early-2023 hobbyist-balloon shootdowns.
- Luna's May 15, 2026 X post (with [[jon-kosloski]] and [[eric-burlison]]) said the trio completed review of "40+ videos set for declassification" and expressed "full support" for Kosloski's good-faith declass efforts.

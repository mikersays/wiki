**Richard Doty** is a former employee of the Air Force Office of Special Investigations (AFOSI) who acknowledged feeding disinformation — faking documents and staging UFO sightings — to UFO researchers in the 1980s.

## Bennewitz Affair

Doty's most documented operation targeted Paul Bennewitz, an Albuquerque electronics entrepreneur who was monitoring electromagnetic signals from Kirtland Air Force Base. According to [[src-uap-reddit-disclosure-conspiracy-2026-05]] (citing Pilkington 2010), Doty and AFOSI fed Bennewitz fabricated stories of alien bases and hostile extraterrestrial activity, ultimately contributing to Bennewitz's psychological breakdown.

## Legacy

Doty later reinvented himself as a self-described expert on UFOs and government disinformation on the subject. His case is cited by Bastos & Duarte (2026) as a prime example of how U.S. intelligence agencies weaponized UFO narratives to protect classified programs — a practice that constitutes a [[confirmed-conspiracy-theory]]. His activities are connected to the broader [[majestic-12]] document forgeries and the [[robertson-panel]]'s legacy of [[uap-stigma]].

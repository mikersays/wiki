A **sulphur-bearing atmosphere** is an exoplanet atmosphere dominated by heavy sulphur-containing molecules (e.g. SO₂, H₂S, sulphur allotrope vapors). Because these molecules are heavy compared to H₂/He, they raise the atmosphere's mean molecular weight and can drive strong [[greenhouse-effect|greenhouse]] warming.

## Cases in this wiki

- **[[l-98-59-d]]** — 2026 [[university-of-oxford|Oxford]]-led study proposes a thick sulphur-bearing atmosphere as the engine driving a persistent [[magma-ocean-world|magma ocean]] over Gyr timescales ([[src-l-98-59-d-sulphur-magma-world-2026-05]]).
- **[[toi-1130b]]** — JWST transmission spectroscopy detected SO₂ within a 5.5 amu high-μ atmosphere ([[src-jwst-toi-1130b-atmosphere-2026-05]]).

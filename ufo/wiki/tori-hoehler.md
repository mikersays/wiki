# Tori Hoehler

Director of the [[center-for-life-detection]] at [[nasa-ames-research-center|NASA Ames Research Center]]; Deputy Principal Investigator of NASA's **[[invow|Investigating Ocean Worlds (InvOW)]]** project (2026–2031). Frames InvOW as a bridge between planetary science and Earth oceanography:

> "Bringing together planetary scientists and Earth oceanographers to understand alien oceans the way we understand our own — as complex systems where geology, physics, chemistry, and possibly biology, work together in concert." — Tori Hoehler ([[src-whoi-invow-ocean-worlds-2026-05]])

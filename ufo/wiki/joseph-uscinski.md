**Joseph Uscinski** is a political scientist at the University of Miami and co-author of *American Conspiracy Theories*. Quoted in the May 2026 Christian Science Monitor PURSUE coverage, Uscinski argues that UFO belief has persisted for decades and that unidentified sightings do not justify an alien conclusion: "If you see something that you don't know what it is, you don't get to say it's aliens."

# Library and Archives Canada

Canadian federal archive. Holds the [[national-research-council-canada|NRC]]'s 1967–1995 UAP-report archive — approximately 15,000 pages in total, with ~9,500 currently digitized and accessible online ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]]).

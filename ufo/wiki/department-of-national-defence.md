# Department of National Defence (DND)

Canada's federal defence ministry, including the Canadian Armed Forces. Per the [[sky-canada-project]] final report ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]]), DND held only "introductory level discussions" with the project and was not involved in its work. DND routinely receives public UAP reports but typically does not investigate unless the case falls inside an existing mandate (credible threat or search-and-rescue).

## Historical UAP programs

- **[[project-second-storey]]** (1950s) — DND committee that recommended ending Canadian military UAP investigations.

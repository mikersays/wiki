# Magnetotactic Bacteria

Bacteria that biomineralize intracellular magnetite (Fe₃O₄) or greigite crystals (**magnetosomes**) and align passively with geomagnetic field lines. The crystals' size, shape, and crystallographic purity are tightly biologically controlled, making them candidate **[[biosignatures]]** preservable in sediments long after the cells themselves are gone.

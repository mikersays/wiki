# Drake Equation

Frank Drake's 1961 factorization of the expected number of communicating civilizations in the Milky Way as a product of star-formation rate, planet fraction, habitable-fraction, life-emergence probability, intelligence emergence, communicative emergence, and civilization lifetime. Iconic in the [[seti-ai-breakthrough-listen-2025|SETI]] literature for emphasizing that the answer hinges on parameters whose values span many orders of magnitude.

The structure inspired the [[venus-life-equation]] (Izenberg et al. 2021) and is cited as such in [[src-venus-panspermia-earth-origin-2026-04]].

**Arnaud Saint-Martin** is a French MP affiliated with La France Insoumise (LFI). He is the **lead organizer** of the June 29, 2026 [[french-national-assembly|National Assembly]] symposium on unidentified aerospace phenomena — the first such event in French parliamentary history ([[src-france-national-assembly-uap-conference-2026-05]]).

Saint-Martin frames the work as "research into unidentified aerospace phenomena beyond fantasy" and pairs with [[pierre-henriet]] (Horizons), vice president of [[opecst]]. The symposium has institutional support from [[cnes]] and [[geipan]].

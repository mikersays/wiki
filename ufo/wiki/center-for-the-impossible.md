# Center For The Impossible

A research center at **Rice University** dedicated to the study of anomalous and "impossible" experience, closely associated with [[jeffrey-kripal|Jeffrey Kripal]]'s **Archives of the Impossible**. [[karin-austin|Karin Austin]] is its Managing Director (a role she holds jointly with the [[john-mack-institute|John Mack Institute]]). The Center is the institutional home, alongside Harvard, of the [[john-mack|John Mack]] archives transferred in 2022, and hosts a multidisciplinary team conducting **metadata analysis of experiencer reports across multiple archival collections** — work co-led with Amanda Focke of Rice's Woodson Research Center.

The Center co-sponsored the July 2026 [[src-breaking-the-taboo-uap-contact-symposium-2026-05|"Breaking the Taboo" symposium]].

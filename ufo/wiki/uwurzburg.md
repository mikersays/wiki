The **Julius Maximilian University of Würzburg** is a public research university in Bavaria, Germany. In 2022 it became the first Western university to formally add UAP investigation to its research canon, and operates the **AllSkyCAM** civilian UAP detection network. Würzburg is cited as the leading European counter-example to the U.S. academic UAP gap (see [[src-academic-uap-stigma-2026-04]]).

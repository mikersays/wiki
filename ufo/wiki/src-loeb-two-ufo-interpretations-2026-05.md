## Summary

Medium transcript by [[avi-loeb]] (May 11, 2026) of his interview on *The Hill's Rising* — one of ~30 interviews in three days following the May 8 [[pursue|PURSUE]] Release 01 ([[src-pursue-portal-launch-2026-05]]). Loeb crystallizes a **two-interpretations framing** of the unresolved UAP records: either they are **human-made adversarial technology** revealing U.S. defense vulnerabilities (citing the Chinese spy-balloon precedent) or, "maybe one in a million," they are **non-human technology** — in either case "a serious matter" warranting attention. He repeats his "best yet to come" thesis ([[src-loeb-pentagon-uap-release-best-yet-2026-05]]) and previews the next tranche of **46 [[anna-paulina-luna]]-demanded videos** ([[src-luna-46-uap-videos-demand-2026-03]]; cf. [[src-debrief-pursue-next-batch-preview-2026-05]]), disclosing that Luna **visited his Harvard office a few weeks ago** for an extended conversation. He gives a new prosaic reading of [[buzz-aldrin|Aldrin]]'s [[apollo-11]] flashes as **cosmic rays**, and argues [[artemis-ii]]'s recent dark-side-of-the-Moon imaging found nothing anomalous — so Apollo lunar lights are most likely asteroid impacts or optical artifacts ([[lunar-uap-historical-claims]]). Loeb also confirms the [[galileo-project]] is in **active communication with [[aaro]]** and discloses he is filming a cameo as himself in the movie *Sol Hershkowitz's Guide to Extraterrestrial Life*.

## Key Claims

- **Two-interpretations framing** of unresolved UAP: (1) adversarial human-made tech exposing U.S. defense vulnerabilities; (2) ~1-in-a-million from beyond Earth — "the biggest discovery ever made by humanity." Either is "a serious matter."
- It is a "win-win situation" to take the data seriously regardless of which interpretation prevails.
- **Aldrin's Apollo 11 flashes** are most plausibly **cosmic rays** — a new prosaic reading vs prior PURSUE coverage that had framed them as anomalous.
- [[artemis-ii]]'s recent dark-side-of-the-Moon imaging shows **no anomalous orbital lights**, undercutting Apollo lunar-light claims; Apollo flashes most likely **asteroid impacts** on the lunar surface or optical artifacts.
- PURSUE Release 01 does **not** include enough information to infer **distance, velocity, or acceleration** of objects — Loeb's team tried and "couldn't."
- [[galileo-project]] uses **three observatories** observing each object from different directions to derive altitude / velocity / acceleration and test against human-tech performance envelopes.
- The 46 videos demanded by Luna "hopefully will be released in the next batch... within the next month." Luna **visited Loeb's Harvard office a few weeks ago** for an extended discussion.
- U.S. has Earth-imaging satellite spatial resolution of "a few inches" and tracks IR flashes from Iranian ballistic-missile launches; the most sensitive UAP data will not be released because sensors themselves are classified.
- 2026 defense budget ≈ **$1 trillion**, ~100× NASA's science budget — intelligence agencies, not astronomers, would notice rare sky events first.
- **Galileo Project is in active communication with [[aaro]]** (first explicit confirmation in the vault).
- Invokes the **Albert Michelson 1894** University of Chicago "physics is done to the fifth decimal" speech — given a decade before special relativity — as a warning against scientific complacency on anomalies.
- References the [[interstellar-meteor-2014|2014 Pacific Ocean interstellar meteor]] expedition as proof Galileo can analyze any U.S.-government-held crash material for extra-solar origin.
- Loeb is filming a **cameo as himself** in *Sol Hershkowitz's Guide to Extraterrestrial Life*.

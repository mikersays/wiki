# Miranda Devine

**Miranda Devine** is a New York Post columnist and host of the *Pod Force One* podcast. Her April 29, 2026 *Pod Force One* interview with Rep. [[anna-paulina-luna]] elicited Luna's most explicit on-record UAP statement to date — that she has "observed things that are of nonhuman origin and creation" inside a SCIF — and Luna's pledge to hold a press conference once the underlying material is declassified ([[src-luna-nonhuman-origin-pod-force-one-2026-04]]). Devine pressed Luna directly on whether aliens are "moving among us with their strange machines"; Luna sidestepped the term "aliens" while affirming the [[non-human-intelligence|non-human]] framing.

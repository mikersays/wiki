# National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)

U.S. scientific agency under the Department of Commerce; oceanic, atmospheric, and weather mission. [[tim-gallaudet]] served as Acting Administrator and Deputy Administrator and as Acting Under Secretary / Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Oceans and Atmosphere.

## Earth-observation platforms

NOAA co-operates [[suomi-npp]] (with [[nasa]]), **NOAA-20**, and **NOAA-21** — polar-orbiting platforms hosting the [[viirs-dnb|VIIRS Day/Night Band]] sensor used for the global nighttime-lights record (relevant here as the calibration data behind [[terrestrial-technosignature]] analyses; see [[src-terrestrial-technosignatures-satellites-2026-04]]).

## Harmful algal bloom forecasting

NOAA issues seasonal harmful-algal-bloom (HAB) forecasts in partnership with state and local agencies — the operational complement to the [[nasa-jpl|NASA JPL]] orbital HAB-detection machine-learning tool framed as a remote biosignature-detection analogue ([[src-nasa-orbital-biosignature-algae-ai-2026-05]]).

# James Fox

Investigative documentary filmmaker focused on UAP. Director of *Out of the Blue*, *I Know What I Saw*, *The Phenomenon* (2020), and *Moment of Contact* (2022, on the [[varginha-incident]]).

## Activity

- **January 2026** — Hosted the **UAP (UFO) Press Conference** marking the 30th anniversary of the [[varginha-incident]]. Brought multiple alleged witnesses, including physician [[italo-venturelli]], to speak publicly; Rep. [[eric-burlison]] attended and used the venue to call for passage of the [[uap-disclosure-act]] and standalone [[uap-whistleblower-protections]]. Argued that in-person witness encounters carry a qualitatively different weight than filmed testimony. ([[src-burlison-marching-orders-2026-01]])

**Defense Autonomous Warfare Group** is a [[department-of-war]] organization focused on drones and autonomous-warfare capability. Its RDT&E line saw a "massive increase" in the Trump FY27 budget request, per DefenseScoop's May 2026 coverage of the [[lucas-drone|LUCAS]]/[[hivemind]] selection.

The group sits within the broader [[autonomous-warfare]] / [[drone-dominance]] priority cluster ($53.6B for autonomy/drones/contested logistics in FY27).

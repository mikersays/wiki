# Jeffrey Kripal

J. Newton Rayzor Professor of Religion at **Rice University**; historian of religion specializing in mystical and "impossible" experience. Co-author with [[whitley-strieber]] of *The Super Natural* (2016), which advances a phenomenological framework for treating experiencer accounts (UFO encounters, abductions, mystical states) as **data about consciousness** rather than claims about external physics. Founder of Rice's **Archives of the Impossible**, which houses [[jacques-vallee|Vallée]]'s papers and Strieber correspondence among other experiencer-account collections.

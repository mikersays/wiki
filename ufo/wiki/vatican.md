The **Vatican** (Holy See) is the sovereign city-state and seat of the Catholic Church. It appears in UAP discourse primarily through unverified claims that the Catholic Church holds evidence of [[non-human-intelligence]].

[[david-grusch]]'s 2023 congressional testimony alleged that [[pope-pius-xii]] knew of a 1933 UFO crash near Milan and backchanneled information to the U.S. government, which took possession after WWII. The claim resurfaces in [[the-age-of-disclosure]] (2025), where a former CIA official reportedly cites Vatican knowledge of NHI.

The Vatican does not engage publicly with UFO/UAP claims. The [[vatican-observatory]] has a stated policy of avoiding the topic in media due to [[uap-stigma]] (see [[guy-consolmagno]]). Italian-press headlines about "Holy See opens to UFOs" trace to a parish "Cultural Wednesdays" series at St. Anne's, not an official Holy See engagement.

# Strait of Hormuz

Maritime chokepoint between Iran and Oman connecting the Persian Gulf to the Gulf of Oman and the Indian Ocean. More than 80% of the oil and LNG transiting the Strait flows to Indo-Pacific allies (Japan, South Korea, China, India), making it one of the most strategically consequential waterways in the world.

## April 2026 — Operation Epic Fury

During [[operation-epic-fury]], Iran placed naval mines in the Strait; the U.S. responded by transiting Avenger-class minehunters from Japan via Sri Lanka ([[src-epic-fury-update-2026-04]]).

On April 22, 2026 — in retaliation for U.S. seizures of three sanctioned Iranian-affiliated tankers under the U.S. naval blockade — [[irgc-navy]] small craft seized two tankers in the Strait: the Liberian-flagged *Epaminondas* and the Panama-flagged *MSC Francesca*.

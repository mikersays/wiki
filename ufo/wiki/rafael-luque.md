**Rafael Luque** is an exoplanet astronomer and lead author of the 2025 *Astronomy & Astrophysics* panchromatic [[jwst]] reanalysis of [[k2-18b]]'s atmosphere. The study found insufficient evidence for dimethyl sulfide (DMS) or dimethyl disulfide (DMDS), formally rebutting the earlier [[biosignatures|biosignature]] claim by [[nikku-madhusudhan]]'s team.

**Glass Darkly Films** is the Catholic film production company founded by filmmaker [[sam-sorich]]. The company is producing a late-2026 documentary investigating UAP/UFO claims through a Catholic-Magisterium lens, in direct response to [[the-age-of-disclosure]]'s allegations that the [[vatican]] holds evidence of [[non-human-intelligence]].

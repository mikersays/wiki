**Pierre Henriet** is a French MP affiliated with Horizons (HOR) and vice president of the [[opecst|Parliamentary Office for the Evaluation of Scientific and Technological Choices]]. He co-organizes the June 29, 2026 [[french-national-assembly|National Assembly]] UAP symposium with [[arnaud-saint-martin]] (LFI) ([[src-france-national-assembly-uap-conference-2026-05]]).

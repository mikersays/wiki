# Lockheed Martin Skunkworks

Lockheed Martin's Advanced Development Programs division, headquartered in Palmdale, California; producer of historically classified aerospace projects (U-2, SR-71, F-117, F-22 contributions).

Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Liberation Times sources allege activity at a National Capital Region installation on the Chesapeake Bay tied to UAP material custody and exotic patents. These claims are uncorroborated and presented as anonymous third-party sourcing rather than independently verified reporting; cross-reference [[uap-reverse-engineering-claims-overview-2025]] for context.

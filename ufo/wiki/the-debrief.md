# The Debrief

**The Debrief** (thedebrief.org) is an independent science-and-defense outlet that publishes some of the deepest UAP-beat reporting outside legacy media. Recurring bylines include [[micah-hanks]] (UAP/intelligence), Christopher Plain, Tim McMillan (co-founder), and Marik von Rennenkampff.

Cited frequently on the disclosure beat for **rapid analytic coverage** of major events, sourced primary documents, and critical-but-engaged framing — neither the credulous tone of advocacy outlets nor the dismissive tone of mainstream skeptic coverage.

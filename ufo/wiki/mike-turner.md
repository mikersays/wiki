# Mike Turner

U.S. Representative (R-OH); former chair of the [[house-permanent-select-committee-on-intelligence]] (HPSCI). Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Turner reportedly opposed key elements of the [[uap-disclosure-act]] during the FY2024 NDAA conference committee, contributing to the bill's dilution from its original Schumer–Rounds form.

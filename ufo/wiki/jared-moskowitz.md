**Jared Moskowitz** is a Democratic U.S. Representative from Florida and the leading Democratic voice in the otherwise Republican-heavy congressional UAP-disclosure coalition. He co-chairs the bipartisan [[us-congress-uap-caucus|House UAP Caucus]] alongside Republican [[tim-burchett]].

## UAP activity

- Listed as a participant in the **June 9, 2026** Capitol Hill UAP disclosure press conference featuring whistleblower [[david-grusch]], hosted by [[leslie-kean]] and [[james-fox]], alongside Reps. [[eric-burlison]], [[anna-paulina-luna]], and [[tim-burchett]]. See [[src-grusch-disclosure-press-conference-2026-05]].
- His bipartisan co-chairmanship of the caucus with Burchett is frequently cited as evidence that UAP transparency has cross-party support, not just a Republican base-mobilization issue.

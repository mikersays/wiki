# Fort Bliss

U.S. Army installation in Texas, abutting the U.S.–Mexico border. One of two Army bases (with [[fort-huachuca]]) selected for the May 2026 [[jiatf-401]] [[directed-energy-weapons]] [[counter-uas]] pilot program announced by [[scott-mclellan]] and [[matt-ross]] (per [[src-directed-energy-5-bases-pilot-2026-05]]).

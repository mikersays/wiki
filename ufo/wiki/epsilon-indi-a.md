# Epsilon Indi A

Sun-like K-type star (~12 ly away in the constellation Indus); slightly cooler and smaller than the Sun. Host of the directly-imaged Jupiter analogue [[epsilon-indi-ab]].

## Why it matters

Epsilon Indi A is one of the closest Sun analogues with a confirmed Jupiter-class planet at a true Jupiter-like orbital separation, making the [[epsilon-indi-ab|Ab]] system a benchmark target for [[direct-imaging]] studies of cold gas-giant atmospheres.

**NASA-DARES** (NASA Decadal Astrobiology Research and Exploration Strategy) is a strategic planning initiative by [[nasa]] to develop a decadal-scale framework for astrobiology research and exploration. Task Force 2 is developing the strategy.

## Status

- Draft framework previewed at [[abscicon26]] (May 18, 2026)
- Public comment period forthcoming
- Final publication targeted for **December 2026**
- Frames [[astrobiology]] as "a central pillar of NASA science and exploration in the decades ahead"

**George Knapp** is a Las Vegas-based investigative journalist with a long history of UFO/UAP reporting, including early reporting on [[bob-lazar]]'s claims about S-4/Area 51.

## Role in the 2026 disclosure push

Per [[src-war-department-uap-release-2026-04]], Knapp worked with [[jeremy-corbell]] to provide Congress with file names, locations, and additional USAF UAP materials — feeding Rep. [[anna-paulina-luna]]'s 46-video request to Secretary of War [[pete-hegseth]].

## WEAPONIZED podcast (December 2025)

Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Knapp and [[jeremy-corbell]] hosted Reps. [[eric-burlison]] and [[tim-burchett]] on the WEAPONIZED podcast in late 2025; the episode produced Burlison's Epstein-list-style subpoena proposal for forcing the [[uap-disclosure-act]] to a vote.

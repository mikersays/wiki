# Last Universal Common Ancestor (LUCA)

Hypothetical most recent population of organisms from which all currently living organisms on Earth descend; conventionally dated ~4.2 billion years ago.

Per [[src-loeb-panspermia-impact-survival-2026-03]], [[avi-loeb]] argues LUCA may have been delivered to Earth from Mars via [[panspermia]] — Mars cooled to habitable conditions before Earth, [[alh84001]] demonstrates the transit thermal-survival window, and the new ~2 GPa impact-survival evidence underwrites the spallation-ejection step. Loeb summarizes: "It is possible that we are all Martians."

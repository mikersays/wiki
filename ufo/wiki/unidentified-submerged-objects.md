# Unidentified Submerged Objects (USOs)

Anomalous objects observed entering, leaving, or operating beneath water. A growing focus area within the UAP discussion, distinct from purely aerial UAP because of the dramatically different sensing and detection regime in marine environments.

## Advocacy

- [[tim-gallaudet]], retired Rear Admiral and former naval oceanographer, is a leading proponent of expanded USO scrutiny. December 2025: "If there were any extraterrestrial visitors that did not want to be seen, this is where they'd hide." (See [[src-gallaudet-kirkpatrick-clash-2026-04]].)
- Connects to [[uss-nimitz-uap-incident]] (Tic Tac entered/exited water) and to broader [[uap-disclosure]] / [[non-human-intelligence]] hypotheses.

**Jeffrey Nuccetelli** is a USAF veteran and UAP witness who served 16 years in the military, including as a military police officer.

## Key Testimony

Testified at the September 9, 2025 House Oversight hearing on UAP transparency (see [[src-uap-transparency-hearing-2026-04]]). Described multiple UAP incursions near Vandenberg AFB in 2003–2005 during high-stakes NRO launches. Objects he observed appeared "like flying buildings" and were witnessed by multiple personnel. He called for independent research funding, end to over-classification, and strong witness protections.

## Notable Quotes

> "Transparency is the foundation of truth. Without it, witnesses like us are dismissed. Protect the witnesses. Many stay silent out of fear for their careers, reputations, and the safety of their families. Protect them and you will embolden others to join this cause."

## Pre-hearing announcement

Confirmed as a Sept 9, 2025 hearing witness in the September 3, 2025 DefenseScoop preview that also reported the introduction of the **UAP Whistleblower Protection Act (H.R. 5060)** by Reps. [[tim-burchett]] and [[anna-paulina-luna]] ([[src-uap-whistleblower-protection-act-2025-09]]).

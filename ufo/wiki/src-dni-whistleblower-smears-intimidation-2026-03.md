## Summary

An [[odni]]-overseen UAP investigation conducted by the **Director's Initiatives Group** — a task force created by DNI [[tulsi-gabbard]] to address Intelligence Community weaponization — is alleged to have been compromised by [[cia]]-linked elements. Multiple sources claim the effort sought to discourage Congress from pursuing further UAP hearings and to deter additional whistleblowers. The task force was wound down in February 2026 after alleged missteps. The allegations surfaced on the WEAPONIZED podcast, where [[jeremy-corbell]] and [[george-knapp]] interviewed [[matthew-brown]]. Brown and [[dylan-borland]] were allegedly targeted in a smear campaign originating from a private SCIF within ODNI.

## Key Claims

- **SCIF-based smear campaign:** An ODNI official told Corbell that, inside a SCIF, Brown was labeled "a racist, Jew hater," accused of fabricating his encounter with the [[immaculate-constellation]] document, and that words like "counter espionage" and "treason" were used. Brown denies all allegations (Corbell/Brown)
- **ICIG whistleblower process as a trap:** Brown was invited to Liberty Crossing Intelligence Campus and asked to become an ICIG whistleblower. He declined, citing Borland's warning that the process was designed "to assess just how much I know, not to move forward with an investigation" (Brown/Borland)
- **CIA compromise of the inquiry:** Multiple sources allege CIA-linked elements within the Director's Initiatives Group worked to undermine the UAP investigation from within (anonymous sources)
- **Access mapping, not fact-finding:** Brown felt the group was more interested in identifying which classified compartments he had accessed than in the substance of his disclosures (Brown)
- **Gabbard not implicated:** Sources said there was no indication that DNI Gabbard was involved in any whistleblower intimidation or smear effort (anonymous sources)

## Notable Quotes

> "One individual from ODNI told me that, in the SCIF, Matthew Brown is a racist, Jew hater... Matthew Brown admitted to completely making up and fabricating that he saw an Immaculate Constellation document." — Jeremy Corbell, relaying ODNI official's claims

> "We know UAP are real. We want to get to the bottom of it. Come in, meet with us and tell us your story." — Unnamed Director's Initiatives Group leader to Brown

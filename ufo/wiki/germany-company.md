# Germany Company

**Germany Company** is a Cali, Colombia–based retailer of metal detectors, marketed for finding "hidden treasure" — gold, silver, jewelry, coins, *guacas*. [[david-velez-el-potro|David Vélez "El Potro"]] is a shareholder.

## Role in the Buga Sphere narrative

Per [[src-buga-sphere-el-pais-cali-maussan-handoff-2025-10]]: after [[jose-arias-restrepo|José Arias Restrepo]] handed the [[buga-sphere|Buga Sphere]] to his cousin Vélez, the object was held on a display stand at Germany Company's Cali premises for approximately two months before being transferred to [[jaime-maussan]]'s team and exported to Mexico.

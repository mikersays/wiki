# Twentyfour Industries

Munich, Germany–based manufacturer and **co-production partner of [[perennial-autonomy|Perennial Autonomy]]**, building the [[merops-interceptor|Merops]] interceptor in Germany to serve European and NATO buyers (per [[src-dronexl-perennial-interceptor-doctrine-2026-05]]). The arrangement supports eastern-flank deployments — Lithuania bought 48 Merops in April 2026, with Poland and Romania training/deploying on the interceptor. An open question raised by the source is whether co-production lowers Merops unit cost or simply adds production volume.

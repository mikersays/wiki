# Royal Canadian Mounted Police (RCMP)

Canada's federal and national police service. The [[sky-canada-project]] final report ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]]) names the RCMP as one of four federal entities routinely receiving public UAP reports — alongside [[transport-canada]], [[department-of-national-defence]], and [[canadian-space-agency]] — and notes the RCMP is "often the first point of contact" for unusual sightings.

## Position on UAP reporting

The RCMP welcomed the Sky Canada Project's final recommendations, agreeing that "a centralized, science-led service would help ensure that reports are handled consistently and transparently."

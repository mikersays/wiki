**New Paradigm Institute** (NPI) is a UAP advocacy and education organization. It hosts the full proposed [[uap-disclosure-act]] legislative text on its website, serving as a public reference for the most comprehensive UAP transparency bill introduced in Congress.

Part of the broader UAP disclosure advocacy ecosystem alongside the [[sol-foundation]] and [[uap-disclosure-fund]].

# Hexamethylenetetramine (HMT)

Nitrogen-bearing prebiotic organic molecule (C₆H₁₂N₄) detected in the [[murchison-meteorite]] in 1969. HMT polymerizes to formaldehyde and ammonia under aqueous/UV conditions, making it a plausible upstream feedstock for amino-acid and nucleobase synthesis pathways relevant to [[prebiotic-chemistry]].

## Why it matters

HMT detection in Murchison is one of the foundational data points for [[exogenous-organics|exogenous organic delivery]] models — meteorites carry not just simple organics but also reactive nitrogen-bearing precursors that could seed prebiotic chemistry on planetary surfaces.

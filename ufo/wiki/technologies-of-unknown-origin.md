**Technologies of unknown origin** is a statutory term from the proposed [[uap-disclosure-act]] (Sec. L103), defined as any materials, debris, vehicles, or equipment associated with UAPs lacking known human manufacture.

## Legislative Significance

Under Section L110 of the UAPDA, the federal government would exercise **eminent domain** over all recovered technologies of unknown origin and biological evidence of [[non-human-intelligence]], including materials held by private contractors. This provision is distinct from but overlapping with alleged [[crash-retrieval-programs]].

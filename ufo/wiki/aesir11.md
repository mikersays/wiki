**AESIR11** is the USAF F-16C pilot callsign attached to the [[lake-huron-shootdown-2023|February 12, 2023 Lake Huron AIM-9X Sidewinder shootdown]] of an unidentified object. Per [[micah-hanks]]'s May 21 2026 preview of [[pursue]] Release 02, AESIR11 captured the in-cockpit video that is one of up to 46 [[aaro]]-held UAP clips slated for the next PURSUE tranche.

Former AARO director [[sean-kirkpatrick]], in an April 27 2026 [[center-for-naval-analyses]] presentation, characterized the Lake Huron incident as one of several early-2023 hobbyist-balloon shootdowns.

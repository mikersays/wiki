**Joint C-sUAS University (JCU)** is the [[jiatf-401]]-operated training pipeline for joint counter–small-UAS personnel. JCU is one of three institutional supports JIATF-401 offers [[installation-commanders]] alongside transparent knowledge sharing and the [[jiatf-401-marketplace]] materiel pipeline, as named in the December 8, 2025 SecWar-signed homeland C-UAS guidance ([[src-jiatf-401-counter-drone-homeland-guidance-2026-01]]).

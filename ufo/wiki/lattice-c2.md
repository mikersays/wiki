**Lattice** is [[anduril]]'s AI-enabled, open-architecture command-and-control software suite that fuses sensors and effectors for distributed [[counter-uas]] detection, tracking, classification, and engagement. In March 2026 it became the [[jiatf-401]] C-UAS C2 backbone via an **$87M task order** — the first task order issued under [[anduril]]'s new **$20B firm-fixed-price enterprise IDIQ** contract vehicle ([[army-enterprise-contracts]]); see [[src-anduril-army-20b-counter-drone-idiq-2026-03]].

Lattice complements Anduril's hardware portfolio (Wisp / Heimdal / Pulsar sensors, Anvil / Roadrunner interceptors) deployed in U.S. C-UAS architectures — including the [[flyaway-kit]].

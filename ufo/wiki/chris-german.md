# Chris German

Senior scientist at the [[whoi|Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution]]; specialist in seafloor hydrothermal systems and [[ocean-worlds|ocean-world]] [[biosignatures|biosignature]] interpretation. Principal Investigator of NASA's **[[invow|Investigating Ocean Worlds (InvOW)]]** project (2026–2031, ~$5M, 16-lab consortium); previously led the predecessor NASA-funded **[[exow|Exploring Ocean Worlds]]** initiative.

In the InvOW announcement German frames the program as a "needle-in-a-haystack" signal-to-noise problem: on distant ocean worlds, non-biological organics may dominate, inverting Earth's situation, and InvOW must prepare interpretive groundwork before [[europa-clipper]]'s 2030 Europa arrival.

> "If you are alive today, this is the first generation where the question of whether there is life elsewhere in the universe could be answered in your lifetime." — Chris German ([[src-whoi-invow-ocean-worlds-2026-05]])

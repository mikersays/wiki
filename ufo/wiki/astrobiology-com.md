# Astrobiology.com

Astrobiology news aggregator edited by [[keith-cowing|Keith Cowing]] (also founder of SpaceRef). Recurring vault source for press-release republication, conference coverage (notably [[abscicon26|AbSciCon26]]), and arXiv preprint summaries. Tends to reproduce primary press releases verbatim, so vault sources from Astrobiology.com are typically passthrough copies of institutional or AAS-society releases.

**Joel Hurowitz** is a professor of geosciences at Stony Brook University and a member of the Perseverance science team. He led the 2025 *Nature* paper on potential biosignature minerals in the Cheyava Falls rock at [[jezero-crater]] (see [[src-mars-perseverance-biosignature-2025]]) and refined the interpretation of [[henry-manelski]]'s 2026 [[neretva-vallis]] nickel discovery (see [[src-perseverance-neretva-nickel-rocks-2026-04]]).

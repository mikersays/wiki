**Maria Cantwell** is a Democratic senator from Washington and ranking member of the Senate Committee on Commerce, Science and Transportation.

In March 2026, Cantwell criticized DOD/FAA coordination failures after Texas [[counter-uas|C-UAS]] incidents, drawing a parallel to the 2024 Reagan National Airport midair collision. She referenced the upcoming FIFA World Cup 2026 as an urgency driver for resolving interagency gaps. See [[src-texas-counter-drone-senators-2026-03]].

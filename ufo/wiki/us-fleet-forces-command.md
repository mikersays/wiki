# U.S. Fleet Forces Command

U.S. Navy four-star Atlantic-fleet command. In January 2015, its operations officer authored the "URGENT SAFETY OF FLIGHT ISSUE" email warning of multiple near-midair collisions during the [[uss-theodore-roosevelt]] pre-deployment exercise — attaching the [[go-fast-video|"Go Fast" FLIR video]]. The email vanished from recipients' accounts the next day per [[tim-gallaudet|Gallaudet's]] testimony ([[src-gallaudet-house-testimony-2024-11]]).

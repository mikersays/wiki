# Tulsi Gabbard

**Tulsi Gabbard** is the Director of National Intelligence in [[donald-trump]]'s second administration. She is the named [[odni]] coordinator for the May 2026 interagency UAP declassification effort under [[pursue]].

## UAP Disclosure Role

- **2026-05-08:** ODNI is "actively coordinating the Intelligence Community's declassification efforts with the Department of War" for "comprehensive and unprecedented review" of holdings; called the May 8 release "the first in what will be an ongoing joint declassification and release effort" ([[src-dow-uap-files-release-2026-05]], [[src-trump-uap-apollo-photos-2026-05]]).
- Coordinates IC contributions across [[fbi]], [[department-of-energy]], [[nasa]], [[aaro]], and "additional Intelligence Community components."

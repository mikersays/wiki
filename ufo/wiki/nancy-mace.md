**Nancy Mace** is a U.S. Representative (R-SC) and member of the [[us-congress-uap-caucus]]. She has pushed for classified SCIF briefings on UAP, specifically requesting a session with Secretary of State [[marco-rubio]] (Dec 21, 2025).

## UAP Involvement

- Advocated for a SCIF-first approach to UAP disclosure, reflecting the caucus's Dec 2025 strategic shift from public hearings to classified sessions
- Her SCIF request targets [[marco-rubio]] specifically, leveraging his Senate Intelligence Committee UAP briefing history

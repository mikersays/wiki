**Adam Frank** is an astrophysicist at the University of Rochester involved in the search for intelligent life. In a May 13, 2026 [[npr]] *All Things Considered* interview, Frank dismissed [[pursue]] Release 01 as "fuzzy blob videos or photos and unverifiable personal testimony" and criticized the inclusion of at least one apparently unrelated document (a launch-failure report). He demands scientific evidence — bodies, craft, or published study results — to substantiate congressional claims about crash retrievals and nonhuman biologics.

Frank's skepticism aligns with [[sean-kirkpatrick]]'s and [[mick-west]]'s reactions to PURSUE. See [[src-npr-astrophysicist-uap-reaction-2026-05]].

The **Earth-Life Science Institute (ELSI)** is an astrobiology and origins-of-life research center at the Institute of Science Tokyo. In April 2026, ELSI researchers [[harrison-b-smith]] and [[lana-sinapayen]] published the [[agnostic-biosignature|agnostic population-statistics biosignature detection method]], which searches for signs of life across exoplanet populations via statistical correlations arising from [[panspermia]] and environmental modification, without identifying any specific biosignature chemistry. See [[src-panspermia-agnostic-biosignature-2026-04]].

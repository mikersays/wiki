# Brown dwarfs

Substellar objects between ~13 and ~80 Jupiter masses — too massive for [[white-dwarf]]-style stellar evolution but not massive enough for sustained hydrogen fusion. Sit just above the planetary-mass boundary that exoplanet entities like [[wd-0806b]] straddle.

## Relevance to the vault

- Methodological neighbor to **cold-giant exoplanet atmosphere retrievals**: T- and Y-dwarf atmosphere models inform interpretations of cold directly imaged planets.
- The 2026 [[ben-lew]] et al. [[jwst|JWST]] [[wd-0806b]] paper (see [[src-aas-nova-wd-0806b-atmosphere-2026-04]]) leverages brown-dwarf retrieval techniques and extends them with altitude-dependent [[eddy-diffusion|K_zz]] — applicable to cool brown dwarfs as well as cold-giant exoplanets.

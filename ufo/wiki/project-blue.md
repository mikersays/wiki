**Project Blue** is a [[seti-institute]]-associated proposal for a dedicated small space telescope to directly image planets around [[alpha-centauri]] A and B in visible light. The design target is a contrast ratio of approximately **10⁻¹⁰**, sufficient to detect an Earth-analog in the habitable zone of a nearby Sun-like star.

If funded and built, Project Blue would provide a targeted follow-up path for the [[jwst]] gas-giant candidate at [[alpha-centauri-a]] ([[src-alpha-centauri-a-exoplanet-2026]]) and for any future rocky-planet candidates in the system.

Not to be confused with [[kona-blue]] (DHS UAP reverse-engineering program) or [[project-blue-book]] (USAF UFO investigation).

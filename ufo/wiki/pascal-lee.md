**Pascal Lee** is a planetary scientist at the [[seti-institute]] and the Mars Institute. He co-authored (with [[connor-bunn]]) the Orpheus Mars VTOL hopper mission proposal, presented at the 57th [[lunar-and-planetary-science-conference]], targeting extant-life [[biosignatures]] in Cerberus Fossae volcanic vents ([[src-orpheus-mars-volcanic-hopper-2026-04]]).

# UFO File Release: What's Inside the 162 Secret Pentagon UAP Files?

## Summary

Sunday Guardian (Sumit Kumar) inventories the May 8, 2026 [[pursue]] **Release 01** of 162 declassified UAP records published by the [[department-of-war]] at war.gov/UFO. Format breakdown given as **~120 PDFs, 28 videos, ~14 photographs**, sourced from the [[fbi]], [[nasa]], [[department-of-state]], and the Pentagon. Specific incidents catalogued: late-2025 western-U.S. infrared footage; a 2024 [[us-indo-pacific-command]] "football-shaped" object near Japan; an October 2023 Greece video of an object making **90-degree turns at ~80 mph**; Syria footage of two orange semi-transparent formations vanishing within seconds; and [[apollo-12]]/[[apollo-17]] lunar photos. The deepest single-case treatment is [[fbi-case-file-62-hq-83894]], framed as covering 1947–1968 with **18 separate reports**, including a 1967 **Chesapeake** witness encounter with "**undersized creatures**" wearing T-shirts. Pentagon framing remains "unexplained, not confirmed extraterrestrial."

## Key Claims

- Release 01: **162 declassified files** = ~120 PDFs + 28 videos + ~14 photographs.
- Contributing agencies: [[fbi]], [[nasa]], [[department-of-state]], Pentagon ([[department-of-war]]).
- Many cases remain officially **"unresolved"** — investigators could not determine what objects were.
- Redactions limited to witness identities and facility locations, but per Pentagon: "**No redactions have been made … concerning information about the nature or existence of any encounter reported as a UAP.**"
- Public access via the [[pursue]] UFO Archive Portal at war.gov/UFO without security clearance.
- Late-2025 western-U.S. infrared imagery shows UFOs "moving at unusual speeds."
- 2024 sighting near Japan reported by [[us-indo-pacific-command]] personnel: "football-shaped airborne object."
- October 2023 Greece video: object making **90-degree turns at ~80 mph**.
- Syria footage: **two orange semi-transparent aerial formations** appearing then vanishing within seconds.
- [[apollo-12]] and [[apollo-17]] photographs include unexplained lights/objects above the lunar surface; Pentagon offers no determination.
- [[fbi-case-file-62-hq-83894]] characterized as covering 1947–1968 with **18 separate reports** including eyewitness testimony, technical analysis, photographs, and period media coverage.
- **1967 Chesapeake report**: a man encountered a "transparent craft" occupied by "undersized creatures" wearing "regular trouser pants and T-Shirts"; witness "spoke coherently" but under "emotional strain."

## Notable Quotes

> "No redactions have been made to any files released under President Trump's directive concerning information about the nature or existence of any encounter reported as a UAP or related phenomena." — Pentagon statement

> "Undersized creatures wearing regular trouser pants and T-Shirts." — 1967 Chesapeake witness, on occupants of a "transparent craft" (FBI memo, 62-HQ-83894)

# Cartel Drone Threat

Framing of international drug cartels and other criminal actors as a primary domestic [[drone-incursions|drone threat]] vector — distinct from foreign-state intelligence collection (e.g., the China / [[barksdale-afb|Barksdale]] / [[nuclear-triad]] narrative).

Central to Rep. [[gabe-vasquez|Vasquez's]] case for the [[counter-act|COUNTER Act]] and [[secure-our-skies-drone-safety-act|Secure Our Skies Drone Safety Act]]. Also cited by the [[faa]] as the (later disputed) reason for the February 2026 [[el-paso-international-airport]] TFR.

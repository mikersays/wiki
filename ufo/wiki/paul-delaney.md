# Paul Delaney

Emeritus astronomer at York University (Toronto). Not involved in the [[sky-canada-project]] but quoted in CTV News's coverage of the final report ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]]) estimating that **1–2% of UAP cases remain truly unidentified** after investigation — a figure broadly consistent with [[geipan|GEIPAN]]'s long-running French statistics.

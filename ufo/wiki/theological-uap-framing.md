**Theological UAP framing** describes the religious and theological vocabulary increasingly applied to UAP and [[non-human-intelligence]] claims by public figures and religious institutions during the 2025–2026 disclosure cycle. As [[uap-disclosure]] has moved into mainstream U.S. politics, multiple parallel framings have emerged:

- **Demonic / fallen-angel / [[nephilim|Nephilim]]**: [[perry-stone]]'s 2026 framing of the alleged phenomenon as Genesis-6-style spiritual manifestation ([[src-perry-stone-pastor-briefing-2026-05]]). Rep. [[lauren-boebert]] sources the explicit Nephilim language to a reported [[courthouse-news-service|Courthouse News]] piece (May 15, 2026): "There's nothing that says that fallen angels, that Nephilim just disappeared. I do believe that this is more spiritual, and, if you really want to go there, demonic." ([[src-religious-republicans-uap-files-2026-05]])
- **"Spiritual demons"**: [[jd-vance]]'s framing in 2026 commentary ([[src-trump-pentagon-uap-files-2026-05]]).
- **Ezekiel's wheel / Genesis 1 open cosmology**: Rep. [[tim-burchett]] (R-TN) cites the Book of Ezekiel's "wheel within a wheel… like a gyroscope" and Genesis 1:1 to argue against confining God "into some Americanized version" ([[src-religious-republicans-uap-files-2026-05]]).
- **Stick-to-scripture moderation**: Rep. [[eric-burlison]] (R-MO) advises sticking to what the Bible actually says ("the Bible doesn't say Satan has a tail, horns and a pitchfork") while affirming that the briefings he has received "underscore the Christian worldview" — first vault placement of Burlison within the theological-framing cluster ([[src-religious-republicans-uap-files-2026-05]]).
- **Interdimensional beings / Book of Enoch / "biblically accurate" angels**: [[anna-paulina-luna]]'s on-podcast framing referencing the Book of Enoch's Watchers; Luna explicitly directed followers to read the Book of Enoch on the morning of the May 8 [[pursue]] launch ([[src-newsweek-luna-teases-disclosure-2026-05]]). She also posted a "biblically accurate" angel image — interlocking rings studded with eyes and wings — that commenters linked to the 2013 Middle East eight-pointed-star UAP clip ([[dow-uap-pr38]]); Luna told Courthouse News the post was "unrelated" ([[src-religious-republicans-uap-files-2026-05]]).
- **Rapture-cover narrative**: speculation (Stone via his son Jonathan) that government may frame an "alien invasion" to explain a future rapture.
- **Catholic-Magisterium engagement**: filmmaker [[sam-sorich]]'s 2026 Catholic documentary positioning the Catholic intellectual tradition as a serious counterpart to disclosure ([[src-catholic-filmmaker-vatican-ufo-2026-04]]).
- **Institutional avoidance**: the [[vatican-observatory]]'s explicit policy of declining UFO-topic media engagement (per [[guy-consolmagno]]) — itself a theological-stigma stance.

These framings sit alongside, and often at odds with, scientific framings of UAP (cf. [[uap-scientific-study]], [[non-human-intelligence]]).

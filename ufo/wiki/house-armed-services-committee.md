# House Armed Services Committee (HASC)

U.S. House standing committee with jurisdiction over DoD authorization. Venue through which the [[counter-act|COUNTER Act]] advanced into the 2026 NDAA, championed by member Rep. [[gabe-vasquez]]. **H.R. 5060** (UAP Whistleblower Protection Act, Burchett/Luna, Aug 2025) was jointly referred here alongside [[house-oversight-committee|House Oversight]] and [[house-permanent-select-committee-on-intelligence|HPSCI]] ([[src-uap-whistleblower-protection-act-2025-09]]).

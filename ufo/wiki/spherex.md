# SPHEREx

NASA all-sky near-infrared spectrophotometric survey (Spectro-Photometer for the History of the Universe, Epoch of Reionization, and Ices Explorer). Per [[src-loeb-panspermia-impact-survival-2026-03]], [[avi-loeb]] cites SPHEREx as having detected organic molecules on interstellar object [[3i-atlas]] — observational support for treating interstellar objects as targets of astrobiological interest.

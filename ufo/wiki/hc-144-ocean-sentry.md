The **HC-144 Ocean Sentry** is a U.S. Coast Guard medium-range maritime patrol aircraft (EADS-CASA CN-235 derivative). Per [[micah-hanks]]'s [[pursue]] Release 02 preview, an HC-144 FLIR captured a [[tic-tac-uap|Tic Tac-shaped]] object — one of up to 46 [[aaro]]-held UAP videos slated for the next PURSUE tranche.

The **Society for UAP Studies** is a nonprofit scholarly society that operates the double-blind peer-reviewed journal *[[limina-journal|Limina: The Journal of UAP Studies]]* and convenes international UAP symposia. Per the 2026 *The Conversation* essay by [[darrell-evans]] (see [[src-academic-uap-stigma-2026-04]]), it is one of the few formal scholarly venues for UAP research but lacks a tenured-faculty footprint at U.S. research universities. Prof. Tim Murithi, the [[icer]] South African representative, sits on the Society's Advisory Board.

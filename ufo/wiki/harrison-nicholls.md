**Harrison Nicholls** is a researcher in the Department of Physics at the [[university-of-oxford|University of Oxford]]. He led the 2026 study proposing [[l-98-59-d]] as the prototype of a new exoplanet class — a sulphur-rich global [[magma-ocean-world]] ([[src-l-98-59-d-sulphur-magma-world-2026-05]]). Nicholls argues the result demonstrates that current small-planet taxonomy (rocky / gaseous / water-rich) is too coarse.

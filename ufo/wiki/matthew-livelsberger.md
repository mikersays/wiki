# Matthew Livelsberger

Former U.S. Army Green Beret who died after detonating a vehicle outside the Trump International Hotel in Las Vegas on January 1, 2025. A manifesto recovered at the scene was placed under Pentagon control and classified per the Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Department.

Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Liberation Times sources describe the manifesto's UAP and "anti-gravitic" claims as credible enough to warrant urgent scrutiny, including allegations connecting [[drone-incursions]] to exotic propulsion programs. The classification of his materials means his claims have not been independently verified or rebutted in public.

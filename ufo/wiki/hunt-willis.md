**Hunt Willis** is the chief legal officer of the [[disclosure-foundation]]. He led the FOIA legal effort against the [[nsa]] that resulted in the May 2026 release of 300+ pages of UFO intelligence reports, many formerly classified [[top-secret-umbra]].

## Key Actions

- Directed FOIA request and appeal to the NSA for UAP-related records connected to the **Yeates Memo** (a classified NSA affidavit from a 1980 FOIA lawsuit, declassified in 2009)
- Successfully overturned the NSA's initial blanket denial of the request
- Pledged further legal challenges to remaining NSA redactions on UAP material dating to the 1960s

## Notable Quotes

> "It is simply unacceptable for security classification exemptions to remain on government documents that predate the Civil Rights Act." — Hunt Willis

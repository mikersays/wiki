**Richard F. Gordon Jr.** was the command module pilot of [[apollo-12]] (Nov 1969). Per [[newsweek|Newsweek]] coverage of [[pursue]] Release 02 ([[src-newsweek-pursue-release-02-2026-05]]), Gordon — along with [[pete-conrad]] and [[alan-bean]] — appears in a post-mission medical-debrief audio describing eyes-closed "flashes" during sleep periods; NASA later attributed the phenomenon to internal visual effects (cosmic-ray phosphenes).

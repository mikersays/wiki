**Henry Manelski** is a planetary scientist at [[purdue-university]] (EAPS). He is lead author of a March 2026 *Nature Communications* paper reporting nickel-rich iron sulfides in [[neretva-vallis]] on Mars, the highest Martian bedrock nickel concentrations yet measured (~1.1 wt% across 32 rock targets). Manelski built the SuperCam LIBS nickel calibration that enabled the quantitative result and frames the find as catalytic-metal context for the early-Mars [[prebiotic-chemistry]] story.

# USS Dwight D. Eisenhower UAP Incident (2021)

Cluster of UAP encounters during the [[uss-dwight-d-eisenhower]] 2021 deployment, per [[tim-gallaudet]] in his May 1, 2025 Capitol Hill expert briefing ([[src-uap-expert-briefing-2025-05]]).

- An SH-60 Seahawk crew chief reported FLIR video of a metallic sphere a few hundred feet above the carrier, traveling horizontally before accelerating to the horizon and disappearing on landing.
- F-18 pilots from the Eisenhower Strike Group reported repeated UAP encounters at high altitude during the same deployment.

Sits alongside the [[uss-nimitz-uap-incident]] and the 2015 [[uss-theodore-roosevelt]] / [[go-fast-video]] cluster as a Navy-carrier UAP datum.

**Alan Bean** was the lunar module pilot of [[apollo-12]] (Nov 1969), the fourth human to walk on the Moon. Per [[newsweek|Newsweek]] coverage of [[pursue]] Release 02 ([[src-newsweek-pursue-release-02-2026-05]]), Bean — along with [[pete-conrad]] and [[richard-gordon]] — appears in a post-mission medical-debrief audio describing eyes-closed "flashes" during sleep periods; NASA later attributed the phenomenon to internal visual effects (cosmic-ray phosphenes).

# Whitley Strieber

American author; *Communion* (1987) popularized the modern grey-alien abduction narrative and seeded much of the visual iconography (large-eyed humanoids) now treated as a [[alien-abduction-phenomenon|cultural template]]. Co-author with [[jeffrey-kripal]] of *The Super Natural* (2016), which reframes his own experiencer history as data for a phenomenological [[psychological-uap-framing|psychological-phenomenon]] research program rather than as testimony about extraterrestrial hardware.

# Sullivan (UAP Whistleblower)

39-year-old Bronze Star-winning U.S. intelligence officer (full name not disclosed in IBTimes UK reporting) who reportedly served at [[nasic]] (Wright-Patterson) and the [[nsa]] (Fort Meade). Found dead on **May 12, 2024**, weeks before he was scheduled to testify to Congress on [[crash-retrieval-programs]] and alleged [[non-human-intelligence|non-human technology]] encounters.

## Status

- **Cause of death:** officially ruled accidental lethal overdose.
- **FBI referral:** Rep. [[eric-burlison]] (R-MO) referred the case to the [[fbi]] in April 2026, calling it "sudden and suspicious."
- Cited as one of an alleged ~11 deaths/disappearances since 2022 of personnel linked to UAP/aerospace/advanced-tech programs (see [[uap-personnel-deaths-pattern]]).

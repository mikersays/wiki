# Wladyslaw Krasuski

Subject of a 1957 [[fbi]] interview surfaced in [[pursue]] Release 01 on May 8, 2026, in which Krasuski recounted a 1944 wartime sighting of a "circular vertically-rising vehicle" near a German military compound.

Per [[src-newsweek-luna-teases-disclosure-2026-05]], Krasuski described the object as:

- **Shape:** "circular in shape"
- **Size:** "75 to 100 yards in diameter, and about 14 feet high"
- **Behavior:** rapidly moving middle section "producing a continuous blur similar to an aeroplane propeller"
- **Setting:** observed in 1944 near a German military compound

The record is part of the FBI document tranche in PURSUE Release 01 (likely within the [[fbi-case-file-62-hq-83894]] series or its mid-century adjuncts), and slots into the broader WWII-era "foo fighter / unconventional aerial-form" eyewitness record set.

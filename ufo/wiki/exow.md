# Exploring Ocean Worlds (ExOW)

Prior [[nasa]]-funded [[ocean-worlds|ocean-world]] research initiative led by [[whoi|WHOI]] and PI [[chris-german]]; focused on **physical and geological processes** that shape ocean-world habitability. Predecessor to the 2026 **[[invow|Investigating Ocean Worlds]]** project, which pivots toward [[biosignatures|organic-molecule]] interpretation while inheriting much of ExOW's institutional network.

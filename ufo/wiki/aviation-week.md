**Aviation Week & Space Technology** is a U.S. aerospace and defense trade publication. Its coverage targets an industry/operator audience and tends to emphasize **platform, sensor, and procurement provenance** over political framing.

## UAP coverage

- May 8 2026 [[pursue|PURSUE]] Release 01 coverage by Pentagon editor Brian Everstine and senior aerospace journalist Guy Norris ([[src-aviation-week-pursue-transparency-2026-05]]) foregrounds [[reaper-uas|MQ-9 Reaper]] dominance, names a CENTCOM-submitted Greece **short-wave infrared (SWIR)**-only diamond UAP, surfaces a 2022 Russian Su-27/Su-35 close approach to an MQ-9 over western Syria, and notes X-band radar jamming from the Turkish border during a February 2023 F-15E Syria sortie — operational details largely absent from mainstream-wire PURSUE coverage.
- Posture is **neutral-to-skeptical**: questions whether future tranches will disclose collection methodology, classification, or full-resolution sensor data.

# Julia Mossbridge

**Julia Mossbridge** is a cognitive neuroscientist (University of San Diego) and UAP researcher who has publicly assessed the [[buga-sphere]] as likely human-made and called for it to be submitted to [[galileo-project]]-grade controlled, reproducible analysis.

## Position on the Buga Sphere

Per [[src-buga-sphere-ibtimes-ball-from-nowhere-2026-03]]:

- Says the sphere "seems kind of like an art project... only humans can do," suggesting it is human-fabricated rather than non-terrestrial.
- Calls for submission to the [[galileo-project]]'s rigorous-analysis framework as the appropriate scientific bar.

# FBI Confirms UFO Files for Public Release 'Very Soon'

## Summary

Two days before the May 8, 2026 [[pursue]] portal launch, FBI Director [[kash-patel]] used a Sean Hannity podcast appearance (May 6) to **pre-confirm** that UAP files compiled under [[donald-trump]]'s February 19, 2026 directive ([[src-uap-trump-disclosure-2026]]) had "already been delivered" and would be "publicly releasing this information very soon." Patel attributed the interagency lead to the [[department-of-war]] under [[pete-hegseth]] — corroborated two days later by [[src-dow-uap-files-release-2026-05]] and [[src-pursue-portal-launch-2026-05]]. The piece is a Yahoo aggregation of original Men's Journal reporting by Kevin Harrish; it adds no new documentary content but timestamps Patel's pre-launch framing immediately preceding the rollout. Historical context retold from prior NYT reporting includes the FY2024 NDAA stripping of [[uap-disclosure-act]] provisions, with [[tim-burchett]] and [[chuck-schumer]] quoted from 2023.

## Key Claims

- Patel told Hannity: Trump "stood up an interagency process with the [[department-of-war]] leading that effort to get out the documentation."
- Patel: documents "have already been delivered" and will be released "very soon."
- Trump's February 2026 directive instructed agencies to release records on UAP and ET life ([[src-uap-trump-disclosure-2026]]).
- As of the May 6 podcast taping, no documents had yet been made public — the wait ends May 8 with [[pursue]] Release 01.
- Historical: in 2023 House conferees stripped UAP declassification provisions from the FY2024 NDAA; [[tim-burchett]] and [[chuck-schumer]] quoted decrying the strip-out.

## Notable Quotes

> "[Trump] stood up an interagency process with the Department of War leading that effort to get out the documentation. … They're going to be publicly releasing this information very soon. We are all for it." — [[kash-patel]]

> "We've already delivered the documents, they're coming out — that's it." — [[kash-patel]]

> "We got totally ripped off. We got completely hosed. They stripped out every part." — [[tim-burchett]] (on FY2024 NDAA, 2023)

# Scott McLellan

U.S. Army Colonel; deputy director of [[jiatf-401]] as of May 2026. Per [[src-directed-energy-5-bases-pilot-2026-05]], McLellan was the on-record JIATF-401 spokesperson for the May 7 2026 announcement of the five-base [[directed-energy-weapons]] [[counter-uas]] pilot, citing prior testing at [[white-sands-missile-range]] and operational use along the U.S.–Mexico border to argue directed-energy systems can defeat drones without "undue risk to passenger aircraft."

**Voice370** is an advocacy group representing families of passengers and crew of [[malaysia-airlines-flight-mh370]], which vanished in March 2014 with 239 people aboard.

The group has persistently pressed the Malaysian government to continue search efforts. According to [[src-mh370-families-extend-search-2026-03]], Voice370 urged Malaysia to extend [[ocean-infinity]]'s "no-find, no-fee" contract before its June 2026 expiry and to open similar terms to other deep-sea exploration firms. The group warned that southern-hemisphere winter weather would likely prevent a resumed search before the contract lapses.

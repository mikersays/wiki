**Brent Ingraham** is the Assistant Secretary of the Army for Acquisition, Logistics and Technology (ASA(ALT)) and the architect of the Army's enterprise-contract consolidation push. Under his direction the Army awarded **14 enterprise contracts in eight months** through early 2026, collapsing 118 prior disparate contracts (an 88% reduction). The first task order under the resulting [[anduril]] $20B vehicle — an $87M [[lattice-c2]] award supporting [[jiatf-401]] — was announced in March 2026 (see [[src-anduril-army-20b-counter-drone-idiq-2026-03]]).

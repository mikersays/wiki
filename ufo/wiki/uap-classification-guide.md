# UAP Classification Guide

[[chris-mellon|Mellon's]] claim — voiced at the May 1, 2025 Capitol Hill expert briefing ([[src-uap-expert-briefing-2025-05]]) — that a **UAP-specific classification guide** was created in the U.S. government shortly after he provided three then-unclassified Navy UAP videos to the *New York Times* in late 2017, retroactively reclassifying UAP material as national-security-sensitive in apparent contradiction to the executive order on classification.

If accurate, this guide is a candidate mechanism for the over-classification the FY2026 [[ndaa-uap-provisions]] now require [[aaro]] to account for.

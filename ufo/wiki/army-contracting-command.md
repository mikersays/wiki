**Army Contracting Command** (ACC) is the Army's acquisition execution headquarters. It runs the new generation of [[army-enterprise-contracts]] — firm-fixed-price IDIQ vehicles with pre-negotiated terms — including the March 2026 $20B [[anduril]] award and the August 2025 $10B [[palantir]] award. As of early 2026, ACC was on its third RFI re-opening for an additional commercial-software enterprise IDIQ winner (see [[src-anduril-army-20b-counter-drone-idiq-2026-03]]).

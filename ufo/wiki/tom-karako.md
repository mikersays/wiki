# Tom Karako

Senior fellow and director of the **Missile Defense Project** at [[csis|CSIS]]. Frequently quoted defense analyst on U.S. air- and missile-defense posture and, increasingly, [[counter-uas|counter-drone]] capability.

In April 2026 commentary on the [[jiatf-401|JIATF-401]] C-UAS surge, Karako argued the U.S. [[drone-incursions|drone problem]] had been "artificially divided" between overseas combat and homeland threats and that increased investment, while "dramatically overdue," reflects a long-anticipated requirement ([[src-counter-drone-middle-east-jiatf-2026-04]]). He suspects [[coyote-interceptor|Coyote interceptors]] and Merops systems have done "the lion's share of the drone killing" in CENTCOM.

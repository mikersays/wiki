# Bumblebee Counter-UAS System

[[counter-uas|C-UAS]] platform under operational assessment by [[jiatf-401]].

- **V1**: small low-cost low-collateral kinetic effector for dual reconnaissance + C-UAS use; trained with [[3rd-infantry-regiment]] at [[fort-belvoir]] in April 2026 in a JIATF-401 / [[jtf-ncr]] joint exercise.
- **V2**: first-person-view (FPV) multi-rotor drone-on-drone interceptor procured by JIATF-401.

CONOPS includes both **follow-home** to identify the operator and **air-to-air defeat** with minimal collateral. Spokesperson: Lt. Col. [[adam-scher]].

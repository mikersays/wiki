# Spitzer Space Telescope

[[nasa|NASA]] infrared space telescope, operational 2003–2020. The IR predecessor to [[jwst|JWST]] for cold-target exoplanet science.

## Vault-relevant work

- IRAC instrument **discovered [[wd-0806b]]** in 2011 — the second-coldest [[direct-imaging|directly imaged]] exoplanet known. The 2026 JWST follow-up (see [[src-aas-nova-wd-0806b-atmosphere-2026-04]]) builds directly on this initial detection.

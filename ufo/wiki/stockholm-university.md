**Stockholm University** is a Swedish public research university. It has actively published peer-reviewed UAP research since 2017, often in collaboration with the [[nordic-institute-theoretical-physics]]. Their most recent paper (October 2025) appeared in *Scientific Reports*. Stockholm is cited as part of an emerging Nordic UAP research stream that contrasts with U.S. academic stigma (see [[src-academic-uap-stigma-2026-04]]).

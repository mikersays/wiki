# Leonard David

**Leonard David** is a veteran U.S. space journalist who has covered civil and military space, planetary science, and astrobiology for several decades. He runs the **Inside Outer Space** column at leonarddavid.com and is a long-time contributor to SPACE.com, Aerospace America, and Scientific American. His tone is straight-ahead aerospace-trade reportage — neutral, primary-source-quoting, restrained on speculation.

# Philippe Ailleris

European UAP researcher; author of EuroUFO's annual **European UAP Barometer**. The 2025 edition (December 24 2025) totaled **33,600 reports across 40 European countries (2019–2024)**, adding 5 new national organizations and 2 international collections to the underlying corpus ([[src-european-uap-day-third-2026-03]]).

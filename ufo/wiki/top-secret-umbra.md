**TOP SECRET UMBRA** is a classification designation used for highly sensitive **signals intelligence** (SIGINT). Many of the 300+ [[nsa]] UFO intelligence reports released to the [[disclosure-foundation]] via FOIA in May 2026 carried this classification.

[[hunt-willis]] (Disclosure Foundation chief legal officer) argued that pre-Civil-Rights-Act-era documents should not retain this classification, and pledged legal challenges to remaining NSA UMBRA redactions on UAP material dating to the 1960s.

# Naval Base Kitsap

U.S. Navy installation in Washington state; homeport for the Pacific Fleet's ballistic-missile and guided-missile submarine force, and a designated nuclear-weapons storage facility. Strategic significance is comparable to [[whiteman-afb]] within the [[nuclear-triad|nuclear triad]]. Selected as the Navy site in the May 2026 [[jiatf-401]] [[directed-energy-weapons]] [[counter-uas]] pilot (per [[src-directed-energy-5-bases-pilot-2026-05]]).

**Sam Sorich** is a Catholic filmmaker and founder of [[glass-darkly-films]]. He teaches at John Paul the Great Catholic University (San Diego) and previously worked at Salt and Light Catholic Media (Toronto), Spirit Juice Studios (Chicago), and on the 8BEATS anthology and a René Girard documentary purchased by Peter Thiel.

Sorich is producing a late-2026 Catholic-perspective UAP documentary as a direct response to claims aired in [[the-age-of-disclosure]] that the [[vatican]] holds evidence of [[non-human-intelligence]]. The film team has shot at the [[bunker-soratte]] in Italy and features podcaster [[jesse-michels]], former military chaplain [[matthew-gray]], and investor [[brantly-millegan]] (ChurchPOP). Sorich frames experiencer outreach as pastoral.

# Project Magnet

Early Canadian government UAP investigation effort run by [[transport-canada]] in the 1950s. Per the [[sky-canada-project]] final report ([[src-canada-sky-canada-final-2025-07]]), Project Magnet was "quickly shut down" and is cited as a historical precedent for how Canadian federal UAP programs have failed to sustain themselves.

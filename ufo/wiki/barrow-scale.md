The **Barrow Scale** classifies civilizations by their capacity for inward miniaturization of technology — micro/nano-scale engineering — complementing the outward-facing [[kardashev-scale]]. While the Kardashev scale measures energy use at planetary, stellar, and galactic levels, the Barrow scale tracks mastery of progressively smaller structures.

Vidal et al. (2026) argue in their comprehensive [[technosignatures]] review that both scales should inform [[seti]] search strategies, since detectable signatures may arise from miniaturized engineering as well as megastructures. See [[src-technosignatures-review-2026-05]].

# Kirsten Gillibrand

U.S. Senator (D-NY); long-time co-author of [[uap-disclosure-act]] provisions and original sponsor of the Senate amendment that established the modern Pentagon [[aaro|All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office]] reporting and oversight architecture. Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Gillibrand has done relatively little public advocacy for further transparency in 2025 compared to her earlier Armed Services posture.

## Hearing activity

- **Nov 19, 2024** — Senate Armed Services Emerging Threats subcommittee hearing with [[jon-kosloski]]. Her questioning prompted Kosloski's now-frequently-cited unresolved-case descriptions (orange orb / "blacker than black" object pursued by a law-enforcement officer; large stationary cylinder over a Southwest facility) — see [[src-aaro-kosloski-testimony-2024]] and the analytical lens in [[src-debrief-aaro-course-correcting-2024-11]].

# Kirsten Gillibrand

U.S. Senator (D-NY); long-time co-author of [[uap-disclosure-act]] provisions and original sponsor of the Senate amendment that established the modern Pentagon [[aaro|All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office]] reporting and oversight architecture. Per [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]], Gillibrand has done relatively little public advocacy for further transparency in 2025 compared to her earlier Armed Services posture.

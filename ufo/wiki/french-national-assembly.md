The **French National Assembly** (Assemblée nationale) is the lower house of the French Parliament. It pairs with the Senate in the joint [[opecst]] science-advisory body.

On **June 29, 2026** the National Assembly will host the **first French parliamentary symposium on unidentified aerospace phenomena**, organized by MP [[arnaud-saint-martin]] (LFI) and co-organized by MP [[pierre-henriet]] (HOR, OPECST vice president). The event will run in the Assembly's largest hall (250-seat capacity) with institutional support from [[cnes]] and [[geipan]] ([[src-france-national-assembly-uap-conference-2026-05]]).

Parallels other parliamentary UAP initiatives — [[japan-uap-parliamentary-group]] and the [[sky-canada-project]] track.

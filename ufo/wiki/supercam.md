**SuperCam** is the multi-mode remote-sensing instrument suite on NASA's [[perseverance-rover]]: laser-induced breakdown spectroscopy (LIBS), Raman spectroscopy, visible/infrared spectroscopy, color imaging, and an integrated microphone. Heritage flows from ChemCam on Curiosity. [[roger-wiens]] led instrument development at [[los-alamos-national-laboratory]] and now operates a calibration lab at [[purdue-university]].

LIBS is the technique that first detected anomalous nickel concentrations in [[neretva-vallis]] before [[pixl]] localized the signal at finer scale; quantification depended on Purdue-built nickel calibration standards (see [[src-perseverance-neretva-nickel-rocks-2026-04]]).

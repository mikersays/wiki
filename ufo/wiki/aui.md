**Associated Universities Inc. (AUI)** is a non-profit research management consortium that contracts with federal agencies to run large-scale scientific facilities. AUI hosted and co-organized [[aaro]]'s August 5–6, 2025 invite-only UAP research workshop, with **Dr. Gretchen Stahlman** and **Dr. Tim Souck** as principal organizers.

The workshop produced AARO's ~17-page whitepaper *"2025 UAP Workshop: Narrative Data, Infrastructures, and Analysis"* ([[src-aaro-uap-data-collection-whitepaper-2026-03]], companion to [[src-uap-aaro-research-workshop-2025]]). Workshop governance was conducted under IRB approval.

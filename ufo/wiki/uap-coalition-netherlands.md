# UAP Coalition Netherlands

Dutch UAP advocacy organization that has led much of the European-Union-level institutional engagement effort on UAP since 2024. Per [[src-european-uap-day-third-2026-03]]:

- **April 2025**: Met MEP [[fabio-de-masi]] (Germany), who had filed a December 2024 European Commission parliamentary question on UAP and member-state critical infrastructure.
- **September 2025**: Met MEP [[lukas-mandl]] (Austria) on the same agenda — stigma & mental health, flight safety, EU cooperation.
- **September 2025**: Sponsored 24-scientist (later 36) joint feedback to the European Research Area (ERA) Act framing [[uap-stigma]] as a systemic research barrier.
- **September 2025**: Filed a UAP-recognition feedback to the EU Space Act consultation.
- **December 2025**: Met with the European Commission's DG RTD (Research and Innovation) on stigma in unconventional research.

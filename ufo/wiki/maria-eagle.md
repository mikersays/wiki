# Maria Eagle

UK Labour MP and Minister for Defence Procurement. On 27 November 2024 told Parliament that the UK uses "multi-layered and credible force protection measures" against [[drone-incursions]] over USAF-operated UK bases ([[raf-lakenheath]], [[raf-mildenhall]], [[raf-feltwell]]) but declined to specify them — the capability counterpart to [[luke-pollard|Pollard's]] later UAP-policy [[src-uk-mod-uap-no-threat-2024|"no threat"]] line.

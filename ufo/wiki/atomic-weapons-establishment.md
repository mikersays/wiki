# Atomic Weapons Establishment (AWE)

UK organization responsible for design, manufacture, and maintenance of nuclear warheads. Operates sites at **Aldermaston, Burghfield, Blacknest, and Coulport**. Flagged in the 27 November 2024 Westminster Hall debate as undefended by [[ground-based-air-defence|GBAD]] (see [[src-uk-airspace-defense-gap-2024-11]]). Relevant to [[nuclear-uap-correlation]].

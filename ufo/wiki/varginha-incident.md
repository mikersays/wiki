# Varginha Incident

Alleged January 20, 1996 incident in Varginha, Minas Gerais, [[brazil]], during which the Brazilian military and civil authorities are said to have captured two non-human entities. Eyewitnesses include three young women who reported seeing a small humanoid creature in the city, and medical personnel who claimed to have examined captured entities. One of the foundational cases in modern Brazilian UAP lore.

The incident is the subject of [[james-fox]]'s 2022 documentary *Moment of Contact*.

## 30th-anniversary press conference (January 2026)

In January 2026, James Fox hosted a UAP (UFO) Press Conference marking the 30th anniversary. Rep. [[eric-burlison]] (R-MO) attended and used the platform to publicly call for passage of the [[uap-disclosure-act]] and standalone [[uap-whistleblower-protections]], framing the legislative push as "marching orders." Physician [[italo-venturelli]] gave first-person medical testimony of examining a "being not of this planet" on the day of the incident, describing a conscious, calm entity resembling a 7-year-old with a teardrop-shaped head. Multiple alleged witnesses said they had suppressed their accounts for decades due to fear of professional and physical retaliation. ([[src-burlison-marching-orders-2026-01]])

## Significance

- One of two foundational Brazilian UAP cases alongside the May 1986 [[brazil-night-of-ufos-1986]] mass radar sighting.
- Distinct from the 1977 [[operation-prato]] military investigation in Maranhão and Pará.
- Cited at Brazil's June 2022 Senate UAP hearings as part of the country's documented UAP history. ([[src-brazil-uap-senate-hearings-2026]])
- 2026 anniversary platform demonstrates ongoing cross-border influence on U.S. UAP disclosure advocacy: a Brazilian historical case used by a U.S. congressman to argue for U.S. legislation.

# Golden Dome for America

**Golden Dome for America** is the [[donald-trump|Trump]] administration's named homeland aerial-threat-defense initiative, established by one of the President's earliest executive orders. Per [[pete-hegseth|Secretary of War Hegseth]]'s FY27 posture testimony to the [[house-armed-services-committee]] ([[src-hegseth-fy27-posture-testimony-2026-04]]), it is framed as "a revolutionary approach to defending our nation from advanced aerial threats" and is the centerpiece of the "Defend the U.S. Homeland" line of effort.

## Relevance to UAP / drone defense

Although Hegseth's testimony does not name [[norad]], [[usnorthcom]], [[jiatf-401]], or the [[flyaway-kit|FAK]] in connection with Golden Dome, the program is the closest organizational umbrella in the FY27 budget for the homeland counter-aerial-threat mission that has been driven, in practice, by the 2023–2026 [[drone-incursions]] over U.S. military bases and nuclear sites. It overlaps in scope with the [[counter-uas]] portfolio currently being scaled by [[jiatf-401]].

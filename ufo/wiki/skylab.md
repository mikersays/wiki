# Skylab

**Skylab** was the United States' first space station (1973–1979), hosting three crewed missions (Skylab 2, 3, 4) totaling 171 days of crewed occupation.

## UAP Relevance

The **Skylab Technical Crew Debriefing (1973)** — record `NASA-UAP-D7` — was included in [[pursue]] Release 01 (May 8, 2026) alongside debriefings from Apollo 11, 12, and 17. It is the first appearance in this wiki of Skylab as part of an official U.S. government UAP record set ([[src-pursue-portal-launch-2026-05]], [[pursue-release-01-catalog]]).

The specific UAP-relevant content has not yet been catalogued in this vault — pending text extraction from `ufo/raw/assets/pursue-release-01/nasa-uap-d7-skylab-techincal-crew-debriefing-1973.pdf` (note: filename retains the original portal's misspelling of "Technical"). The record's existence is confirmed via the PURSUE manifest CSV.

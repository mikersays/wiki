# Kelly Luis

**Kelly Luis** is a [[nasa-jpl|NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory]] scientist and co-author of the self-supervised machine-learning harmful-algal-bloom detection tool led by [[michelle-gierach|Michelle Gierach]], published in [[american-geophysical-union|AGU]]'s *Earth and Space Science* ([[src-nasa-orbital-biosignature-algae-ai-2026-05]]). She framed the goal as bridging satellite technologies "to better serve end users and their needs, from aquaculture to tourism."

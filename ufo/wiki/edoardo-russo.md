# Edoardo Russo

Italian ufologist; editor of [[euro-ufo-network|EuroUFO]]. In July 2025 presented "New Perspectives in UAP Studies from the Old Continent" at MUFON's Cincinnati symposium, surveying European tooling for UAP investigation: AR field-investigation tools, cognitive-psychology interview techniques, photo-analysis software, automated IFO identification, machine-learning case classification, automatic sky-monitoring, mass digitization, specialized search engines, AI archival tools, and UFO chatbots ([[src-european-uap-day-third-2026-03]]).

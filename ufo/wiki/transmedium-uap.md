**Transmedium UAP** is the [[aaro]] definitional category for unidentified anomalous phenomena observed transitioning between airborne, seaborne, spaceborne, and other physical domains. The transmedium attribute encompasses [[unidentified-submerged-objects|Unidentified Submerged Objects (USOs)]] observed "in and out of water."

PURSUE Release 02 previewed by [[micah-hanks]] includes at least two transmedium-coded clips:

- March 25, 2022 — multiple spherical objects observed near a U.S. submarine, transitioning between air and water.
- "UAP USO formation Wiley 2X Zinc" — formation observed transmedium.

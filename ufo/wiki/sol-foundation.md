# Sol Foundation

Stanford-associated UAP research and policy organization. Advocates a whole-of-government UAP approach including a designated cabinet-department coordinator. Publications cited in [[tim-gallaudet|Gallaudet's]] November 2024 testimony include "Beneath the Surface" (on [[unidentified-submerged-objects]]) and "UAP in Crowded Skies."

Senior strategic advisors include [[tim-gallaudet]] ([[src-gallaudet-house-testimony-2024-11]]).

## Third SOL Symposium — Baveno, Italy (October 2025)

Per [[src-european-uap-day-third-2026-03]], the third SOL Symposium ran October 24-27 2025 at Baveno on Lake Maggiore (Italy) — the **first** SOL Symposium held in Europe. 20+ speakers and 440 guests; described by EuroUFO as the main European UAP event of 2025.

## Post-PURSUE statement (May 9, 2026)

In Fortune/AP coverage of [[pursue]] Release 01 ([[src-fortune-aldrin-apollo-11-uap-2026-05]]), the Sol Foundation called for legislation mandating "thorough" review of classified UAP records "with the aim of providing Americans with the full truth about longstanding government knowledge and programs concerning technologies and vehicles **not of human origin**." The phrasing positions the foundation as a [[uap-disclosure-act|UAPDA]]-aligned voice in the post-PURSUE policy conversation.

# Enceladus Orbilander

[[nasa]] mission concept for [[enceladus]] combining an orbital phase (sampling the south polar [[enceladus-plumes|plumes]] from above) with a surface landing phase. Ranked as a top priority by the 2022 NASA planetary science decadal survey for development in the late 2030s. ([[src-cassini-huygens-ocean-worlds-2026-04]])

The mission's engineering brief was partially revised by Xu et al.'s 2026 *Nature Communications* finding that Enceladus-supplied plasma reshapes Saturn's magnetosphere, changing the radiation environment a future orbiter must design against.

Distinct from but adjacent to the [[esa]] Voyage 2050 Enceladus mission concept ([[src-esa-enceladus-life-mission-2025]]).

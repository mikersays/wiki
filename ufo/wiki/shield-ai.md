**Shield AI** is a San Diego-based U.S. defense AI / autonomy firm whose flagship product, [[hivemind]], is described as an "AI pilot" for autonomous aircraft and drone swarms. In May 2026 the Pentagon selected Shield AI to integrate Hivemind onto the [[lucas-drone|LUCAS]] one-way attack drone (Iranian clone first used in combat by [[centcom]] during [[operation-epic-fury]]); a capability demonstration is planned for fall 2026. Per Shield AI's May 19 press release, Hivemind will "enable groups of drones to coordinate, maneuver, and adapt together to changing conditions in real time, based on warfighter input."

Frame alongside [[anduril]] and [[perennial-autonomy]] as autonomy-stack vendors aligned with the [[drone-dominance]] / [[autonomous-warfare]] FY27 priority lines.

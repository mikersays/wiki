# JD Vance

50th Vice President of the United States, serving under [[donald-trump]] since January 2025. Self-described as "obsessed" with UFO files; has publicly pledged to investigate [[area-51]] and "get to the bottom of the UFO files" before the end of his term.

## UAP positions and statements

- **March 2026** — On podcaster [[benny-johnson]]'s show, Vance said: "I've still got three more years as vice president. I will get to the bottom of the UFO files." Also said he had been trying to find time to investigate [[area-51]] since taking office. ([[src-trump-pentagon-uap-files-2026-05]])
- **Christian-faith framing** — In the same interview, Vance suggested some UAP sightings reported as aliens may instead be "spiritual demons," casting his interest partly through a religious lens. ([[src-trump-pentagon-uap-files-2026-05]])

His advocacy aligns him with the Trump administration's broader [[uap-disclosure]] push but introduces a distinct theological angle absent from other senior officials in [[department-of-war]] or [[aaro]] coordination.

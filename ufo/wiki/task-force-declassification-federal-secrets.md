# Task Force on the Declassification of Federal Secrets

Subcommittee within the [[house-oversight-committee|House Oversight and Government Reform Committee]], chaired by Rep. [[anna-paulina-luna]] (R-FL). Functions as the primary congressional venue for UAP transparency hearings and whistleblower outreach.

## Activity

- **Sept 9, 2025** — Hosted "Restoring Public Trust Through UAP Transparency and Whistleblower Protection" hearing; witnesses included USAF veterans [[jeffrey-nuccetelli]] and [[dylan-borland]]. Discussion topics included [[aaro]] effectiveness. ([[src-uap-whistleblower-protection-act-2025-09]], [[src-uap-whistleblower-hearing-2025]])
- **Fall 2025** — Heard testimony from current and former service members on UAP encounters near U.S. military installations; one senior Navy officer described a 2023 California-coast Tic Tac sighting in which a glowing object emerged from the ocean and joined three similar objects before they sped away. ([[src-trump-pentagon-uap-files-2026-05]])
- **April 2026** — Continued hearings on UAP transparency, including the first active-duty Navy testimony from [[alexandro-wiggins]] and the Yemen orb Hellfire video released by [[eric-burlison]]. ([[src-uap-transparency-hearing-2026-04]])
- **April 29, 2026** — Chair Luna on [[miranda-devine]]'s *Pod Force One* says she has "observed things that are of nonhuman origin and creation" inside a SCIF; pledges a press conference upon declassification. Frames the work as Task Force advocacy for further [[uap-disclosure]] ([[src-luna-nonhuman-origin-pod-force-one-2026-04]]).
- **May 22–26, 2026** — Task Force member [[eric-burlison]] sent UAP records interrogatories to [[mitre|The MITRE Corporation]], an [[ffrdc]], demanding a classified briefing for Task Force members and invoking the FY2024 NDAA [[uap-records-collection]] ([[nara]] Record Group 615); builds on a prior [[mit-lincoln-laboratory]] letter. Extends Task Force pressure from agencies to private contractors. ([[src-burlison-mitre-press-release-2026-05]], [[src-burlison-mitre-uap-records-2026-05]])

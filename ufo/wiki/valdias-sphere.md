# Valdías Mountain Range Sphere

The **Valdías Mountain Range Sphere** (alt. *Las Baldías*) is a second metallic spherical object promoted by [[jaime-maussan]] in May 2026 as a sibling artifact to the [[buga-sphere]]. Per the May 10 2026 *No Humano* TV segment summarized in [[src-buga-sphere-maussan-tv-valdias-report-2026-05]], the sphere was recovered by [[william-zuniga|William Zúñiga]] from a remote site on the edge of a national park near **San Félix hill in the Antioquia / Medellín region of Colombia**, then physically delivered to Maussan in Mexico City. **All physical, electrical, and chain-of-custody claims are single-source and unverified.**

## Recovery account (per Maussan TV)

- **Trigger:** On March 15 2026 — during the [[buga-sphere|Buga Sphere]]'s reveal at Mexico City's National Auditorium — Zúñiga's Medellín team relayed (a) a daytime craft video over Medellín, broadcast live to ~8,000 attendees, and (b) a nighttime video of a luminous sphere descending into the Valdías range. A local couple corroborated the descent.
- **Expedition:** Zúñiga's second attempt succeeded after 12+ days in the jungle, navigating by compass with no cell signal. He carried the sphere out in a backpack, sustaining severe back pain, a broken tooth, and a split tongue while evading guerrilla and army checkpoints.
- **Handoff:** Zúñiga delivered the sphere to Maussan on-air in Mexico City.

## Site anomalies (claimed)

- Soil at "ground zero" allegedly emits electrical pulses on instrument readings.
- Five soil samples taken in a cross pattern, per engineer **Rodolfo Garrido**'s protocol.
- A white stone with parallel stripes and an unnatural 90° cut was also collected.

No named accredited laboratory, analyst, or methodology has been disclosed. Soil samples remain with the Medellín team.

## Release strategy

Contrasting the Buga rollout, Maussan announced he will **withhold individual findings** and only publish at the end of the full investigation, citing "haters and hackers." Planned platforms: *Interstellar*, *Tercer Milenio*, in-person appearances with [[william-zuniga|Zúñiga]], [[david-velez-el-potro|Vélez]], and Italian researcher Pier Giorgio Caría.

## Caveats

- All claims originate with Maussan's *No Humano* broadcast and Zúñiga's team; treat as **unverified, single-source**.
- The "29 black dots," "electrical-pulse soil," and physical-recovery hardships are described pro-Maussan; no independent press coverage of the recovery is known.
- Maussan's announced policy of not releasing intermediate findings reduces externally checkable evidence.
- Consistent with the pattern documented on [[jaime-maussan]] and [[buga-sphere]]: dramatic Latin-American artifact reveal, no peer review, named "experts" without institutional affiliation.

**National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC)** is a longstanding civilian clearinghouse for UFO/UAP sighting reports. NUFORC was a participant in [[aaro]]'s August 2025 UAP narrative-data workshop ([[src-aaro-uap-data-collection-whitepaper-2026-03]]), represented by CTO **Christian Stepien**, who presented on NUFORC's history on Day 2 and served on a panel addressing the harmonization of qualitative and quantitative UAP data.

NUFORC also published a public recap of the workshop.

See also: [[scu]], [[galileo-project]], [[uapx]], [[uap-reporting-infrastructure]].

# Venus Life Equation (VLE)

Framework introduced by [[noam-izenberg]] et al. (2021) for estimating the likelihood of extant life on [[venus]], structured as:

> **L = O × R × C**
>
> where O = origination probability, R = robustness/persistence under Venusian conditions, and C = continuity (sustained habitability over time).

The VLE is explicitly modeled on the [[drake-equation]] and shares its profound parameter uncertainty. Per [[src-venus-panspermia-earth-origin-2026-04]], a [[jhuapl]]/[[sandia-national-laboratories]] team applied the VLE in a 2026 LPSC paper combining bolide-fragmentation simulations to estimate Earth-to-Venus [[panspermia]] cell-transfer rates.

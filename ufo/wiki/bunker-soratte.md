The **Bunker Soratte** is a fascist-era underground military complex in San Oreste, Italy, with documented Vatican ties. The site was repurposed as a museum by mayor and museum founder Gregory Paolucci, who reportedly received a WWII-era Swiss Guard jacket from a member of Italian intelligence services when he established the museum.

The bunker appears in UAP discourse through filmmaker [[sam-sorich]]'s in-progress Catholic UAP documentary (see [[src-catholic-filmmaker-vatican-ufo-2026-04]]), which films at the site as an alleged study location for a 1933 Milan UFO crash referenced in [[david-grusch]]'s 2023 congressional testimony. Paolucci confirmed a "real" Vatican-bunker connection but says he cannot link it to the alleged UFO.

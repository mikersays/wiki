# Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory (JHUAPL)

Johns Hopkins University–affiliated [[ffrdc|federally funded research and development center]]; major NASA mission contributor (Parker Solar Probe, DART, MESSENGER) with planetary-science depth on [[venus]] and ocean worlds. Co-author institution on the 2026 LPSC Earth-to-Venus [[panspermia]] modeling study reported in [[src-venus-panspermia-earth-origin-2026-04]]; home of [[noam-izenberg]] and the [[venus-life-equation]] framework.

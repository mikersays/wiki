**Metallic orb** is an [[aaro]]-recognized spherical UAP shape class characterized by reflective / metallic-appearing spheres recurring across pilot reports in Iraq, Syria, Afghanistan, and other CENTCOM-area observations.

AARO's 2025 mission brief described a 2022 [[reaper-uas|MQ-9 Reaper]] electro-optical capture of a metallic orb whose characteristics were "consistent with other 'metallic orb' observations in the region." The metallic-orb pattern is one of the most well-attested recurring UAP shape classes in declassified DoD imagery and a focus area for [[pursue]] Release 02 previews ([[src-debrief-pursue-next-batch-preview-2026-05]]).

Distinguish from [[tic-tac-uap|Tic Tac]] (elongated/cigar-shaped) and from the broader [[transmedium-uap]] category.

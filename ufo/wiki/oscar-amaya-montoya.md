# Óscar Amaya Montoya

**Óscar Amaya Montoya** is the director of the **Fundación Casa de la Ciencia de Buga** (Buga Science House Foundation), a local science-education NGO in [[buga|Buga, Valle del Cauca, Colombia]]. He has discussed the [[buga-sphere]] publicly as part of community-facing outreach after the case ignited local UAP panic.

## Position on the Buga Sphere

Per [[src-buga-sphere-el-pais-cali-maussan-handoff-2025-10]] and [[src-buga-sphere-infobae-true-origin-2025-10]]:

- Describes the sphere as gray, ball-shaped, lacking visible welding seams, with engravings — but emphasizes "ufology has no scientific certainty."
- Considers it "probable that this is a recent technological artifact manufactured on Earth."
- Flags AI-era video fabrication as a plausible alternative explanation for circulating sphere clips.

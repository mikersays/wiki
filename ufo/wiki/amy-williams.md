# Amy Williams

Professor of geological sciences at the University of Florida and a member of both the [[curiosity-rover]] and [[perseverance-rover]] science teams.

Lead author of the April 2026 *Nature Communications* paper on the [[sample-analysis-at-mars|SAM]]/TMAH detection of more than 20 [[organic-molecules-mars|organic molecules]] in [[gale-crater|Gale Crater]]'s Glen Torridon clays — including a nitrogen-bearing DNA-precursor-like compound and benzothiophene ([[src-curiosity-mars-life-molecules-2026-04]]).

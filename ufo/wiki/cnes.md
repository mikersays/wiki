**CNES** (Centre National d'Études Spatiales) is the French national space agency. CNES is the parent organization of [[geipan]], France's official UAP investigation office, since GEIPAN's 1977 establishment.

CNES is an institutional supporter of the June 29, 2026 [[french-national-assembly|National Assembly]] UAP symposium ([[src-france-national-assembly-uap-conference-2026-05]]).

# Marik Von Rennenkampff

**Marik Von Rennenkampff** is a former US Department of State analyst who writes about [[uap-disclosure]]. In this vault he appears as a public-readiness commentator on [[donald-trump]]'s February 2026 disclosure directive.

## UAP positions

- In [[src-newsnation-elizondo-pandoras-box-2026-02]] (Feb 21, 2026), Von Rennenkampff argued the public has reached "**critical mass**" on UAP and that Trump's timing is "perfect." He framed the **day-after problem** — "what happens the day after — geopolitically, economically, socially and culturally" — as the central open question for any large-scale disclosure event.

**Los Alamos National Laboratory** (LANL) is a U.S. Department of Energy national laboratory in northern New Mexico. It is the heritage institution for the Mars rover [[supercam]] LIBS instrument and hosted follow-on calibration work for the 2026 [[neretva-vallis]] nickel-rich iron sulfide quantification (see [[src-perseverance-neretva-nickel-rocks-2026-04]]).

**MIT Lincoln Laboratory** is a [[ffrdc|federally funded research and development center]] operated by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology for the U.S. Department of Defense, focused on advanced technology in areas such as air defense, sensors, and space surveillance.

## Burlison UAP-records letter

Before his May 2026 inquiry to [[mitre|The MITRE Corporation]], Rep. [[eric-burlison]] sent MIT Lincoln Laboratory a letter concerning historical UAP-related records tied to the **Beacon Hill Study** and a reel-to-reel recording labeled **"AF-ATIC-FILM, 03/52."** The letter asked the lab to identify, preserve, digitize, and coordinate transfer of responsive government-funded UAP records to [[nara|NARA]] for the [[uap-records-collection|UAP Records Collection]] ([[src-burlison-mitre-press-release-2026-05]]).

Context: "AF-ATIC" references the Air Force Air Technical Intelligence Center, an early-1950s USAF intelligence body in the lineage of [[project-blue-book|Project Blue Book]]-era UFO investigation; the Beacon Hill Study was a 1952 MIT-convened classified air-defense / reconnaissance study. The Lincoln Laboratory letter established the FFRDC-accountability template that Burlison then extended to MITRE.

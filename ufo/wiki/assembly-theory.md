# Assembly Theory

Framework developed by Lee Cronin (Glasgow) and Sara Imari Walker (ASU) that uses **molecular construction complexity** — specifically the minimum number of recursive steps required to build a molecule — as an **agnostic biosignature**. The core claim is that above a threshold assembly index, molecules are unlikely to form abiotically and indicate selection / construction by an information-bearing process (life).

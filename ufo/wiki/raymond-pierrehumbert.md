**Raymond Pierrehumbert** is a planetary-atmosphere theorist and Halley Professor of Physics at the [[university-of-oxford|University of Oxford]]. He co-authored the 2026 [[l-98-59-d]] [[magma-ocean-world|magma-ocean]] study ([[src-l-98-59-d-sulphur-magma-world-2026-05]]) led by [[harrison-nicholls]]. Pierrehumbert framed the work as a demonstration that coupled atmosphere–interior modelling can reconstruct the deep history of planets humans will never visit, from mass, radius, and atmospheric data alone.

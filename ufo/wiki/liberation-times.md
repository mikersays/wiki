**Liberation Times** is an independent UAP-focused news outlet that has broken several stories on the 2026 [[uap-disclosure]] effort, including the April 2026 piece confirming [[aaro]] and the White House were coordinating release of previously unseen UAP material ([[src-war-department-uap-release-2026-04]]).

Key reporter: [[christopher-sharp]] — author of the December 14, 2025 investigation [[src-uap-transparency-stalls-2025-12]] documenting the FY2026 NDAA exclusion of the [[uap-disclosure-act]], the [[four-corners-ndaa-process|"Four Corners"]] precondition, and uncorroborated source claims about Chinese anti-gravitic [[drone-incursions]] and [[matthew-livelsberger]]'s classified manifesto.

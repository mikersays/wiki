# Fabian Klenner

Planetary scientist at [[uc-riverside]]; co-author with [[gideon-yoffe]] of the May 2026 *Nature Astronomy* paper proposing a statistical [[agnostic-biosignature]] method based on amino-acid and fatty-acid richness/evenness ([[src-alien-biology-detection-method-2026-05]]). Previously known for mass-spectrometry analysis of icy grains from ocean-world plumes (Enceladus, Europa), connecting his work to [[ocean-worlds]] biosignature detection.

## Key contributions

- Statistical-diversity biosignature framework (2026): biological samples show more diverse and evenly distributed amino-acid pools than abiotic ones; fatty acids follow the inverse pattern.
- Method validated across ~100 datasets including microbes, soils, fossilized dinosaur eggshells, [[bennu-asteroid|Bennu]] and Murchison meteorites, asteroids, and lab-synthesis abiotics.
- Instrument-agnostic: works on data from existing mission payloads.

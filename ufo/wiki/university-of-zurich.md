# University of Zurich

Swiss research university. Hosts the planetary-interior modeling work of [[ravit-helled]] and [[simon-muller]], who contributed the [[toi-5205-b]] interior models reported in [[src-sciencedaily-forbidden-exoplanet-atmosphere-2026-04]]. Their models imply the bulk planet is ~100× more metal-rich than the visible atmosphere — interpreted as inward heavy-element migration and the cessation of interior–atmosphere mixing.

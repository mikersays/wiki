# Brandi Vincent

**Brandi Vincent** is a reporter for DefenseScoop who covers UAP/UFO policy, the Pentagon, and defense technology. She is a primary source for reporting on [[aaro]], congressional UAP hearings, and [[uap-disclosure]] developments.

## Coverage in This Wiki

Vincent is the author (or co-author) of six source documents ingested from DefenseScoop:

- [[src-uap-whistleblower-hearing-2025]] — September 2025 congressional UAP hearing
- [[src-uap-ndaa-fy2026-intercept-provisions]] — FY2026 NDAA UAP provisions (December 2025)
- [[src-uap-trump-disclosure-2026]] — Trump disclosure directive (February 2026)
- [[src-uap-aaro-research-workshop-2025]] — AARO research workshop (reported March 2026)
- [[src-alien-gov-domains-2026-03]] — White House alien.gov / aliens.gov domain registrations (with Madison Alder)
- [[src-northcom-drone-incursion-2026-03]] — NORTHCOM Flyaway Kit drone defeat during Operation Epic Fury
- [[src-aaro-uap-data-collection-whitepaper-2026-03]] — AARO's 2025 UAP narrative-data workshop whitepaper (companion to the 2026 DefenseScoop reveal)
- [[src-uap-whistleblower-protection-act-2025-09]] — September 3, 2025 preview of the H.R. 5060 introduction and the September 9 House Oversight task-force hearing

She broke the story of [[aaro]]'s invite-only research workshop in August 2025 ([[src-uap-aaro-research-workshop-2025]]) and the subsequent AARO whitepaper.

# Valdías (or Las Baldías) Mountain Range Sphere: Summary of Report from Maussan TV (10th May 2026)

The transcript is an excerpt from a Spanish-language TV segment "No Humano" hosted by Jaime Maussan ("Don Jaime"). It discusses the Buga sphere (a previously presented object claimed to be of non-human origin) and introduces a new/second sphere recovered from Colombia.

**Background on the Buga Sphere**

* Presented with complete authenticity on the show.

The New Sphere: Valdías (or Las Baldías) Mountain Range Sphere

* Location: Recovered from the Valdías / Las Baldías mountain range (near the edge of a national park), associated with San Félix hill in the Medellín area, Colombia. A high-risk zone due to guerrilla control and a nearby army high-mountain base.

Discovery Context:

* Triggered by recent UFO/UAP activity reported by William's team.
* Daytime video: A craft in broad daylight over Medellín that turned and disappeared (broadcast live at the Mexico City event to ~8,000 people).
* Nighttime video: Luminous sphere over San Félix hill showing bright flashes (as if in trouble), descending/disappearing into the mountain.
* Local witnesses (a couple) saw the same intense light (not a helicopter), illuminating vegetation, descending to the summit, and not re-emerging. They waited ~2 hours.

Recovery Expedition:

* Led by William Suúñiga / Zúñiga (head of an "international UFO hunters" organization with presence in 21+ countries, based in Medellín; they have documented 300+ videos of UAP/"wild animals" patterns exiting via San Félix hill toward Valdías).
* Duration: Over 12 days deep in the jungle, guided primarily by compass (no cell signal). Second attempt succeeded.
* Challenges: Health complications, extreme physical toll (carried the sphere in a bag/backpack causing severe back pain), broken tooth that split his tongue, fear of authorities/guerrillas (risk of being shot or kidnapped for ransom). Public order issues in Colombia at the time complicated travel/checkpoints.
* Advised against by friends including Piero Caría / Pier Giorgio Caría (who called him "crazy" and said he'd never find it; Caría participated in Maussan's Disclosure Project event in Washington).
* Assisted/participated: David Vélez "El Potro" (traveling to Mexico City for follow-up appearances).

Physical Description and Comparison to Buga Sphere:

* Similar overall appearance (metallic sphere).
* Differences: Less damaged; equator with 29 black dots (vs. Buga's 31); no visible proto-Sanskrit message on the exterior (possibly inside, per David Vélez); "hard aluminum"; natural-looking "belt" attachment; a hole with no continuity (analyzed on video).
* Video shown of the recovery moment (recorded by "William the old man" or team); includes tests, soil sampling, and comments on differences.

* Ground Zero soil: Emits electrical pulses (tested with equipment; soil samples taken in a cross pattern per engineer Rodolfo Garrido's instructions). Five samples collected; now with the team in Medellín for further study. Ground "retains energy" in an unexplained way.
* Nearby: A curious white stone with stripes and an unusual 90-degree cut, collected as well.

# Sky Map (Ukrainian C-UAS platform)

Ukrainian-developed [[counter-uas|counter-unmanned-aerial-system]] command-and-control platform. Synthesizes data from radars and sensors and presents a dashboard of maps and live video feeds to detect incoming drone threats.

In April 2026, Sky Map became the first known Ukrainian counter-drone technology to be operationally deployed by U.S. forces — at [[prince-sultan-air-base]] in Saudi Arabia, with Ukrainian trainers — to plug a gap during [[operation-epic-fury]] after Iranian and Iranian-proxy [[shahed-drone|drones]] caused U.S. casualties and damaged high-value aircraft ([[src-epic-fury-update-2026-04]]). Pentagon officials described U.S. counter-drone capability as "lagging."

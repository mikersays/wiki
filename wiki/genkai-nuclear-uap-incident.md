The **Genkai Nuclear Power Plant UAP Incident** is an ongoing Japanese UAP investigation cited by the [[japan-uap-parliamentary-group]] as part of the rationale for establishing a specialized government UAP body ([[src-japan-uap-strategy-proposal-2026-03]]).

The parliamentary group identified **"irreconcilable discrepancies"** between:

- **Kyushu Electric Power Company**'s records of the event
- The **Saga Prefectural Police**'s official explanation

The case fits within the broader [[nuclear-uap-correlation]] pattern — unexplained aerial activity appearing near nuclear infrastructure — and is structurally analogous to U.S. incidents cited in FY2026 [[ndaa-uap-provisions|NDAA]] debates.

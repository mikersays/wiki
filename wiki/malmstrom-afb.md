**Malmstrom Air Force Base** is a U.S. Air Force installation in Great Falls, Montana, home to the 341st Missile Wing, which operates approximately 150 Minuteman III intercontinental ballistic missiles — one of the primary land-based legs of the U.S. [[nuclear-triad]].

## UAP and Intelligence Incidents

- **1967**: [[robert-salas]] claims a UAP incident at Malmstrom temporarily disabled multiple nuclear-armed Minuteman ICBMs under his watch — one of the foundational cases in the [[nuclear-uap-correlation]] pattern.
- **2023**: The Chinese spy balloon loitered 24–48 hours over the 341st Missile Wing's ICBM silos, maneuvered via Iridium satellite, and reportedly exfiltrated data through U.S. ISPs. A 75-page exploitation report exists but has not been publicly released. (see [[src-spy-drones-nuclear-triad-2026-04]])
- **2024–2026**: Multiple unidentified drone swarm incidents reported over Malmstrom's missile silos and launch control centers; launch points undiscovered, no operators apprehended.

According to [[stephen-bryen]]'s analysis (see [[src-spy-drones-nuclear-triad-2026-04]]), Malmstrom is part of a documented Chinese intelligence campaign targeting all three legs of the nuclear triad.

# RAF Mildenhall

Royal Air Force base in Suffolk, England, operating as a U.S. Air Force station under [[usafe-afa]]. Hosts the **100th Air Refueling Wing** (KC-135 Stratotankers) and is a key forward node for U.S. air operations in Europe.

Targeted in the November 2024 [[drone-incursions|UAP/sUAS incursion]] wave alongside [[raf-lakenheath]] and [[raf-feltwell]] ([[src-uk-mod-uap-no-threat-2024]]).

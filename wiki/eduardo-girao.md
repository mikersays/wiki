**Eduardo Girão** is a Brazilian Senator who organized and presided over the June 24, 2022 Brazilian Senate UAP hearing — one of the first formal national-legislature UAP hearings outside the United States (see [[src-brazil-uap-senate-hearings-2026]]).

Girão invited commanders of Brazil's three armed forces to participate, though the invitation was declined. The hearing drew domestic researchers, a former federal representative, two Brazilian Air Force observers, and international guests including [[gary-heseltine]] and [[robert-salas]].

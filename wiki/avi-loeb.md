**Avi Loeb** is a Harvard astrophysicist and founder of the [[galileo-project]], a scientific initiative searching for extraterrestrial technological artifacts.

## On UAP disclosure (2026)

Per [[src-trump-uap-files-delay-2026-03]], Loeb argues the real "gold mine" of any U.S. government UAP release would be high-resolution satellite imagery of UAP events — capable of identifying unfamiliar objects and measuring their speed — plus any material recovery details from alleged crash sites.

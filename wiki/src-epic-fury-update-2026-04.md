## Summary

SOF News status update on [[operation-epic-fury]] as of April 24, 2026. The planned Islamabad ceasefire meeting collapsed when Iran declined to attend; [[donald-trump|President Trump]] extended the ceasefire indefinitely while keeping the U.S. naval blockade of Iranian ports intact. Three sanctioned tankers have been seized by U.S. forces; in retaliation [[irgc-navy]] small craft seized two tankers in the [[strait-of-hormuz]] on April 22. The administration calls this the "economic phase" of the campaign. Most relevant to the wiki's [[drone-incursions]] thread: the Pentagon has imported **Ukrainian counter-drone technology** ([[sky-map-c-uas|"Sky Map" C2 platform]]) to [[prince-sultan-air-base|Prince Sultan Air Base]] in Saudi Arabia after suffering casualties and high-value-aircraft damage from Iranian-proxy drones — an explicit admission that U.S. [[counter-uas|counter-drone capability]] is "lagging."

## Key Claims

- Iran did not appear at the April 22 Islamabad ceasefire talks; Trump extended the pause unilaterally pending an Iranian proposal.
- U.S. naval blockade has redirected 33 ships; M/T Majestic X is third vessel boarded this week. M/V Touska was seized April 19 by Marines from USS Tripoli (LHA 7) after USS Spruance disabled its propulsion.
- IRGC Navy seized Liberian-flagged Epaminondas and Panama-flagged MSC Francesca on April 22 in the Strait of Hormuz.
- Three aircraft carriers now in CENTCOM AOR; USS George H.W. Bush (CVN-77) operating in the Indian Ocean as of April 23.
- U.S. military "lagging" on counter-drone defense; Ukrainian **Sky Map** C2 platform plus Ukrainian trainers deployed at Prince Sultan AB to plug the gap.
- UAE Embassy reports UAE absorbed 2,819 Iranian missile/drone attacks Feb 28-Apr 14 — the most of any nation, ahead of Israel (1,357).
- [[csis|CSIS]] estimates U.S. has expended 850/3,100 Tomahawks, 1,000/4,400 JASSMs, 290/360 THAAD interceptors, and up to 1,430/2,330 Patriots.
- Iran has placed mines in the Strait of Hormuz; U.S. Avenger-class minehunters are transiting from Japan via Sri Lanka.
- New Iranian supreme leader [[mojtaba-khamenei]] is in hiding, reportedly gravely injured; IRGC commanders effectively running diplomacy/war decisions.
- Retired BG [[donald-bolduc]] publishes critique that Epic Fury produced tactical wins but exposed strategic gaps and an exit-strategy void.
- Israel-Lebanon ceasefire extended three weeks from April 23 via White House talks.

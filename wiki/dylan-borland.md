**Dylan Borland** is a USAF veteran and UAP witness who testified at the September 9, 2025 House Oversight hearing on [[uap-whistleblower-protections]] (see [[src-uap-transparency-hearing-2026-04]]).

## Key Testimony

Borland described a 2012 UAP experience at [[langley-afb]] and alleged more than a decade of subsequent professional retaliation. He stated his career was "deliberately obstructed" due to his knowledge of legacy UAP programs, underscoring the documented pattern of [[uap-stigma]] within military institutions.

**Picatinny Arsenal** is a U.S. Army advanced-weapons research and development installation in northern New Jersey. The arsenal experienced 11 confirmed drone sightings in December 2024 that helped ignite the widely publicized New Jersey drone wave, per [[src-60-minutes-drone-swarms-2025|CBS 60 Minutes]].

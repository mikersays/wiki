**Joe Spielberger** is senior policy counsel at the **Project on Government Oversight (POGO)**, a nonprofit watchdog organization focused on government transparency and accountability.

## Key Testimony

Testified at the September 9, 2025 House Oversight hearing on UAP transparency ([[src-uap-transparency-hearing-2026-04]]) in favor of strengthened disclosure channels and [[uap-whistleblower-protections]]. Argued that national security misconduct disclosures "often have a direct impact on public safety, civil liberties and the security of our country."

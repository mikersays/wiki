**Gary Heseltine** is a former British police detective and vice president of the [[icer|International Coalition for Extraterrestrial Research (ICER)]]. He spoke at the June 2022 Brazilian Senate UAP hearing (see [[src-brazil-uap-senate-hearings-2026]]) on the global character of the UAP issue, connecting international cases to the broader [[uap-disclosure]] movement.

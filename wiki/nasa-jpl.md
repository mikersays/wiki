# NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL)

NASA Federally Funded Research and Development Center managed by Caltech in Pasadena, California. Designs, builds, and operates many of NASA's robotic interplanetary missions.

Operates flight operations for [[curiosity-rover]] and [[perseverance-rover]] on Mars, the [[europa-clipper]] mission to Jupiter's moon Europa, and many other missions. Closely associated with [[nasa]] and complementary to [[nasa-goddard]] (which leads many heliophysics, astrophysics, and instrument-development programs, including [[sample-analysis-at-mars|SAM]] for Curiosity).

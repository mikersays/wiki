# Coyote Interceptor

Family of Raytheon (RTX) kinetic [[counter-uas|counter-drone interceptors]] (Block 2/3 variants), originally derived from a target-drone airframe and now fielded as the U.S. military's primary precision hard-kill effector against Group 1–3 sUAS, including [[shahed-drone|Shahed-class]] one-way attack drones.

[[tom-karako]] of [[csis]] suspects Coyote — alongside the Merops optical-tracking system — has done "the lion's share of the drone killing" in [[centcom]] during [[operation-epic-fury]] ([[src-counter-drone-middle-east-jiatf-2026-04]]).

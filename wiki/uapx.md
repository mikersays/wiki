**UAPx** is a civilian UAP field-research organization. UAPx was one of the participating organizations in [[aaro]]'s August 2025 UAP narrative-data workshop ([[src-aaro-uap-data-collection-whitepaper-2026-03]]), alongside [[nuforc]], [[scu]], [[galileo-project]], and the National Archives — representing the breadth of civilian-science partners now engaging with the federal UAP investigation apparatus.

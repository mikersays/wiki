**Minot Air Force Base** is a USAF installation in North Dakota that hosts nuclear-capable B-52 bombers and Minuteman III ICBMs under [[us-stratcom|U.S. Strategic Command]]. Minot's nuclear mission makes it one of the most nationally sensitive domestic military sites.

In October 2025, NORTHCOM certified its [[flyaway-kit|Flyaway Kit (FAK)]] counter-drone package, operators, and processes at Minot during STRATCOM's **Global Thunder** exercise ([[src-northcom-drone-incursion-2026-03]]).

Minot is relevant to the [[nuclear-uap-correlation]] pattern of UAP activity near nuclear sites.

The **Very Large Telescope (VLT)** is the European Southern Observatory's flagship optical/infrared facility at Paranal Observatory, Chile. It comprises four 8.2 m Unit Telescopes and four 1.8 m Auxiliary Telescopes whose light can be combined interferometrically. The VLT hosts numerous instruments relevant to exoplanet science, including [[espresso]] for [[radial-velocity]] measurements.

The VLT contributed ESPRESSO data to the 2026 confirmation of [[gj-887-d]] ([[src-gj-887-d-habitability-2026-03]]).

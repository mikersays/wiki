**Grayce C. Brown** is a research assistant at the [[seti-institute]] and co-author of the 2026 Astrophysical Journal paper on [[stellar-plasma-scattering]] of radio [[technosignatures]] (see [[src-plasma-technosignature-scattering-2026]]). She argued that [[seti]] search designs should match what actually arrives at Earth — accounting for near-source plasma broadening — rather than optimizing only for ideally transmitted razor-thin signals.

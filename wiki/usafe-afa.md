# U.S. Air Forces in Europe – Air Forces Africa (USAFE-AFA)

U.S. Air Force major command headquartered at Ramstein Air Base, Germany. Responsible for USAF forces and operations across Europe, Africa, the Middle East (in coordination with [[centcom]]), and the Arctic.

## UAP reporting

USAFE-AFA's **48th Fighter Wing** at [[raf-lakenheath]] publicly reported multiple [[drone-incursions|UAP/sUAS incursions]] over [[raf-lakenheath]], [[raf-mildenhall]], and [[raf-feltwell]] in late November 2024, with [[raf-fairford]] added days later. The disclosures contributed to political pressure on the UK MoD, which responded in December 2024 with [[luke-pollard]]'s "no military threat" statement ([[src-uk-mod-uap-no-threat-2024]]).

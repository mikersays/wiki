# RAF Feltwell

Royal Air Force station in Norfolk, England, operating as a USAF support installation under [[usafe-afa]]; primarily provides housing and base-support functions for personnel at [[raf-lakenheath]] and [[raf-mildenhall]].

Targeted in the November 2024 [[drone-incursions|UAP/sUAS incursion]] wave reported by the 48th Fighter Wing ([[src-uk-mod-uap-no-threat-2024]]).

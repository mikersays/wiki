**Wright-Patterson Air Force Base** is a USAF installation near Dayton, Ohio. Originating from 1917 Army aviation facilities sited near the Wright brothers, it hosts the [[air-force-research-laboratory]] and the [[nasic|National Air and Space Intelligence Center]].

## Connection to UAP lore

Wright-Patterson is central to U.S. UFO mythology, long rumored to be the destination of debris and bodies from the 1947 [[roswell-incident]] — including the legendary "Hangar 18" and the base's former **Foreign Technology Division**. [[lue-elizondo]] has described the base as "the center of gravity" for U.S. government "spooky" R&D.

Rep. Jared Moskowitz (D-FL) publicly called for congressional access to Wright-Patterson, Dugway Proving Ground, and Groom Lake at a 2023 hearing.

# Breakthrough Listen

**Breakthrough Listen** is the world's most comprehensive **technosignature** search program, part of the Breakthrough Initiatives founded by **Yuri Milner**. Headquartered at **Oxford University**, with Dr. Andrew Siemion as Principal Investigator.

## Survey Scope

- **1 million nearby stars**
- The **entire galactic plane** of the Milky Way
- **100 nearby galaxies**

## Telescope Partners

- **Allen Telescope Array** (Hat Creek, California)
- **Green Bank Telescope** (West Virginia)
- **Parkes Observatory** (Australia)

## AI Detection Breakthrough (2025)

According to [[src-seti-ai-breakthrough-listen-2025]], Breakthrough Listen partnered with NVIDIA to develop a deep learning system achieving **600x speed improvement** in detecting Fast Radio Bursts and anomalous signals. The system:

- Processes data **160x faster than real-time** (previously 4x slower)
- Reduces false positives by ~10x
- Improves detection accuracy by 7%
- Uses the **NVIDIA Holoscan platform** to bypass traditional dedispersion techniques

This transforms [[seti]] from retrospective analysis into real-time monitoring.

## Key Personnel

- **Dr. Andrew Siemion**: Principal Investigator, Oxford University
- **Peter Ma**: Led the AI research (University of Toronto / UC Berkeley)

## Parallel Efforts

The 2023 VLA + MeerKAT deep-target search on [[k2-18b]] (reported 2026, [[src-k2-18b-technosignature-null-2026]]) is a parallel [[technosignatures]] campaign using different facilities. Its filtering pipeline is portable to Listen's hycean-world targets.

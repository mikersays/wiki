# RAF Lakenheath

Royal Air Force base in Suffolk, England, operating as the home of the U.S. Air Force's **48th Fighter Wing** under [[usafe-afa]]. Currently fields F-15E Strike Eagles and F-35A Lightning IIs. Reported to host U.S. nuclear weapons storage (B61 gravity bombs) — making it a high-value target in the [[nuclear-uap-correlation|nuclear-UAP correlation]] thread.

## November 2024 incursions

In late November 2024, the 48th Fighter Wing publicly reported multiple UAP/sUAS incursions over Lakenheath, [[raf-mildenhall]], and [[raf-feltwell]]. The disclosure triggered widespread concern over UK base security and contributed to the political pressure that yielded the December 2024 [[ministry-of-defence|MoD]] statement reaffirming a "no military threat" posture ([[src-uk-mod-uap-no-threat-2024]]).

The UK incidents are part of the [[drone-incursions|broader transatlantic incursion pattern]] that includes the [[langley-afb]] December 2023 swarm, the [[barksdale-afb]] March 2026 waves, and other [[us-stratcom|nuclear-related]] sites.

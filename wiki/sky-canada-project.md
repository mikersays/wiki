# Sky Canada Project

The **Sky Canada Project** is a report released in July 2025 by Canada's Office of the Chief Science Advisor. It represents the most comprehensive national UAP framework outside the United States and the first time a major U.S. ally produced a formal, government-backed framework for systematic UAP investigation.

## Key Recommendations

- Establishment of a dedicated UAP office under the **Canadian Space Agency**
- Development of a bilingual (English/French) public reporting app
- Creation of a standardized database for UAP reports

## Significance

The project signals that UAP investigation is gaining international traction beyond the United States. It contributes to global [[uap-reporting-infrastructure]] and provides a model for other nations developing their own frameworks. Its government backing also helps erode [[uap-stigma]] at the international level.

# UFO Secret Files, Drone Swarms and Nuclear-Linked Sightings Stun Experts in 2025

## Summary

A Fox News year-end roundup surveying the major UFO/UAP developments of 2025. The article covers scientific discoveries linking UAP activity to nuclear test sites, military encounters, underwater object clusters, and the documentary "The Age of Disclosure." It portrays 2025 as a year of significant escalation in both the quantity and quality of UAP evidence.

## Key Claims

- Scientists at **Palomar Observatory** analyzed archival photographs (1949-1957) and found transient phenomena were **45% more likely to appear within 24 hours of a nuclear test**, published in Nature's Scientific Reports (October 20, 2025). This supports the [[nuclear-uap-correlation]].
- [[americans-for-safe-aerospace]] reported over 700 UAP reports in 2025, more than doubling from ~300 in 2024.
- [[aaro]] catalogued over 2,400 UAP cases as of early March 2026, with roughly 171 remaining officially unexplained.
- The Enigma app documented over 9,000 **unidentified submersible object** (USO) sightings within 10 miles of U.S. shorelines since August 2025, with California and Florida showing highest concentrations.
- During a September 2025 House hearing, Rep. Eric Burlison presented video of an MQ-9 Reaper drone striking an unidentified orb off Yemen (October 2024) with a Hellfire missile; the orb reportedly continued flying unharmed.
- A UK police helicopter near RAF Lakenheath took emergency evasive action from two fast-moving craft (November 22, 2024); the Airprox Board attributed them to F-15 lights, a conclusion disputed by observers.
- Multiple witnesses in Colorado reported orbs near a Lockheed Martin facility (January 12, 2025).
- Multiple pilots in Fresno, California reported unexplained sightings including an object following an aircraft at 3,500 feet (January 2025).

## Documentary: The Age of Disclosure

"The Age of Disclosure" (2025) features 34 senior U.S. officials discussing what the film characterizes as an "80-year global cover-up." Includes a statement from Marco Rubio: "We've had repeated instances of something operating in the airspace over restricted nuclear facilities."

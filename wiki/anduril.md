**Anduril Industries** is a U.S. defense technology company focused on autonomous systems, counter-UAS, and AI-driven sensing. Its **Anvil** drone interceptor is part of the NORTHCOM [[flyaway-kit|Flyaway Kit]] loadout used in the March 2026 drone defeat during [[operation-epic-fury]] ([[src-northcom-drone-incursion-2026-03]]).

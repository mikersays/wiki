# Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS)

Bipartisan defense and foreign-policy think tank in Washington, DC. Hosts the **Missile Defense Project**, directed by [[tom-karako]], which is a leading public source of analysis on U.S. air- and missile-defense posture.

## Recent UAP/defense relevance

- April 2026 CSIS estimates of U.S. munitions expenditure during [[operation-epic-fury]]: ~850/3,100 Tomahawks, 1,000/4,400 JASSMs, 290/360 THAAD interceptors, up to 1,430/2,330 Patriots ([[src-epic-fury-update-2026-04]]).
- CSIS commentary (Karako) on the post-Iran-war [[counter-uas|C-UAS]] surge by [[jiatf-401]], including the line that the [[drone-incursions|drone problem]] has been "artificially divided" between overseas combat and homeland threats ([[src-counter-drone-middle-east-jiatf-2026-04]]).

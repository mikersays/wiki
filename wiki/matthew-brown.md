# Matthew Brown

**Matthew Brown** is a former Pentagon intelligence analyst who became the first government insider to publicly confirm the existence of [[immaculate-constellation]] in April 2025.

## Background

- **Policy Advisor** for the Office of the Secretary of Defense
- **Technical Advisor** for the Office of the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence & Security (OUSD(I&S))
- **Program Advisor** for the Department of State
- Holder of **Top Secret/Sensitive Compartmented Information (TS/SCI)** security clearance

## Discovery of Immaculate Constellation

Brown discovered [[immaculate-constellation]] accidentally while managing files on a Pentagon server. He encountered a **classified 2018 Schriever War Game document** containing program details and UAP imagery. The document included:

- References to Russian naval awareness of UAP near the Kamchatka Peninsula
- Global UAP imagery from multiple sensor platforms
- Evidence of cross-national UAP tracking

## Public Disclosure

Appeared on-camera in a multi-part interview series by Jeremy Corbell and George Knapp titled "Immaculate Constellation -- UFO Whistleblower Goes Public."

Brown was measured in his claims, stating: "Nothing in those videos proves extraterrestrial or non-human... certainly anomalous, exotic, and unexplainable."

On the personal risk: "My hope is that the stakes are not paid out. But they are life imprisonment and the possibility of execution."

## Significance

Brown's testimony reinforces [[david-grusch]]'s claims about [[crash-retrieval-programs]] while focusing on the surveillance and data-collection side, suggesting a larger ecosystem of classified UAP activities. His senior credentials place him within the upper echelons of Pentagon intelligence.

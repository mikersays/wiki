**The War Zone** (twz.com) is a defense-focused news outlet known for investigative reporting on military aviation, emerging weapons, and anomalous aerial events. The outlet first documented the multi-year pattern of unexplained drone incursions over U.S. military and nuclear sites — including the December 2023 [[langley-afb|Langley]] swarm, the 2019 [[palo-verde-nuclear-plant|Palo Verde]] incursion, and the March 2026 [[barksdale-afb|Barksdale]] waves. Reporter Howard Altman has led much of the coverage.

The War Zone's reporting is routinely cited by mainstream outlets (including [[src-60-minutes-drone-swarms-2025|CBS 60 Minutes]]) as the primary open-source chronicle of homeland C-sUAS events.

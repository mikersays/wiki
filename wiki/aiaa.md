# AIAA (American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics)

The **American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics** is the world's largest technical society dedicated to aerospace. Its involvement in UAP investigation lends significant professional engineering credibility to the field.

## Role in UAP Investigation

- Established the **Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena Integration Committee**, chaired by [[ryan-graves]]
- Endorsed Rep. Robert Garcia's **Safe Airspace for Americans Act** (H.R. 5231) in September 2025
- Developing reporting standards and peer-reviewed research protocols for UAP investigation
- Provides a framework for standardized data collection and analysis, advancing [[uap-scientific-study]]

## Significance

AIAA's endorsement of UAP-related legislation and its creation of a dedicated UAP committee represent the aerospace engineering establishment formally backing UAP research. This is a major factor in reducing [[uap-stigma]] around the topic.

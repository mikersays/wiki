**Maj. Gen. William Neil McCasland (USAF, ret.)** is a retired U.S. Air Force general officer who commanded the [[air-force-research-laboratory]] at [[wright-patterson-afb]] before retiring c. 2013.

In early 2026, McCasland disappeared from his Albuquerque home. He had reportedly experienced "mental fog" and stepped down from groups in the days before. Authorities recovered a shirt and hiking boots but not his wallet, revolver, or backpack ([[src-wright-patterson-general-missing-2026-03]]).

Officials say there is no evidence linking his disappearance to UFO research. The coincidence nonetheless revived public speculation given Wright-Patterson's decades-long entanglement with [[crash-retrieval-programs]] lore.

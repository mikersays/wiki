**Yoshiharu Asakawa** is a former member of the Japanese Diet. In **May 2025** he submitted a proposal to the Defense Minister to establish a specialized UAP department within the Japanese government. The proposal received no positive response.

His effort nonetheless laid groundwork for the current [[japan-uap-parliamentary-group]], whose March 2026 proposal ([[src-japan-uap-strategy-proposal-2026-03]]) adopts a similar aim but places the proposed body under the Cabinet Secretariat rather than the Defense Ministry — a broader, whole-of-government framing.

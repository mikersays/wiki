**National Air and Space Intelligence Center (NASIC)** is the U.S. Air Force intelligence center at [[wright-patterson-afb]] that analyzes foreign air and space systems. NASIC is the successor to the base's former **Foreign Technology Division**.

Marik Von Rennenkampff told CNN that NASIC is the alleged destination for retrieved UAP materials — "where those retrieved materials were ultimately taken for research and study" ([[src-wright-patterson-general-missing-2026-03]]). This claim remains unverified and is a legacy UFO-lore assertion rather than an AARO-confirmed fact.

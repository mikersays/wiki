# NASA Goddard Space Flight Center

NASA's heliophysics, astrophysics, and Earth-science research center in Greenbelt, Maryland. Goddard leads many instrument-development efforts for missions operated by [[nasa-jpl]].

Goddard hosts the [[sample-analysis-at-mars|SAM]] instrument-team leadership for [[curiosity-rover]], including astrobiologist [[jennifer-eigenbrode]]. Goddard scientists also conduct biosignature-survival research relevant to [[mars-sample-return]] and [[biosignatures]] interpretation.

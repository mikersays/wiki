**U.S. Strategic Command (STRATCOM)** oversees the U.S. nuclear arsenal and related global strike, missile defense integration, and space operations.

STRATCOM runs the annual **Global Thunder** nuclear-enterprise exercise. NORTHCOM used Global Thunder 2025 at [[minot-afb]] to certify its first [[flyaway-kit|Flyaway Kit]] counter-drone package ([[src-northcom-drone-incursion-2026-03]]).

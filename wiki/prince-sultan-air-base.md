# Prince Sultan Air Base

U.S. and coalition airbase in Saudi Arabia (~80 km southeast of Riyadh). Hosts USAF expeditionary operations and serves as a major hub for U.S. air power in the [[centcom]] AOR.

In April 2026, the Pentagon's first known operational deployment of the Ukrainian [[sky-map-c-uas|Sky Map]] [[counter-uas|C-UAS platform]] occurred here, accompanied by Ukrainian trainers, in response to drone-induced U.S. casualties and high-value-aircraft damage during [[operation-epic-fury]] ([[src-epic-fury-update-2026-04]]).

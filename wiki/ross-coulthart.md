**Ross Coulthart** is an Australian investigative journalist known for in-depth coverage of UAP/UFO topics. He has closely followed the careers of key disclosure figures including [[lue-elizondo]] and has reported extensively on [[uap-disclosure]] developments for News Nation and other outlets.

Coulthart publicly shared details of Elizondo's March 17, 2026 near-fatal motorcycle crash and urged helmet use in public safety messaging (see [[src-elizondo-motorcycle-crash-2026-04]]).

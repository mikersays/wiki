**Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL)** is the USAF's primary R&D organization, headquartered at [[wright-patterson-afb]] in Ohio. It develops advanced aircraft materials, propulsion, sensors, and directed-energy systems.

Retired Maj. Gen. [[william-neil-mccasland]] commanded AFRL before retirement c. 2013. AFRL's Wright-Patterson home is historically central to U.S. UFO lore, including rumored crash-retrieval housing (see [[crash-retrieval-programs]]).

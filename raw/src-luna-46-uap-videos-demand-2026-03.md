# Luna Continues Transparency Investigation into UAPs — Letter to Secretary of War Pete Hegseth Demanding 46 UAP Videos by April 14, 2026

## Context

On **March 31, 2026**, Rep. **Anna Paulina Luna** (R-FL), Chairwoman of the House Oversight Committee's **Task Force on the Declassification of Federal Secrets**, sent a four-page letter to **Secretary of War Pete Hegseth** demanding that the Department of War (formerly Department of Defense) deliver 46 specifically named UAP video files to the Task Force **no later than April 14, 2026**.

The letter is described by reporters as the most specific UAP disclosure demand in congressional history: unlike prior general-language requests, it names individual video files by title, date, location, and in some cases military callsign. The demand cites whistleblower testimony delivered at the September 9, 2025 hearing of the Task Force, which alleged that the **All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO)** possesses additional video records beyond what has been shared with Congress, and that prior departmental responses have been "less than adequate."

## Verbatim Quotes from the Letter (as reproduced by Newsweek and BroBible)

> "The continued lack of transparency surrounding these anomalies and the potential national security threat they pose is troubling."

> "The lack of disclosure regarding the very real threat posed by [UAPs] in and around U.S. restricted airspace is concerning."

> "The presence of UAPs in and around the sensitive airspaces of U.S. military installations poses a threat to the security of the armed forces and their readiness."

## Examples of the 46 Named Videos (as summarized by fliegerfaust.com and Unidentified Phenomena)

The letter names individual video files by title, date, and location. Reported examples include:

- "4 UAP formation – Iran, 8/26/22"
- "Syrian UAP instant acceleration, 2021"
- "Multiple Spherical UAP USO near Sub. Cactus 1X in and out of water, 3/25/22" (a transmedium item — unidentified submerged object entering/exiting water)
- Multiple spherical UAP encounters described as moving in and out of clouds or pulsing over water
- "Cigar-shaped or fat spherical" objects
- USO formations interacting with the ocean surface
- A military intercept over **Lake Huron**
- Civil-airspace cases near **Columbus, Ohio**
- Combat-theatre cases tied to **Iran** and **Syria**

The full 46-file manifest is contained in the PDF attachment to the oversight.house.gov release; it was not retrievable through this fetch.

## Rationale Cited in the Letter

- **Whistleblower testimony (Sept. 9, 2025 hearing)** — The Task Force's public hearing produced testimony that AARO holds additional video records beyond what has been provided to Congress.
- **Prior "less than adequate" responses** — The letter characterizes previous Pentagon/AARO responses to Task Force inquiries as insufficient.
- **National security framing** — The letter frames UAP activity as a national-security concern involving incursions near restricted airspace and military installations, including sensitive sites.

## Deadline and Follow-Up

The letter set a hard deadline of **April 14, 2026** for delivery of the 46 files. The deadline passed without the videos being produced. Luna posted to X on the evening of April 14–15:

> "No one from the Pentagon had responded until we reached out, and it appears that someone did not pass the letter to the appropriate authorities. How convenient."

> "Nonetheless, we will be getting the requested list. We are not waiting for a briefing at some unspecified future date."

The War Department subsequently stated that the White House is coordinating the release of "never-before-seen UFO material" (per Liberation Times, April 2026), framing the delay as an interagency coordination matter rather than a refusal.

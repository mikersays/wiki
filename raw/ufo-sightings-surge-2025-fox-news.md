# UFO Secret Files, Drone Swarms and Nuclear-Linked Sightings Stun Experts in 2025
**Source:** https://www.foxnews.com/us/ufo-secret-files-drone-swarms-nuclear-linked-sightings-stun-experts-2025
**Author:** Fox News staff
**Date:** 2025 (year-end roundup)

## Overview

This Fox News article surveys the major UFO/UAP developments of 2025, covering a range of incidents from scientific discoveries linking UAP activity to nuclear test sites, to military encounters, underwater object clusters, and a major documentary release featuring senior U.S. officials.

## Nuclear Sites and the Palomar Observatory Discovery

Scientists at California's Palomar Observatory made a significant discovery by analyzing archival photographs taken between 1949 and 1957. They identified transient, star-like flashes in those decades-old images that may correlate with unidentified aerial phenomena appearing near nuclear testing sites. The research was published in Nature's Scientific Reports on October 20, 2025.

The study identified 124 instances of above-ground nuclear tests during the period examined. The researchers found that transient phenomena were **45% more likely to appear within 24 hours of a nuclear test**. The paper noted: "We speculate that some transients could potentially be UAP in Earth orbit." This finding added scientific weight to the long-standing observation, reported by military witnesses across decades, that UAP activity appears concentrated around nuclear weapons facilities and testing sites.

## UK Police Helicopter Incident at RAF Lakenheath

On November 22, 2024, a UK National Police Air Service (NPAS) EC135 helicopter operating near RAF Lakenheath -- a major U.S. Air Force installation in Suffolk, England -- was forced to take emergency evasive action when the pilot reported two fast-moving craft that appeared to "target" and pursue the crew. The objects were traveling at approximately 190 mph.

Police logs documented roughly 20 drone sightings across three RAF bases between November 20 and 22, 2024. Despite multiple eyewitness accounts from trained helicopter pilots, Britain's Airprox Board ultimately concluded that the objects were U.S. Air Force F-15 lights. This conclusion was disputed by some observers who noted the flight characteristics described did not match those of F-15 aircraft.

## Underwater Object Clusters (USOs)

The UFO-reporting app Enigma documented over 9,000 unidentified submersible object (USO) sightings within 10 miles of U.S. shorelines since August 2025. California and Florida showed the highest concentrations of activity. This data point is notable because it reflects growing attention to the "transmedium" aspect of UAP phenomena -- objects that appear capable of operating in both air and water environments. The sheer volume of reports, while not verified individually, represents a significant pattern in citizen-reported data.

## Colorado Sightings Near Lockheed Martin Facility

On January 12, 2025, multiple witnesses in Colorado reported observing orbs near a Lockheed Martin facility. The objects produced no sound, and witnesses stated they had ruled out both Starlink satellites and drones as explanations. The proximity to a major defense contractor facility added to the pattern of UAP sightings near sensitive military and industrial sites.

## Fresno, California Pilot Sightings

In January 2025, multiple pilots in Fresno, California reported unexplained sightings, including one pilot who described an unknown object following his aircraft at 3,500 feet. These reports from trained aviators are considered among the more credible categories of UAP sightings due to the observers' flight experience and ability to identify conventional aircraft.

## The Age of Disclosure Documentary

The documentary film "The Age of Disclosure," released in 2025, features 34 senior U.S. officials discussing what the film characterizes as an "80-year global cover-up" of non-human intelligence. The film includes a statement from Secretary of State Marco Rubio (made prior to his current role): "We've had repeated instances of something operating in the airspace over restricted nuclear facilities."

The documentary is directed and produced by Dan Farah and serves as a major cultural artifact in the ongoing UAP disclosure movement, bringing together government insiders, military veterans, and intelligence officials on camera.

## Reporting Surge

The article notes the broader trend of a sighting surge in 2025. Americans for Safe Aerospace reported its biggest year with more than 700 UAP reports, up from just over 300 in 2024 -- more than doubling year-over-year. The Pentagon's All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) released its quarterly update in early March 2026, revealing that the office has now catalogued over 2,400 UAP cases, with roughly 171 cases remaining officially unexplained after investigation.

## MQ-9 Reaper Orb Incident

In September 2025, during a House hearing, Representative Eric Burlison presented a video purportedly showing an MQ-9 Reaper drone striking an unidentified orb off the coast of Yemen in October 2024 with a Hellfire missile. According to the testimony, the orb continued to fly after being struck, apparently unharmed. This incident represents one of the most dramatic pieces of military-sourced UAP evidence presented to Congress.

## Key Takeaways

The article portrays 2025 as a year of significant escalation in both the quantity and quality of UAP evidence, with patterns emerging around nuclear sites, military installations, and sensitive defense infrastructure. The combination of scientific research (Palomar study), military encounters (Yemen orb, UK helicopter), citizen reporting (Enigma app data), and legislative action paints a picture of a phenomenon receiving unprecedented institutional attention.

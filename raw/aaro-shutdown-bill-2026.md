# AARO Shutdown Bill Targets UAP Office Now

**Author:** Cristina Gomez
**Date:** April 10, 2026
**Publication:** UFO News
**URL:** https://www.ufonews.co/post/aaro-shutdown-bill-targets-uap-office-now

## Bill Details

On April 6, 2026, Congressman Tim Burchett introduced H.R. 8197 to eliminate AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office), the Department of Defense organization handling UAP investigations.

**Key provisions include:**
- The Secretary of War must shut down the office within 60 days
- AARO's responsibilities would be distributed among other DoD departments
- A legal barrier prevents either the Secretary of War or Director of National Intelligence from establishing any replacement centralized office, blocking simple rebranding

## Political Context

Congresswoman Anna Paulina Luna has championed the shutdown effort. She previously exited a classified AARO briefing in April 2024, later calling it unsubstantial. By September 2025, she publicly characterized the former AARO director as "a documented liar on the congressional record."

Luna also introduced into the record a criticism from former Deputy Assistant Secretary of Defense Christopher Mellon, who described AARO's historical report as "the most error-ridden government document he had encountered in decades."

## Current Status

The bill now sits before the House Committee on Armed Services and the Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence. No vote has been scheduled. AARO has not released its 2025 annual report and hasn't briefed media in nearly a year.
